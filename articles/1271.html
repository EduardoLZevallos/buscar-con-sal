Report back webinar: "Women in Nicaragua - Power and Protagonism" February 15th 2023

Enviado por tortilla el Lun, 13/02/2023 - 19:36

Fr

 

 

* español sigue al inglés

Dear comrade,

Black Alliance for Peace, Casa Benjamin Linder and AFGJ, are excited to invite you to the Women in Nicaragua Report-back webinar! From January 7-16, 2023, 22 delegates traveled to Nicaragua to investigate the conditions and lives of Nicaraguan women. Join us to hear the findings of this effort from some of the participants, and to learn from the stories of Nicaraguan resilience and empowerment despite two hundred years of imperialist aggression.

 

Wednesday, February 15

3 pm PT / 4 pm MT / 5 pm Nicaragua & CST / 6 pm ET

Register here!

*After registering, you will receive an email with the link and password to join the session 

You can read the report-back here! For our study guide, recordings and other materials of the Women in Nicaragua: Power and Protagonism series, please visit Casa Benjamin Linder's website.

In solidarity,

Black Alliance for Peace, Casa Benjamin Linder & the Alliance for Global Justice - Nicaragua Network
