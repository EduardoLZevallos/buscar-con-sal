A Greeting to the World’s Peoples and Governments

Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 30/12/2023 - 13:51

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

THE WORLD OF PEOPLES
HAS NOW BEEN BORN!

Beloved Brothers and Sisters,
Beloved Compañeras and Compañeros,
Beloved Peoples and Families
Of So Very Many Fraternal Countries :

As we approach the end of a Year of great Challenges, of so many Struggles, of numerous Tragedies, Misfortunes and Suffering for the Human Family, a Year of great Battles, and therefore of steadfast Sense of Purpose, we greet each other with Undefeated and Victorious Hopes, with Faith in the God of All Triumphs, and Trust in the Spiritual Strength of Heroic and Combative Peoples, opening up New Times to continue fighting against Injustice, the Culture of Death, and uniting our Strengths, Conscience and Efforts to continue building True Life, towards the Future Humanity deserves.

We greet each other as a Union of Nations and Peoples who believe in our Rights, defending Justice, the Common Good, Peace, Work, Security and Joy, as the Patrimony of Humanity.

With Certainty of the History that we have been building and traveling through, along Shared Paths of Dignity, Brotherhood, Solidarity, Respect and Cooperation of Everyone, among Everyone and for the sake of Everyone, we renew our Commitments of Unity, to those Legacies that nothing and no one can ever snatch from us and for our definitive Liberation from the imperial, colonial, onerous, oppressive and genocidal anti-cultures, with new Energies, new Values and renewed Vigor,.

Honoring the Invincible Spirit of the Great Combatants of Humanity, we continue to change the World, and Nicaragua, Sovereign, Dignified and Ever Blessed, salutes their Vision by taking the Paths towards unalterable, luminous Futures, in Sisterly and Brotherly Union, meaning ever more Strength.

The World has changed now, and continues to change, towards that indispensable Multipolarity which is the only just, beneficial, auspicious, creator of more Light, more Suns, of justly deserved Well-being and with the unshakable Convictions of genuine, liberating Independence.

The Other World, this New One of the Peoples, the truly Free World, the World free of Imperialism, Colonialism and Fascism, under the bright Sun of a New Day, of Cooperation out of Solidarity, has now been born, and the Sacred Blood of millions of Martyrs and Heroes accompanies us, illuminating and nourishing New Horizons.

We thank God for the magnificent Courage of our Peoples in Asia, Africa, Europe, Eurasia, Latin America and the Caribbean, and all the brave Citizens of the United States who fight for an end to Imperialism and for Peace among all Peoples, in the New, Magnificent Dawn of Cultures, Tongues, Languages and Practices of a Community that shares, pulsates, dreams and loves, from our own Formidable Ways of Being.

Long Live Our Victorious Struggles the World Over!
Fascism Continues Always To Be Defeated!
Colonialism and Imperialism Are Finished!
They Could Not Prevail, Nor Will They!
We Have Hope! Long Live Peace!
We Have Overcome... We Will Overcome!
Always Further On!

Managua, December 30th 2023
