Mother-Nicaragua, Triumphant Mothers

Enviado por tortilla el Mié, 29/05/2024 - 15:37

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MOTHER-NICARAGUA
TRIUMPHANT MOTHERS!

Beloved Heroic and Triumphant
Mothers of the Blessed, Dignified Nicaragua
And Always Sovereign and Free
Sisters All :

On this Day of Special Joy, and Much-deserved Recognition to all the Mothers who triumph in Nicaragua and with Nicaragua, we triumph, we give each and every one an Embrace, with the Spiritual Strength we share... An Embrace of Love, Hope and absolute Trust in the God of All Victories.

We recognize, with Infinite Respect and Supreme Admiration, the daily work of every Mother, who in addition to reaping the fruits of her successful labors in every kind and type of work, zealously takes care of her Family, transferring Higher Values to her Daughters and Sons, and lovingly protects the Peace, which gives us so much, and for which we have fought so hard, All of Us Together, in our Nicaragua.

Mothers are a Blessed Force of Peace...! Mothers are the Outstanding Intelligence which guides us, along with Heart and Reason, to continue strengthening this Harmony that has allowed us to recover our Rights, advance in the fight against Poverty, and ensure through Devotion and Action, the Security, Stability and Commitment, which guarantees us Prosperity in all Fields.

Mothers are the Solidarity Mirror of the Heart of Jesus, of the Love of Christ Jesus, Son of God and Redeemer of the World.

Mothers are the vigorous pulse of every Emotion and every Feeling of deep Spirituality and too of absolute Identification with the Nation we all love, this Nicaragua of Ours, this Sacred Land, Mother Earth of Ours, where we continue cultivating, sowing, clearing, tending, and reaping every Blessing.

To every Nicaraguan Mother, to those who have their Children and Families close by; to those who have Children, Grandchildren and Relatives in different parts of the World, but also very close to their Generous and Prodigious Souls, to all, the Certainty that we are in tune together to change Life, improving it, as appropriate, in our irrevocable decision to continue advancing against Poverty.

To Mothers everywhere who with their Children, Grandchildren and Families are calm and secure, because we have Peace and Well Being, and because we know, with Sublime and Serene Understanding, how to protect that Sacrosanct Peace, in the Joy of our daily tasks, and of the Festivities that unite us. Because Mother-Nicaragua is a Permanent Celebration, of Praise, Song, Dance and Art and Stories of so much Talent, all springing from Combative Working Mothers who Overcome.

To the Mothers who wait with absolute Trust and Faith for their Encounter with Children who may have departed to the Plane of Eternity, our Accompaniment, our shared Certainty of the Resurrection and Life.

And also, of course, our Embrace to all the Families whose Mothers rest in the Sacred Fields which, we have no doubt, will be full of Flowers, Music and Love, because our Mothers remain in our Hearts, as Luminous Stars, by their Imperishable Example.

To all Nicaragua’s Mothers, who are Light, Life and tremendous Truth and Love, because they deserve Love. With that Strength, recognized and acclaimed by everyone, we know We are Going Onward, Always Further On.
Blessed mothers... How much we love each other...!

Managua, May 29th 2024
