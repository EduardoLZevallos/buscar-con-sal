Message to the President of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 20/08/2024 - 15:56

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Managua, August 6th 2024    

Compañero
Kim Jong-un
Chairman of the Workers' Party of Korea,
Chairman of State Affairs of the DPRK of Korea
and Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces
of the DPRK of Korea
In person

Beloved Compañero and Comrade :

The Sandinista National Liberation Front and the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity are celebrating this August 21st the Historic Commemoration of the 45th Anniversary of the establishment of Diplomatic Relations and Ties of Uncompromising Struggle between our Nicaraguan People and the People, Party and Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

As we celebrate these years that have been arduous, challenging, of immense difficulties, we know that we are celebrating the Infinite Strength of our Peoples and our Revolutions, always remembering the Strength and Leadership of the Presidents and Great Leaders, Kim Il-sun, Kim Jong-il, and you, our Brother, today Head of State, Chairman and Leader of the Communist Party of Korea, Comrade Kim Jong-un.

We are honored to celebrate this Anniversary together, in the indispensable battles for a World of Peace, Well-being and Rights, a World of Sovereign Countries and Peoples, a World of Justice for Everyone.

Our Greetings, beloved Compañero and Comrade Kim Jong-un, to you, to the Party, and to your People.

Much Health and Strength and more Energy in order to continue making your way  victoriously.
