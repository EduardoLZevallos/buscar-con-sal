Webinar on Nicaragua's Elections - Sunday, October 31st 2021

Submitted bytortilla onJue, 28/10/2021 - 19:32

Nicaragua Solidarity Campaign Action Group & Alliance for Global Justice 

Invite you to a webinar in preparation for Nicaragua's 2021 Elections

Sunday, October 31

3pm Eastern, 2pm Central, 1pm Nicaragua, noon Pacific, 7pm Greenwich, UK
 

REGISTER NOW!

 

This 90-minute webinar will be held a week before Nicaragua's crucial national elections on November 7.  Francisco Dominguez will address the FSLN's decades-long struggle to bring democracy to Nicaragua and to assert national sovereignty. Netfa Freeman, who was recently in Nicaragua, will describe his observations of the atmosphere and conditions there. Sofia Clark will summarize the US attacks against Nicaragua and attempts to delegitimize the elections. Nan McCurdy will also do a brief "Nicaraguan Elections 101" overview, addressing such questions as:  What steps are being taken to assure a free and fair election?

Featuring:

Netfa Freeman, Co-host/ producer, Voices with Vision, WPFW 89.3 FM Washington, DC,

and Coordinating Committee member, Black Alliance for Peace

Francisco Dominguez,  Head of Research Group on Latin America, Middlesex University

and Executive Committee member, Nicaragua Solidarity Campaign Action Group

Sofia Clark, Political Research Analyst, Miguel d'Escoto Brockmann Center,

National Autonomous University of Nicaragua (UNAN) in Managua
