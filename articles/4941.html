Celebration to Commemorate the 45th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nicaraguan Army

September 2nd 2024

Address of Daniel

Nicaraguan Brothers and Sisters, Families of the city and of the countryside, on this Glorious Day in which we are commemorating the 45th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Army, made up of the People, defending Nicaraguan Families, defending Peace, which is the greatest objective.

I congratulate General Avilés for the historical synthesis he has given us, really a historical synthesis of the process by which the Army was formed. The roots of the Army are in the struggle of our Heroes, who throughout history have always faced, fought, and given their lives defending the integrity of the Nation.

Today we are able to hold this Celebration in Times of Peace, and how much has it cost to reach this stage of Peace. Peace meaning well-being for the poorest. In Peace we can fight poverty. In Peace we can ensure Education for all Families, for the Children of Working-class Families, Rural Families, Poor Families with low incomes, but with the Government of the Sandinista Front they have the guarantee of free Education, at all Levels, from an Education whereby their children can go ahead and start at preschool level in that child's community, and then move onward, until they reach Higher Education and studies at University level.

Never in the history of our country has it been possible to bring education to so many Families, for free. And bringing Education means that for Families living in territories where we are still building roads and highways, that a School is built where they are.

And so too Communication Systems also arrive to be able to bring Technical Education, Higher Education, to the Families of the Countryside, to Families in the Communities of our Original Peoples. Free University Education, free Higher Technical Education is now available for them.

But we have also achieved something indispensable for progress to be made in these types of Education Programs, for the benefit of the humblest Families, the most impoverished Families, and that is the Health System. We have a Health System that is truly extraordinary, a Health System where Hospitals have been multiplying even in the most remote areas.
