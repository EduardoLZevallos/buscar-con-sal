Gaza, the Flood of Al-Aqsa

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 26/10/2023 - 15:34

Ismail Haniyeh, Hamas, October 26th 2023


Israel bombs the area beside a UNRWA school
housing displaced civilian families

Starting with Gaza; Gaza, the Flood of Al-Aqsa; Gaza, the dignity of prisoners and the path [Al-Quds]; Gaza, O bearer of burdens, O Gaza, who writes today with iron, fire, tears, blood, and body parts, a new history for our people, our cause, our nation, and for all the free people of the world who reject this criminal, brutal, and hated occupation.

Gaza, O soul that flows anew in the body of this nation from one end to another, and a soul that flows in the body of the free people of the world across all the continents. Gaza, O backbone of every Palestinian, O support of every Arab and Muslim, O advanced deterrence arm, O protectors of Al-Aqsa, O hope for the liberation of prisoners, God willing.

Salutations to you, O steadfast people, despite the brutality and the genocide war waged by the enemy; and salutations to the souls of the martyrs, the martyrs of Gaza, the martyrs of the West Bank, and the martyrs of Lebanon from the Islamic Resistance in Hezbollah, the martyrs of Al-Qassam, and the martyrs of Saraya Al-Quds across the nation.

There in South Lebanon, where the front is escalating to send fiery messages to this enemy, the continuation of aggression, killing, and destruction on Gaza and its people will bring the entire region out of control, and it is on a hot plate.

The region has now become a hot plate, and no one can predict its directions or outcomes. However, we are aware, confident, and certain that the outcomes of the Al-Aqsa flood, which Al-Qassam wrote on the first day of the Flood, will accompany all of our stages in Palestine, in the struggle of the nation and the resistance against the zionist project.

On the 20th day of this glorious battle, which is changing the face of history, history will be written in glory for our people and our nation. And, God willing, we will proceed towards achieving the supreme goals of this people who have suffered from this historical injustice, from this occupation, from refuge, displacement, siege, arrests, checkpoints, and from being prevented from reaching the blessed Al-Aqsa Mosque.

The resistance in Gaza is fine, a thousand times fine. From here, I call upon the peoples of the Arab and Islamic nations and the free people of the world. I do not just call upon them to continue what they are doing; I call upon them to support this resistance and back this revolution with money, weapons, technology, and all the sciences that consolidate this victory, embed these heroic deeds, and write, along with the resistance in Gaza and Palestine, the final chapters for this abominable occupation.

I call upon all the thinkers, experts, and strategists to address the post-occupation phase, for the occupation will inevitably perish. This occupier will not be able to regain its health due to this strategic strike and the resounding defeat it has suffered.

The blood in Gaza, along with the resistance and Al-Qassam, will triumph over this occupier. It will prevail over this enemy and the law of the jungle that some want to establish in this reality.

I call on everyone, all the free people of the world, and all friendly, brotherly, and allied countries to exert all the necessary pressure in all forums and in all directions to stop the terrorist aggression on Gaza, to stop this war against our children, our women, our elders, our mosques, our universities, and our homes. This brutal aggression must stop.

This new Holocaust, carried out by the zionist occupation army against our Palestinian people, must stop.

I urge all presidents, leaders, thought leaders, and decision-makers to clearly affirm the legitimacy of the resistance on the blessed land of Palestine, the legitimacy of the Islamic Resistance Movement Hamas, and all resistance movements on Palestinian land. They are liberation movements seeking to free their land, their homeland, and their people. Terrorism is the occupier, terrorism is those who support this occupier, and terrorism is those who remain silent about these massacres and slaughters.

We must win the battle of consciousness, the battle of will, and the battle of accurately describing what is happening on the ground.
We should not fall into this trap, we should not fall into the snares of the devil. Matters should be clear, unequivocal, straightforward, and without ambiguity. I salute all the brave and bold voices and stances that have clearly declared this legitimacy and this right to exercise legitimate resistance, which is guaranteed by all international norms and laws as well as Islamic and heavenly laws.

I stress the need to open all crossings, especially the Rafah crossing, and to allow everything that Gaza needs without restrictions or conditions. Everything that our people in Gaza need should be allowed to restart the cycle of life for all facilities in the Gaza Strip, especially hospitals, electricity, water, bakeries, and so on.

It is completely unacceptable for the enemy to control what enters Gaza or doesn't. The time has passed when this occupier could control Gaza, our people, and our region.

We call for the rapid delivery of everything Gaza needs and everything that the cycle of life in Gaza needs. I salute all those who stand in this square and push in every way to quickly provide what Gaza and the heroic people of Gaza need.

We should not keep moving in the same place; we must move on all fronts. The Flood must continue for the people of the nation, for the free people of the world, for the forces of resistance and opposition, and for the future of our Arab and Islamic countries.

These crowds must continue, and the voice must rise. Stop the genocide war on Gaza; stop this brutality and this Holocaust that these zionists are carrying out against our people, against our Gaza, against our West Bank, and against our prisoners, our prisoners in the occupation prisons.

The prisoners whom the enemy is tormenting now, thinking we are preoccupied with this war, we affirm that the prisoners' issue is a priority for us and is under our attention both on the ground and politically.

Brothers and sisters, our Palestinian people, Gaza, as I said, you are the bearer of burdens, you are the proof against the world, Gaza, children of Gaza, men of Gaza, women of Gaza, you who have taught the world pride, freedom, and dignity.

Greetings to you, greetings to the leaders of Gaza, its resistance, its mujahideen, its families, its women, everything that moves above its pure land. Go forward, move forward, and Allah, the Almighty, is our supporter.

* Ismail Haniyeh is head of the political bureau of Hamas
