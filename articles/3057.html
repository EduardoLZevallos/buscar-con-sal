Letter to Fidel

Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 25/11/2023 - 23:13

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

LETTER TO FIDEL

Today, beloved Fidel, we turn to your Immense Soul and Spirit, on this your Seventh Anniversary of Transition to another Plane of Life from where we know you continue to guide and enlighten your People, and all our Peoples.

We tell you, beloved Fidel, that in this World of so much turbulence, suffering and pain, you shine out, you encourage us, you inspire us, with your inexhaustible and irrepresible Optimism and your Faith in Humanity..

The infinite, stellar luminaries whose Physical and Spiritual Transit through this Earth speak to us of the Duty to continue fighting, waging all the Battles, to be always on the front line, and at the height of the Vitality and Energy of our Peoples, where the Pride of Nation and Humanity mobilizes us to meet our obligations, to overcome, and to revere and guard Life, Justice and Peace.

Making your way through your Legacy of Culture, Dignity, Fraternity, Solidarity, as Perfect Humanism, here we are, with our heads held high and our breast alight in the Sacred Flames of a Revolution that Evolves and Triumphs, in the name of all those unforgivably massacred and humiliated, who still stood fast, making their way thanks to the Strength of unyielding Patriotism.

United, with your Strength, Fidel, we create more and more Future, always transcending the hatred, the evil, and the sacrilegious and degrading warmongering of the Imperialists of the Earth.

You Fidel, Cuba and your People, your Leaders, Raúl, Miguel, Everyone Together, we Always See Further On, in permanent creation of Sublime Horizons, towards that Future which rightfully belongs to those of us who, like You, believe in the Human Person, creating New and Victorious Times.

Managua, Nicaragua,

Always Blessed and Free

Always Further On

November 25th, 2023
