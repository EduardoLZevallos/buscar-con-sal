Message to President Nicolás Maduro

Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 03/02/2024 - 19:03

(Unofficial translation)

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Managua, February 3rd 2024

Compañero
Nicolás Maduro Moros
Constitutional President
of the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela

In person

Dear Nicolás :

Yesterday, February 2, we celebrated in the Great Nation, and among the Free Peoples of the World, the 25th Anniversary of the miraculous event that installed Justice, Truth and Reason, in our Sister Venezuela, Bolivarian, Revolutionary, Evolutionary.

With the Eternal Comandante, Hugo Chávez Frías, the Flame of Dignity and the Right to Liberation of our Peoples was raised once more. His illuminated Personality, his Generosity, his Solidarity, and his Permanent Sense of Brotherhood and Unity for all Struggles, and every Victory, showed the whole World the Power of his Spirit, that Great, Providential and Missionary Spirit, which came to stay opening Paths of Sovereignty, Hope and Certainty, out of the absolute and unbreakable Faith in our Rights and Merits, despite Empire, breaking its nefarious chains of domination and subjugation.

The Bolivarian Revolution, harassed, besieged, by the enslaving and looting Imperialist Powers of the Earth, rose up and stands firm as a Prodigious Sign, as a Sign of the Power of our Peoples, who live with Chavez in our Hearts, with Fidel in our Soul, and with the possible, necessary, indispensable Horizon, which we must continue to seek, in the face of rage and insults from the same demented figures as ever.

Sandino, in his infinite love and admiration for Bolívar, saw, knew how to see, glimpsed, Chávez. He felt him, he intuited him, and he made him ours, in the Struggles Always Further On, and in the Causes that live on, of others and for others, as Sandino predicted, that Follow On - We Move Onward!

In the depth of that call, and in the Supreme Truth of their Calls to Combat, to Strive, to show the Fortitude and Patriotism of us all, Bolivar, Sandino, Fidel, Chávez Live On, and the Struggles, and their Battles, their Combats, noble and fierce, continue on, continue to overcome, with the Providence of everything Sacred, of everything Beloved, and of every Longing sealed with Courage.

We say with Dario :

"Lord, I am the sovereign people
overthrowing the tyrant;
I am the Revolution in all its brilliance
confusing slaves and masters;
I am an inspired prophetess who in her anger
drives tyranny away,
displayed for all time
with Phrygian cap and red banner.
./.
I am the noon.
Before my light
and the warmth of my flames
in this new age of agitation,
the workers' phalanx strikes the anvil,
while the thinking phalanx writes.
./.
After Attila the bloody victor
comes Bolivar redeemer of a world;
after the dark and bloody past,
and the fruitful present,
before the radiant light of freedom !

Rubén Darío's “El Porvenir”

So, here we are... It is 25 Years of a Great Nation and a Vibrant Revolution... Nicolas, Your People Overcome, You Overcome, Chavez in You and with You the Revolution,"inspired prophesies...”

“the Age of Fire;
every fire, every star, every light
a scourge of conscience,
the brotherhood proclaiming
Supreme Reason,
after horrendous lethal slavery;
the freedom that breaks every yoke
and the deluge of harmonies unleashing
like rapids from
his gigantic harp, Victor Hugo”

Rubén Darío's “El Porvenir”

With Immense Love and Admiration... “We have come from far and we are heading to the Future."
