Webinar 21st September 2025 - Housing programs in Nicaragua and Venezuela

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 19/09/2025 - 05:41
 
 
REGISTER:  bit.ly/NicaSept21
 
 
REGISTER: bit.ly/NicaSept21.  
 

Ever wonder why the US government is so critical of Nicaragua and Venezuela?  Maybe it's all the wonderful programs they provide their people -- their top priority.  Housing is one of these.  Join us to learn how Nicaragua and Venezuela are providing dignified social housing to huge numbers of their citizens at zero or low cost.  Speakers are Sofia M. Clark of Miguel d’Escoto Brockmann Center, Managua, and Elizabeth Santos of FundaVivienda, with Gran Misión Vivienda Venezuela.  Spanish <-> English interpretation available.
