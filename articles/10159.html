Presidential Decree awards the Order Augusto C. Sandino to the Army of Nicaragua

Submitted bytortilla onJue, 03/09/2020 - 20:22

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

PRESIDENTIAL AGREEMENT 103-2020

The President of the Republic, in accordance with the powers conferred by the Political Constitution of the Republic of Nicaragua,

CONSIDERING:

1. That the Nicaraguan Army, upon reaching 41 Years since its Foundation, has fulfilled with formidable Merits, its Missions of Safeguarding and Defending the Sovereign Security of Our Motherland;

2. That in the performance of all its Tasks and Missions it has guaranteed the Right to Sovereign Security, at all times and in all circumstances, to all the People, Families, Regions and Communities of our Nicaragua;

3. That their tenacious, unwavering and consistent duty, proclaimed with firmness and cohesion, has been recognized by the entire People of Nicaragua, who feel their presence in all Tasks of Prevention, Attention and Response to the threats of Organized Crime, Drug Trafficking, Crime; guarding of the Air, Maritime and Land Borders; Community Action against Disasters and Calamities. Our People also recognize their active duty towards contributing to Health, Education, Electoral Processes, and Security in all areas of National Production;

4. That the Nicaraguan Army, Worthy Successor of the Army for the Defense of National Sovereignty of our General, Augusto Sandino, and the historical continuation of the Sandinista Popular Army, celebrates 41 Years of commendable work at the service of all Nicaraguan Families, without any distinction;

THEREFORE,

The President of the Republic,

Resolves:

1. Deliver to the Nicaraguan Army, its Commander in Chief, General Command, Military Council, Officers and Soldiers, on this 41st Anniversary, the Order Augusto Sandino in its Highest Degree, Battle of San Jacinto, in the person of the Army General, Julio César Avilés Castillo, Commander in Chief of the Nicaraguan Army;

2. Support and fully recognize all the Missions that are developed and will continue to be developed with the Patriotism and Courage of the Best Children of our Motherland, in defense of National Sovereignty, Nature, and the Dignified Life of the entire Nicaraguan People,

3. Render Honor and Glory to the thousands of Nicaraguan Sisters and Brothers who gave their lives in defense of National Sovereignty, Justice and Peace. Likewise, express all our Solidarity and Recognition to the Retired Sisters and Brothers of the Army, and those who resulted with different disabilities, victims of the terrorist interventions and practices of the aggressor Powers;

4. Ratify our Respect, Love and Solidarity to the Families of these Sisters and Brothers who have been victims of State Terrorism from the same aggressor Powers.

Given in the City of Managua on the 2nd day of the Month of September of the Year 2020.
