Government promotes democratization, fruit of the Revolutionary Triumph of July 19, 1979

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS

 

Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE DELEGATION OF THE GRUN
FOR UNDERSTANDING AND PEACE IN NICARAGUA

Monday, June 3, 2019

“Blessed art thou,
 Who is like unto thee,
 O people saved by the LORD,
 Shield of thy help,
 Sword of thy Excellency?”
 
(from Deuteronomy 33:29)

We begin yet another Week for the Security, Labor, and Peace, which our People and the Nicaraguan families demand.

First of all we give thanks to God, for Life, Hope and absolute Confidence in His Hand of Redemption and His Loving Light that guides us to find the best Ways to strengthen the Common Good, the Tranquility, the Coming Together and The Welfare of all the Nicaraguan Families.

As Christians we are committed to the Supreme Ideal of Fraternity and to the Sense of Family and Community, which from our Faith encourages, motivates and protects us all who love our Blessed, Always Blessed Nicaragua, evermore United and Always Free.

By the Hand of God, with His Grace and for His Glory, we are advancing to:

    1. Create all the indispensable conditions to continue complying with the Nicaraguan Families, with the Reunion of the Families, responsibly abiding with the release agreement, which will be concluded by June 18.

    2. Continue to strengthen Reconciliation, Justice and Peace by implementing our Culture of Peace through the hundreds of Commissions for Reconciliation, Justice and Peace that have been formed and are operating throughout the country, fostering Harmonious Understanding and Coexistence.

    3. Continue to receive with affection our Countrymen and Women who left after the tragedy imposed by the failed attempted coup d’état.

    The Plan for Safe Return, Social and Family Reintegration, is promoted and applied as requested by our Brothers and Sisters from wherever they are.

    4. Begin complying with the Guidelines that arise from the Law for the Comprehensive Plan of Attention to Victims, from the Office of the Human Rights Ombudsman and its various directorates, in order to advance with its Mandates for Care and Solidarity toward the Families that have suffered persecution, torture, kidnappings and losses, both against their Loved Ones and their means of work and subsistence.

    5. Continue to guarantee all processes of strengthening our Democracy, as established in our Constitution and our Laws.

    All Nicaraguans are required to know and respect our Constitution, our Laws and the Institutions that govern our Republic.

    6. Continue to strengthen Electoral Democracy and Institutions in all aspects that will lead us to organize and carry out transparent and successful National Elections in 2021.

    7. We continue to guarantee all the rights contemplated in our Constitution, emphasizing Citizen Security, the mobility of our Culture and Life, our Work, Economy, Trade, Education, Recreation, and the entire Social, Religious, Family and Community Movement according to our particular experiences.

    8. Our Economy is sustained by the Protagonism of Producers, Traders, Entrepreneurs, Business Leaders and Workers, whose Activity and Rights are guaranteed in our Constitution.

    9. We ensure the conditions so that we all may contribute to the recovery of our economy that was impacted by the failed coup. Nicaragua demands Responsibility and Commitment from us all, so that by restoring stability, we may resume the Paths of Growth that Nicaragua, in Christianity and Solidarity, was undertaking.

    10. All of us; Workers, Producers, Farmers, Transporters, Traders and Cooperatives, make up an Economic Model of Protagonism, and we trust that our work contributes to the Economic, Productive, Commercial, Labor, Social and Entrepreneurial Activity may be developed with respect among us all and of course toward our Constitution and Laws.

    11. We continue to express our rejection of Unilateral, Illegal and Coercive Political and Economic Measures that affect our Right as a Laborious, Humble and Peace-loving, to live and work with Programs and Projects that allow us to continue the Fight against Poverty.

Inspired and proud of all our Democratic Processes, the fruit of our Struggles and the Revolutionary Triumphs of July 19, 1979, We Move Forward, Always and Beyond, with Dignity and with Elevated National Spirit and Sovereignty, consolidating Freedom, Fraternity and Good Common each and every day.

Our Inclusive, Christian Plans of irreplaceable values ​​such as Family, Faith and Community Culture, guarantee the Strength of a Generous Life with Tranquility, Work and Improvement, in Solidarity and with Peace.
