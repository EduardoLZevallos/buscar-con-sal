Nicaragua's government again reaffirms its commitment to the National Dialogue - March 13th 2019

 
 
 
Submitted by tortilla on Mié, 13/03/2019 - 10:25

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS


Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE OF THE
GOVERNMENT OF RECONCILIATION AND NATIONAL UNITY
AT THE CONVERSATION TABLE

The Delegation that represents the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity at the Negotiation Table for Understanding and Peace, wants to state its position with respect to the unconceivable, contradictory and unbelievable attitude of Participants at this Table, who, on March 16th, were part of a provocation and alteration of the public order, and of the security of the Nicaraguan Families who have the Right to Tranquility and Peace.

The Delegation of the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity, expects from its Counterpart, seriousness and responsibility, that shows the sincere commitment to converse and agree on positive Outcomes for Nicaragua.

Managua, 18th of March of 2019
Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Republic of Nicaragua
