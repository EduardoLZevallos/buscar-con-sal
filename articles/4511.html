Nicaragua demands respect for Syria

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 28/06/2024 - 19:11

MINISTRY

OF

FOREIGN RELATIONS

Managua, Nicaragua

 

WE CONDEMN THIS INJUSTICE

AND DEMAND RESPECT FOR SYRIA

 

The Government of Reconciliation and National Unity of Nicaragua, Blessed and Forever Sovereign and Free, via our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, condemns the arrest warrant issued by France against the President of the Syrian Arab Republic, Bashar al-Asad, in yet another demonstration of the nefarious attempts by the Colonial and Neocolonial Powers to continue their rule over the World.

 

The Government of the People-President and Nicaragua's Families once again call for Respect for the Sovereignty and Independence of Countries and Peoples. We reject all forms of interference representing a prolongation of the Colonial, Neo-colonial, and Imperialist wars, among so many other ways, whereby they have grabbed the natural resources of the world's Peoples.

 

In support of President Bashar al-Assad, with the Families and People of Greater Syria, for the Cultural and Social Power of Humanity, all our Fraternal Solidarity.

 

Managua, June 28th 2024

 

Denis Moncada Colindres

Minister for Foreign Affairs

Government of Reconciliation

and National Unity

Republic of Nicaragua
