An inside view of Venezuelan resistance to US imperialism - Webinar, Thursday April 23rd

Submitted bytortilla onMar, 21/04/2020 - 15:11
We are excited to announce a special event this Thursday, April 23 at 5:00 pm Eastern/2:00 pm Pacific - a webinar, "An Inside View of Venezuelan Resistance to US Imperialism and How to Build International Solidarity," featuring:

Carlos Ron - Venezuelan Vice Foreign Minister for North America
Ajamu Baraka - Black Alliance for Peace
Bahman Azad - US Peace Council
Margaret Flowers/Kevin Zeese - Popular Resistance

We will hear about US regime change efforts targeting Venezuela and how Venezuelans have protected the Bolivarian process. We'll get an update on the latest outrages, like the US stealing hundreds of millions of dollars from the Venezuelan Central Bank during the pandemic. We'll also learn how to show solidarity with Venezuelans as we work to end US imperialism.

Register for the Zoom link so you can participate here: bit.ly/VZRESISTS.
