Nicaragua has Peace... Nicaragua has Love and Dignity!

Enviado por tortilla el Mié, 22/11/2023 - 18:02

NICARAGUA HAS PEACE...NICARAGUA HAS LOVE, AND DIGNITY

Clearer than ever, we celebrate and sing
Rosario Murillo

Nicaragua has Peace... It has cost us a great deal this Peace that is our Heritage, our Treasure, and fully Our Right.

Nicaragua has Peace and we protect it as our fundamental treasure. We protect and defend it in our Blessed Nation, Always Sovereign and Dignified.

Nicaragua has Peace and we don't put it at risk. Not for all the world.

Hatred and Violence... Those of mean and backward heart, treacherous and disloyal... Those of diabolical malignancy... Those are gone forever. They were expelled, forever, thanks to the Valor, Bravery and Insuperable Love of the Nicaraguan People.

The traitors and cowards are ghosts now, unburied remains of a story from long ago...!

Nicaragua has Peace and loves Life, defends Life and Joy. For Nicaragua is all about Life, Hope, Light, and the Genuine Truth, of lucid Brother and Sisterhood.

Nicaragua has Peace, and nothing can alter that God-given Gift of Grace which we welcome and take to heart as an absolute Duty, as a Responsibility First and Last, as an Undeviating Path, and as a Nation that knows all about Struggle and Honor.

Nicaragua has Peace and does not permit stealthy provocations, nor confabulations, nor distortions, nor crude, vulgar, facile manipulations, typical of the failed and defeated imperial servants, sunk in a bygone history.

In Nicaragua, where, by the Grace of our Father and our Mother Mary, we live in Harmony, where we live a Culture of Peace, and of Family Fortitude and Affection, perfidious scheming has no place nor do the ungrateful, the outlaws, already stateless with their Hearts stood still, mortgaged to the Devil.

In Nicaragua, we don't sell our soul to the devil.
Nor, in Nicaragua, do we sell out Peace.

In these hours, in these days of New Victories, we see the boorish exploitation, along with the crude, malignant terrorist propaganda, aiming to turn a lovely, well-deserved moment of Pride and Celebration, into destructive coup mongering, or into a return, of course impossible, to the nefarious, selfish, criminal practices of those vampire-like shysters, who used to abuse the People, the People's Patrimony, the Natural, Cultural, Legitimate Heritage of Nicaragua’s People.

In Nicaragua that has Peace, those vipers have no space, they have no place, they dwell in darkness, negativity, evil. Their despicable and miserly Hearts have always sought to turn the Golden Dawn of our Peoples into more misery, more subjugation and more domination. But of course those cheapskate lackeys will never pass!

Any kind of cowardly plan bereft of ideas, of ideals, of values and of real leaders; any such diabolical, malevolent Plot mounted against Nicaragua’s Right to live securely, serenely, with all the Vitality and Vigor of Joy, Labor, Security and Peace, will never be Nicaraguan nor ever enjoy the Grace of God; they are just foolish, clumsy, criminal machinations by useless schemers seeking to attack the  loveliness and brilliance of Beauty and Peace.

No guns roar here anymore
nor the voice of violence
nor does the blood of Brothers, Sisters stain
our Glorious dual colored Banner.

Long Live Blessed Nicaragua,
With its Christian Solidarity,
Beautiful, Sovereign, Dignified,
Forever Free!

Long live Nicaragua’s Families!
Long live Peace!

Let all the vainglorious, insane delinquents stop trying to take such base advantage of Nicaragua’s Beauty, Joy and Talent. Let them stop abusing the well-deserved Victory of a lovely girl, trying to hide their insignificance and inability, dirtying our Blessed Waters, with their ridiculous, banal delusions!

Managua, Nicaragua,
November 22, 2023
Always Dignified, Sovereign and Free
