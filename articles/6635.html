The 30th Pastors for Peace Caravan is Coming to the Bay Area
 

 

 
Photo: Bill Hackwell
 
Tuesday June 18, 6:00PM
Berkeley Fellowship of Unitarian Universalists
1924 Cedar St. (at Bonita Ave), Berkeley
Donations at the door (no one turned away for lack of funds)
 
This is a Fundraising Event for Pastors for Peace
 
Potluck
 
Program:
 
A special guest from Cuba who has been active in the Cuban revolutionary process for almost 60 years. She started as a young volunteer teacher in the 1961 literacy brigade. She spent nearly 40 years as an educator, first teaching in schools and then as an adult educator in the workplace.
 
Catherine Murphy, is a San Francisco-based filmmaker who has spent much of the  last 10 years working in Latin America, especially Cuba. She is the founder and director of the Literacy Project, a multi-media documentary project on adult literacy in the Americas. She produced the well-known film "Maestra". Catherine will be representing Pastors for Peace.
 
Co-sponsored by The International Committee for Peace, Justice and Dignity, Richmond, Ca./Regla, Cuba Friendship Committee, Task Force on the Americas, BFUU Social Justice Committee.
 
For more information call 510-219-0092 or 415- 578-7930
