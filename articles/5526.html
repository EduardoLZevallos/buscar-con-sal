ROSARIO: "The World must build Community, the World must build Dialogue and Peace, the World needs Security"

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 19/11/2024 - 12:29

Declarations of Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua,
after the Event marking the XVII China - Latin America Business Summit

November 18th 2024

A very good afternoon, good evening. A day full of achievements, since what concerns us day by day, as we believe and create, is to build Well-being, to make progress against Poverty.

We believe first of all in our God who Blesses this Country of ours and who Blesses us All, who gives us Health and intelligence, we are sure. He gives us the Wisdom, the Serenity to seek Peace and ensure the Rights of all Nicaraguan Families.

Peace, which is Harmony, which is Stability, which is Security, which is Work. Events of great importance like this one, are events that carry us forward, how? By building opportunities that in fact are really rights for Everyone.

From all these alliances that we are forging and above all with the brilliant, constant cooperation of the People's Republic of China, that meeting of Peoples with a Power which prioritizes Solidarity and respectful, unconditional Cooperation, with that extraordinary motto of China's President, “A Future, and what that Future will be, one of Shared Community.” The Community that builds a Shared Future.

The World must build Community, the World must build Dialogue and Peace, the World needs Security, the Peoples need to feel productive, because we are indeed productive, competent, because we are capable, responsible for our present and our future, but by Governments building via the Union and the Alliance of Peoples and Governments, the Routes that are those of Rights, that are Routes for Prosperity.

To advance against the Misery others imposed on us; the slavery and immiseration they imposed on the whole world. To advance, breaking down all those barriers, because they are barriers, they are walls of cruelty, of hatred, that are inhuman.

Moving onward, breaking down those barriers, and building a Future of human beings, for human beings, that is the challenge, it is the challenge that we face with God's Blessing.

An embrace to all Nicaraguan Families in these days that are of Goodness; in these days that announce Prosperity; that announce Progress; that announce the Future we deserve, and thus we continue working in the Always Further On! with our General Sandino, looking forward, never back!

And above all, recognizing ourselves as Brothers and Sisters, as Human Community, and placing Love at the center of everything, and in the case of our Latin American and Caribbean Peoples, from our Christianity because that is our Culture and our Faith. Christianity, Socialism, which mean Rights, Progress, Solidarity, which mean the Common Good that is an essential part of our Peoples and of what we are. Solidarity reflects in us as a principle of Humanity.

And a warm embrace for everyone, as again we say, thanks too for this Prodigious Union of Peoples and Governments, that we have found this Power, that we have had the opportunity to meet and unite with a Power... A Power contributing to the Well-being of our Peoples.

Warm and good embraces, for We Are Going Onward, Always Further On! Embraces Compañeras and Compañeros, thank you.
