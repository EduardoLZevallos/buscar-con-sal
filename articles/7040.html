International Committee
for Peace, Justice and Dignity
Join us for an Evening Event in San Francisco with Special Guest Dan Kovalik,
Saturday August 17, 7:00PM
Dan Kovalik is touring the Bay Area and we will have an unique opportunity to hear him talk about the latest on Venezuela, and other Latin American countries.
 
Saturday August 17, 7:00PM
The Eric Quezada Center for Culture and Politics
518 Valencia, San Francisco
Donations at the door no-one turned away for lack of funds.
 
"Kovalik helps to cut through the Orwellian lies...when such truth-telling is so desperately needed." Oliver Stone
 
Dan Kovalik is the author of The Plot to Overthrow Venezuela, The Plot to Scapegoat Russia, The Plot to Attack Iran, and The Plot to Control the World. Formerly a labor lawyer for the United Steel Workers Union, now teaches human rights law at the University of Pittsburgh.
 

Co-sponsored by Task Force on the Americas and the International Committee for Peace, Justice and Dignity.
