Government pursues common good despite opposition provocations

Submitted bytortilla onMar, 21/05/2019 - 09:45

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS


Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE DELEGATION OF GOVERNMENT OF
RECONCILIATION AND NATIONAL UNITY (GRUN)
TO THE NEGOTIATION TABLE

Tuesday, May 21, 2019

“Vibrant, strong, passionate, proud people; People who have the awareness of being alive, and that gathering their energies in portentous beam, demonstrates to the vigorous Motherland that it can courageously present in its right hand the steel of war or the olive tree of Peace.”

Ruben Dario
(Return)

We thank God who always teaches us His Ways, for these Days of renewed Life, reborn Herbs, green Fields, as the greening of Hope and Faith is revived in all our Sacred Geography.

We thank God, commending ourselves to His designs, trusting in Him, and knowing that He does and will do.

We do not get impatient because of those who move Plans against the Common Good. We do get impatient for the breaches or absences far from the Aspirations and Rights of the Nicaraguan Families. We remain Serene and Prudent, in Respectful Silence before the Will of God, and we know that He “exhibits Justice as Light, and Right as noon.”

We do not let ourselves be provoked, nor do we react with anger or rage. We know that waiting for God will recreate us all with "abundance of Peace".

The Holy Scriptures say that “little by little there will be no evil... you will observe his place, and he will not be there...” That is why nothing disturbs us, and nothing moves us away from the Commitment to fulfill the Nicaraguan Families that want Tranquility, Work, Security and Peace, to follow in Blessings, Prosperities and in Victories.

The Delegation of the GRUN to the Negotiation Table knows that we are working with the Heart and with absolute Good Faith to advance towards the Perfect Days of Solidarity and Affection, which we are restoring fully in our Christian Motherland, Always Blessed and Always Free.

“The steps of a good Woman and Man are ordered by the Lord: and He delighteth in His way.

Turn from Evil and do Good;
then you will dwell in the land forever.”

 ./.

As faithful interpreters and representatives of the Vocation of Peace, Unity and Future for the Nicaraguan Families, we inform to everyone that the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity, that we represent, will continue to comply with all the Agreements taken at the Negotiation Table and moving forward in what corresponds to the actions of the Nicaraguan State, in order to guarantee that all the Points of the Agenda and Road Map will be fulfilled.

While the other party continues to debate in sterile doubts, discussions and even threats, which do not correspond to the Interest and Common Good of our Nicaragua, we will continue working with Nicaragua for Peace and Wellbeing, protecting us all from Evil snares, which nobody wants in our Country.

With Nicaraguan Pride we proclaim, that with the Responsibility, Seriousness and Unquestionable Will of the State, the Government and the People of Nicaragua, we will continue to create the New Times that the Motherland demands, in our Heaven and in our Ground, Sovereign, Dignified, Just and Fraternal.

Today, May 21, we congratulate ourselves for having unilaterally advanced in the fulfillment of the Agreement for the release of Persons who violated Public Security and Order. To date, 336 of these Persons have been released, pending, to comply before June 18, with 132 additional releases.

Once again, we demonstrate that here is a People and a Government that knows how to honor our Commitments, in time and form.

In these Days of Honor and Glory, we remember all the Nicaraguans who sacrificed their Lives in Patriotic Love, in the Days before July 19. With Joy and Conviction of New Victories this 19/J we will be commemorating 40 Years of the Historical Triumph of our Popular Sandinista Revolution, and we continue celebrating as a vibrant, strong, passionate, proud People; A People who are conscious of being alive, and gathering their energies in a portentous beam, demonstrates to the vigorous Motherland that it can bravely present in its right hand the steel of war or the olive branch of Peace.

We move Forward! Forward in Victories! We continue with Faith, Hope and Trust in God, in Nicaragua and in the Nicaraguan Families!

Always and Beyond... Towards the Light...
Towards the Truth!

“Turn from Evil and do Good;
then you will dwell in the land forever.

For the Lord loves the just
and will not forsake his faithful ones;

./.

The mouths of the righteous utter wisdom,
and their tongues speak what is just.

The law of their God is in their hearts;
their feet do not slip.

./.

The salvation of the righteous comes from the Lord; He is their stronghold in time of trouble.

The Lord helps them and delivers them;
He delivers them from the wicked and saves them, because they take refuge in Him”.

(Psalm 37: 27-40)

Tuesday, May 21, 2019

Delegation of GRUN
at the Negotiation Table
