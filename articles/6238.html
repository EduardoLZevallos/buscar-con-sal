ROSARIO: "April to remind them, to make sure they don’t forget, They Could Not Prevail, And They Never Will!"

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 14/03/2025 - 18:54

Initial remarks by Rosario, Co-President of Nicaragua,
in the Midday Edition of Multinoticias Channel 4

March 14th 2025

A very good afternoon, Compañera. Good afternoon, Beloved Families of our Nicaragua, Ours, Our Own, Our Very Own, of Nicaraguans... Nicaraguans, by the grace of God!

April, Nation and Heart. April of our Patriotic Nation and the Heart to Continue to Overcome... Because there is a Patriotic Nation. Because there is Nicaragua. Because there is a Revolution. Because there is a Sandinista Front for National Liberation. Because we are a Revolutionary and Evolutionary People. Because we are make our way with Sandino, with Zeledón, with Carlos, with Tomás, with Daniel... Because We Are Heading, as a People President, Always Further On!

Holy April in Nicaragua. April deliberately desecrated in Nicaragua. April in Nicaragua and so much Holy Blood spilled out of Hatred, out of Ambition!

April Consecrated to Love in Nicaragua and under mass attacked by Filibusters, Pharisees and Demons, the very spawn of William Walker!

Blessed April...! Our Patriotic Nation is always at its highest, claiming the Dreams of “the Month Most Raw for Planting”, in the words of the Hero and Poet Leonel Rugama... In the Month Most Raw for Planting and in the heat of Combat.

April for being reborn with the Strength of the Nicaraguan Soul, by the Grace of God!

And that's how we confronted the sacrilege of those nobodies !

Here, no one surrenders! No one here can be bought! The Renegades, who were born traitors and unworthy, who are cowardly vandals, lackeys and slaves of the Imperialists of the Earth, are no longer Nicaraguans, thanks be to God!

April to live with Christ, and in Christ Jesus, the Spirit, the Strength, the Fortitude, the Faith, the Hope, that floods us!

April of the Patriotic Nation and the Heart to Continue to Overcome! Because there is our Nicaragua, there is our Patriotic Nation, there is our Revolution... And how it muct burn them! That there is a Patriotic Nation, that there is a Revolution, and that We Are Always Going Further On, with Sandino, Zeledón, Carlos, Tomás, Daniel, with the People President!

April to remind them, to make sure they don’t forget, They Could Not Prevail, And They Never Will!

How it must burn, how April must burn for the defeated...! How it thunders at them, stinging, splashing, and spreading their defeat! How it must burn them...! They Couldn't Prevail, They Never Will! It is a Decree of Life and Hope. A Decree of Love for Nicaragua. A Patriotic Decree of those who love Peace. A Decree to scare away the buzzards, full of hatred, who are the living embodiment of betrayal, cowardice and every vice.

Betrayal, all the Deadly Sins, are Hell, just as Hell is Betrayal, the condemnation of those who have no Patriotism, or Self Knowledge, incapable of any Great Love, the very Love that we know, that we practice, that asks for nothing in return... Only to see, just to see that Dreams come true, that Concord vibrates in every Heart in Infinite Melody.

This Blessed Patriotic Nation is protected by that Great Love. So get used to it, you vandals, failures and vanquished... Unsuccessful and defeated vandals!

How they sob at their failure... They cringe like vampires on seeing the Cross... Let them whinge because they are bloodsucking gangsters, who, thank God, have already "bitten the dust of our rugged mountains”" as our Undefeated General, our General Sandino, used to say and still does.

How the Powerful Hand of Blessed Nicaragua must burn  them...! To Hell, to hell they went. And now what are they? The very image of the most absolute collapse.

Here no one surrenders! True Nicaraguan Souls have never been for sale! The stateless, those with no Nation, the criminals, the unworthy, the bloodthirsty, they amount to not even a shadow, and it is a duty to remind them of it, so they may live and relive their most bitter disaster.

In Nicaragua we are the Children of all our Heroes, of all our Great Beings, of all our Extraordinary Human Beings and Formidable Spiritual Beings. In our hands what do we have? The Stone of Andres, the Stone of Hope, the Strength of Love, the Stone of Dignity, the Strength of Spirit, the most Splendid and Sublime Inheritances... Conscience, Bravery, Nobility, History, Glory, Memory, the Courage to know how to be Free, and never heartless slaves, never those heartless zombies.

April... How April must burn those vanquished Losers, Souls of the Devil!
