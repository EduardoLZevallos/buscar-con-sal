More Aggression Against the Truth !

Enviado por tortilla el Dom, 15/09/2024 - 18:47


MORE AGGRESSION AGAINST THE TRUTH!

When will the United States of North America learn that with the acts of aggression they blatantly call Sanctions, (as if they were divinely ordained to dole out punishments)... they are making no more sense other than confirming their pretension to be dictators of the world...!

When are they going to understand that this is another world in which they have neither the moral authority nor the political stature to pose as Prosecutors, Judges or the Police of the Planet...?

When will the Yankees learn that humanity does not belong to them, and no one has declared them to be God's Children, with a Divine Right to continue ravaging us...?

When will those United States, invaders of the whole World, not only of Our America, as our Rubén Darío predicted; when will they have the common sense that even Elementary Intelligence gives to understand that every Country, every People, every Human Being, has as we all have the Right, History, the Memory and the Future, to defend ourselves from Evil and walk with the serene Wisdom and Knowledge of the Human Spirit, and of our own eloquence...?

As the History of Humanity and Civilizations has shown and continues to show, every person, every People, every Culture, is in their own right a Sacred Territory, and we all have the Courage and Reason to protect what belongs to us, to proclaim our Truths, our Principles and Values, with Pride and Certainty.

Because Justice is on its way. Because arrogance and hubris are the dust of defeat for those who believe themselves to be gods, being in fact what they really are, mere statues of clay in absolutely pitiful ignorance and frivolity!

Brothers and Sisters of RT, Sputnik, Rossiya Segodnya, once again our Recognition and Respect for your work and for the creative, thoughtful, illustrative, sensitive and moving way you manage to communicate from Soul to Soul, from Heart to Heart.

We are Beings of Spirit, and the Feelings, the Emotions, the depth in the exposition of our Realities and Truths, certainly move us spiritually, as they should, as one Great Human Community and Family.

The ”key role" of RT is to promote and proclaim a New Culture of Human Spiritual Potency. Because that's the way it is.

Congratulations again for Knowing, Believing, and Prevailing...

Rosario Murillo

 

Managua, September 13th 2024
