False beliefs on Nicaragua : foreign news messaging for Nicaragua's minority opposition

Stephen Sefton, Tortilla con Sal, June 1st 2019

Foreign news coverage by North American or European propaganda media outlets long ago abandoned rational critical thought for demented false beliefs. Here, a few examples from Nicaragua contrast Western media messaging on behalf of Nicaragua's minority political opposition with what they really mean:

The list of insane falsehoods goes on and on. In this case the target country is Nicaragua, but the same applies to Cuba, Venezuela, Iran, Russia, China, North Korea, anywhere resistant to or competing with US and EU foreign policy imperatives. Phony news coverage like this, denying Western news consumers true and fair reporting, is a deliberate attack on democracy. Just as in Venezuela and Cuba, Nicaragua's minority political opposition depend on people's ignorance outside the country so as to foment their sinister fantasy version of Nicaragua's reality. Inside Nicaragua, life goes on as normal under the most successful government the country has ever had.
