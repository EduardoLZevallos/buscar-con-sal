With Love For Nicaragua

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 21/01/2025 - 13:11

WITH LOVE FOR NICARAGUA

1. We call on every one of us to participate actively in all the Activities we will be carrying out on February 21st and 26th in the Presentations of our Nicaraguan Army and the Swearing-in of our Volunteer Police Officers.

    As we all know, we will conclude this Phase of Presentations and Swearing-in with the Inauguration of Army General Julio César Avilés Castillo, at the head of our Nicaraguan Army as Commander-in-Chief, and of First Commissioner General Francisco Díaz, as National Director of our Police.

2. On February 6th we will highlight the Life and Work, the Mastery and Magic, the Sublime Hope, the Heavenly Hope that Rubén Darío, Pride of Nicaragua and the World, inspires in us daily with his Eternal Poetry.

In León Santiago de los Caballeros, Capital of the Revolution, we pay well-deserved and joyful tribute to the Optimism that our Darío has bequeathed us, and having DARÍO WITHIN US we proclaim the Union of Us All, for the Common Good of Everyone.

3. We are called upon to participate in Recognition and Respect for a Hero of the Latin American Revolution who gave his Life for Justice and Peace for our Peoples, Father and Comandante Camilo Torres Restrepo.

    We will honor his Memory and march with his Legacy from the University Center for Agricultural and Livestock Export Technologies, where thousands of young people from Nicaragua's Northern Departments will be studying, creating a Bright Future of Work, Security, Hope and Prosperity.

    These Activities will be held on February 3rd on the 95th Birthday of Father Camilo Torres Restrepo.

4. We are now working on beautiful, joyful and festive Activities in a Campaign that we will call NICARAGUAAMOR, which we are developing between February 7th and 16th with Fairs for the Affectionate Promotion of Creative Ventures, and Promotion of Tourist Activities involving packages prepared by INTUR and the Attorney General's  Municipalities Office, for us to join together in Celebration of Faith, Hope and Love, representing the intense and vibrant Peace that prevails, Thanks be to God, in our Nicaragua.

    NICARAGUAAMOR will be celebrated in all our Municipalities with small businesses enhancing Recreational and Tourist Services and motivating Quality, Imagination, Artistic Talent among the Hardworking Families of our NICARAGUAAMOR.

    On those days, from the various Schools, Study and Training Centers we will also highlight the privileged relationship of Love that creates through having faith in ourselves, illuminating the Future of the Nicaraguan People.

    We will celebrate in a big way this trait that defines us as Spiritual Beings, capable of living with Love, and through Love, ensuring Peace and Goodness throughout our Blessed Nation.

5. SANDINO LIVES and we proclaim it this way daily as we make our way, with our path breaking, irrevocable conviction to consolidate this Christian, Socialist and Solidarity Inspired Project, which is based on the Ideals and Values of our Immortal Augusto Nicolás Calderón Sandino, seeking Justice, Peace and Prosperity, from the deepest National Dignity, for all Nicaraguans proud to be so, by the Grace of God.

    We will hold Keynote Lectures, Assemblies, Meetings, Distribution of Party Cards, Visits by our Social, Community, and Youth Leaders to all the Sandinista Families throughout the country embracing us in Steadfast Patriotism.
    
    Throughout the country we will place flowers on the Monuments and Spaces dedicated to our General and the Army In Defense National Sovereignty, whose irreplaceable Values strengthen Our Character and all that Distinguishes Us as Sandinista Revolutionaries.

    Our Youth organizations, local municipal Offices, Government Institutions, Powers of State, Embassies and Missions, will hold Concerts, Recitals, Parades, Artistic and Cultural Activities, in Full and proud Identification with our Heroic Heritage, Reason and Being, the Being and Reason of our Luminous and Transcendent Presence, as a Magnificent and Heroic People who “Neither Sell Out, Nor Surrender, Ever!”.

6. On February 26th we will pay heartfelt, colorful and committed tribute to the Heroes of MONIMBÓ, Representatives of the Combativeness, Courage and the Decision to Fight to Overcome of the Original Peoples of our Blessed Nicaragua.

    Monimbó is still Nicaragua, Live with it...! And by evoking those Memorable Days, impregnated and sanctified by the Sacred Blood of all our Heroes and Martyrs, between Songs, Dances, Flowers and Cries of Viva!, we swear to defend their Victories.

    In all the Centers of Study, in all the Town Halls, Units of Victory, and institutions of our Revolutionary State, we recognize their Legacy and we will raise the Flags of Glories and Triumphs of our Original Peoples' Heroes, of the Sandinista National Liberation Front, of Carlos, Tomás, Fernando Gordillo, and all those who sang the Value of those of us who as Nicaraguans have been and are capable of dreaming and building the United, Blessed and Prosperous Nation, enjoying, like Leonel Rugama, the Promised Land that is the Territory of the Noble and the Brave and their unwavering Determination to Fight to Overcome.

With Undefeated Hopes, affirming Certainties, with our unswerving resolution to the Life and Force of Love and Peace, in NICARAGUAAMOR We Go Onward with our History, Memory and Glory, Always Further On, because we have been able and continue to be able to transcend all malevolence, moving onward, with the Powerful and Lucid Spirit of Us All.

The struggles continue...
The victories are certain!
Always Further On!

Managua, January 21st 2025
Rosario Murillo
