Enough of Interference Already !

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 27/08/2024 - 19:49

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

ENOUGH OF INTERFERENCE ALREADY!

The Government of Reconciliation and National Unity of Nicaragua, clarifies to the gentlemen and ladies of the European Union, that Nicaragua is a Country and a patriotic Nation, Blessed, Free and Sovereign, and thus, it is not a Colony, nor does it receive or obey orders from any Country in the World.

The Nicaraguan authorities' decisions are made based on the Constitution of the Republic, and the Laws that, as an Independent Country and People, govern the operation of our sovereign institutions.

We remind the gentlemen and ladies of the European Union that the History on which your Countries are based is one of atrocities and crimes against humanity, committed in the voracious desire to subjugate Peoples, to steal their Riches, their Natural and Cultural Heritage and the Supreme Intelligence of the World's Continents.

A visit to Europe's museums is enough to see how proud you are of the savagery, the barbarism, the immorality with which you “conquered” Countries and Peoples from whom you took everything, in exchange for nothing.

The genocide being committed every day in Gaza is just a pale reflection of the Crusades and bloody Conquests of your Empires, of the Colonizers and Neo-Colonialists who still, boldly, try to dictate rules, to a World which not only ignores you, but condemns you.

Enough of interference, of falsehoods, distortions and abuses in the name of a supposed but theatrical “democracy”  bathed in the blood, misery, sweat and tears of the territories where you settled to kill, exterminate and loot.

Comply, European gentlemen and ladies, with respect for the Models and Ways of Life of Brave, Free Peoples, who strongly reject the hypocrisy in your interventionist voices, and who condemn you every day, for your reprehensible history of ignominy.

We are concerned that the legitimate demands of our Peoples have not been listened to, much less fulfilled, by all your phony countries, boldly, shamelessly, and arrogantly believing yourselves masters, when all you represent is the worst and most sacrilegious of the Human Species.

Managua, August 27th 2024
Government of Reconciliation
and National Unity
Presidency of the Republic
