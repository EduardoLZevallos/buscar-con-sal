Venezuela Is Winning Still !

Enviado por tortilla el Lun, 29/07/2024 - 18:54

PRESS RELEASE

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

VENEZUELA IS WINNING STILL !

 

The Government of Reconciliation and National Unity of the Brave, Dignified and Free People of Darío and Sandino, denounces before the International Community what, in accordance with our own experiences of the Imperialists of the Earth, is becoming more and more self-evident.

 

We denounce to the world that the People of Bolívar, of Chávez, of Nicolás, who have resisted and defeated every attempt to humiliate and destroy their Rights, by winning once more in these Elections, defeating the Imperialists and their shamelessness, are now facing a new attempted Coup d'État, based on arrogance and the permanent ambition to dominate and plunder our Peoples and our Resources, colluding again today, continuing to exert every kind of hatred against that Admirable Venezuela, of Great Battles and Infinite Victories.

 

We add our own Experience and Voice to the Peoples and Governments of the World who want Peace, Justice, and the Right to work and prosper together in our own Lands, in Respectful, Fraternal Solidarity and Cooperation.

 

The Fascism that today seeks to rampage again across Venezuela’s Lands, of course It Will Not Pass!

 

We Make Walls of Sublime Will, summoning ourselves to accompany Venezuela in Spiritual Battles and in Formidable Paths of Victory and Light, facing with Virtuosity the Darkness which Has Not Prevailed, Cannot Prevail, Nor Will It Prevail!

 

Always Further On, Nicolás, Heroic Venezuela, Peoples of Our America-and-Caribbean!
