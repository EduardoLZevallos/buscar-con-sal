Nicaragua's government insists on call to end illegal unilateral sanctions

Submitted bytortilla onJue, 02/05/2019 - 09:55

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS


Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE DELEGATION OF THE GRUN
TO THE NEGOTIATION TABLE

Thursday, May 2, 2019

The Delegation of the GRUN to the Negotiation Table greets the Nicaraguan families this May 2, and informs each one of them, and the International Community, that we are making ourselves present at INCAE, as we do every day, starting at 10 am in the morning, to continue working for Consensus that contributes to the Stability, Reunion, Reconciliation and Peace, demanded by the Nicaraguan people.

The Delegation of the GRUN to the Negotiation Table also reiterates to our People and the International Community that all the Agreements that we have taken, as well as those that we still have to reach, will be implemented in strict adherence to our Constitution, our Laws and Legal Framework.

We reiterate that it is elementary for Nicaragua and Nicaraguans to respect and honor our Democracy and our Rule of Law, without advocating for alterations or impositions that violate the legal construction of our Magna Carta, and Maximum Law. Any claim to continue violating the fundamental Rights and Guarantees of Nicaraguans, constitutes an absolute illegality.

As the Delegation of the GRUN to the Negotiation Table informs the Nicaraguan People and the International Community about the status of the Negotiations, it ratifies, once again, its commitment to comply with what is agreed, implementing each Measure, in accordance with our Laws, in the times and schedules established in the Agreements themselves.

We also consider allegiance to the Homeland and the People elementary, to recognize the damage that Illegal and Unilateral Sanctions inflict on all Sectors of the Nicaraguan Society and, particularly, on the most vulnerable, the poorest. The GRUN, by history, by reason of being, and by fidelity and commitment, has always worked, for a “Preferential Option for the Poor”, whom we consider on equal footing, Protagonists of a real Democracy, with Rights, that contemplates all aspects of Social, Labor, Political, Economic and Cultural Life.

We have insisted and insist on the claim that must be made about the Illegal and Unilateral Sanctions that have been applied to Nicaragua. We affirm once again that to ignore this demand is to declare ourselves happy and joyful accomplices of interventionist Policies and violations of Human Rights, and of all the Rights of the Nicaraguan People. That refusal is comparable only to the servile and shameless attitudes of these very Sectors, who refused to denounce and eliminate the roadblocks that our people described and still qualify today as “roadblocks of death.”

Not accepting to include this historic just, and absolutely necessary demand of the Nicaraguan people, is to be total accomplices of hatred, anxiety, violence, discrimination, terrorism, and denial of Life.

As confessed... those who do not accept to recognize the claim on the Illegal Sanctions, are set once again, in tune with Purposes and Plans incompatible with Christian Love, with our Spirit, or with the Freedom, the Justice, the Democracy, National Dignity, and the Reason that assists the Nicaraguan families.

Today, May 2, we begin, precisely, the Month of National Dignity, the Month of the Holy Waters, the Festive Traditions of our People, the Birth of Sandino, General of Dignified Men and Women, a Month that is loved and expected by the Families that celebrate the Blessings of Living with Mother's Love in Nicaragua. At the beginning of this Sacred Month, we reiterate to our People in this Our Homeland, Always Blessed, Always Dignified and Always Free, as well as to the International Community, our commitment and decision to move forward, among all Miseries, fostering In Love of Nicaragua, Respect, Common Good, Justice, Reparation, Work, Peace, Prosperity, Joy and Victories.

Managua, Thursday, May 2, 2019

Delegation of the Government of
Reconciliation and National Unity
to the Negotiation Table
