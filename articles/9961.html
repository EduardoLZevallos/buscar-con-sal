Celebrate International Women's Alliance 10 Years of Resistance - August 10-16

Submitted bytortilla onDom, 09/08/2020 - 18:32

Celebrating IWA’s 10 Years of Resistance: Strengthening the Global Militant Women’s Movement against Imperialism!

The International Women’s Alliance (IWA)
is pleased to announce our
10 Year Anniversary Celebration where we invite you to participate in a Global Week of Action
from August 10-16, 2020.

This will culminate into an online celebration on August 16th, where we will highlight victories of our work
and learn from each other on building the militant women's movement against Imperialism.

IWA 10 Year Anniversary GLOBAL WEEK OF ACTION August 10th -16th

Share the week of action on FB

All member organizations are encouraged to participate in the following events or produce your own actions, events, or prop materials during the week of action and/or the month of August. We will begin the week of action in an educational webinar on the Global Situation of Women on August 10th, and culminate the week of action on our 10 year Anniversary Celebration on August 16th in a 2 hour celebratory online gathering. 

Additionally, IWA is calling for women around the world to share their stories of resilience and determination in the face of the global pandemic. We will collect and share these Pandemic Stories through the month of August.  Send your pandemic stories to internationalwomensalliance@gmail.com

August 10 - Global Situation of Women
     •Join IWA for an educational webinar on the Global political situation of women under the current pandemic. Learn about the struggles, resistance and resilience of women against Imperialism!

August 16 - Celebrating IWA’s 10 Years of Resistance - Online Celebration!
     •An online celebration for IWA’s 10 Years of fight, resistance, struggle and unity against Imperialism. Our program will include a brief report on the global situation on women, messages from our Executive Committee, Solidarity Messages from women all over the world, and highlights from IWA's Women of Valor, cultural presentations and more. We are very excited to share this time with you!

The International Women’s Alliance (IWA) is a global alliance of grassroots-based women’s organizations, institutions,
alliances, networks and individuals committed to advancing the women's movement in the 21st century and in moving forward
the liberation of women. For more information, visit IWA's website at https://intlwomensalliance.org/.
