Veterans for Peace - On-Line Convention August 12-15 2021

Submitted bytortilla onJue, 13/05/2021 - 17:27

Registration is now open for Veterans For Peace's Online Convention!

Veterans For Peace will hold our convention online this year again August 12-15th!

This year's theme is Rising to the Moment.  

We had an amazing experience last year and it was our highest attended convention yet! We know that many of us will miss being able to greet each other in person but we also know that each and every one of us wants to make sure all of our members are safe and healthy.

We are now taking Workshop proposals and finalizing our program!  Be sure to register early for the early bird discount!  We have created a Convention website that detail all things convention!  Be sure to check it out!

We are so excited to be gathering "virtually" again and look forward to seeing new and old faces!

2021 VFP Convention Committee

The 2021 Convention theme this year is “Rising to the Moment.” We are asking members to submit workshop applications based on themes listed here.

We will have the capacity to host nine workshops during this year’s convention and priority will be given to submitters who have a connection with or are organizing via Veterans For Peace already. Complete applications will also be prioritized and incomplete applications will be returned for further work. 

The application deadline for workshops to be considered is June 14th, 2021. This deadline will be firmly adhered to as hosting workshops online requires a tremendous amount of preparation. 
Submit your workshop!

VFP will offer five awards this year. Four of the five awards are for individuals with the fifth award going to Chapter of the Year.

The deadline for the awards nominations is June 5th, 2021!

The award categories are:

  • The Gandhian Non-Violence Award 
  • Howard Zinn Lifetime Achievement Award
  • Service and Stewardship Award 
  • Leadership in Peace Award
  • Chapter of the Year Award

 
Nominate someone!

If you would like to read a poem or sing a song at our virtual 2021 VFP Convention Poetry and Song Soirée, please sign up now so that we can email subsequent announcements, reminders, and other communications relating to this special event.

Guidelines:

  • You must be a current member of Veterans For Peace (associate members welcome).
  • Poetry, spoken word, and song are all welcome. Original works preferred.
  • Creative content should reflect the concerns, values, and mission of Veterans For Peace.

Virtual Business Meeting / Resolutions & Bylaws

Due to the virtual nature of the VFP Convention this year, the business meeting will be conducted over Zoom. Resolutions and By-law amendments will be released before the business meeting for members to review and post their comments. 

All Members, including both Full Members and Associate Members, shall be entitled to speak to resolutions presented before the Annual Convention. All Members, including both Full Members and Associate Members, shall be entitled to one vote on resolutions. Only Full Members may vote on bylaw amendments.

More information on submitting Resolutions & Bylaws.

Deadline for submission is July 14th.

You do not need to register for Convention in order to participate in the business meeting, but it is open to active VFP members only. Click the correct button below to renew your membership or join VFP today!
