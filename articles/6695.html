Address by Compañero Mauricio Aguilar

Address by Compañero Chen Xi
Ambassador of the People's Republic of China
in Nicaragua

Beloved Co-Presidents, Comandante Daniel Ortega and Compañera Rosario Murillo; Beloved Compañero Fidel Moreno and other Compañeros Presiding this event; Beloved Compañer@s of Public Transport and the Media. Good afternoon to all and everyone.

It has been a great joy to attend, once again, the Ceremony of Handing Over Buses from the People's Republic of China.

This batch of 100 Units of Chinese Buses has been a donation from the Government of the People's Republic of China to the Government of the Republic of Nicaragua, within the framework of a non-refundable assistance package from the Chinese Government to the Nicaraguan Government.

Throughout the three years of our diplomatic relations between China and Nicaragua, within the framework of Non-Refundable Assistance, the Chinese Government has provided Nicaragua with a variety of equipment and materials to support the Social and Economic Production Programs promoted by the Sandinista Government, led by Co-Presidents Daniel Ortega and Compañera Rosario Murillo.

In addition, the construction of the First Phase of the Housing program “Nuevas Victorias” in Managua of 920 houses, also under the framework of Non-Refundable Assistance, will be completed within a few weeks for its prompt delivery, while preparatory works for the Second Phase of the Housing Project “Nuevas Victorias are progressing successfully, for prompt implementation also.

The "Luban Workshop" Project, a Technological Vocational Training Program, also with Non-refundable financing from the Chinese Government, is progressing rapidly, for its delivery and commissioning by the end of this year, in order to support the Nicaraguan Government in its effort to raise the level of Technical and Vocational Education.

It is also worth mentioning that the Female Children's Choir "China-Nicaragua Friendship", founded a month ago and supported with Chinese social resources, has established a magnificent platform on which Nicaraguan girls between three and nine years old can now interpret Chinese and Nicaraguan songs with their beautiful voices.

Several other Government Cooperation Projects and Programs between China and Nicaragua are being intensively studied and planned for their impending implementation.

While both Governments are cooperating closely on Social, Health, Educational, Science and Technology Projects, private Companies from both countries have also been cooperating closely. The construction of the Punta Huete International Airport, the Photovoltaic Power Plants of San Isidro and El Hato, the National Emergency Response System (SINAREM) Project, among others, are cooperative works that are in the full construction process; and several other Projects are in friendly and fruitful negotiations, for their impending execution.

None of this Cooperation or the Projects undertaken, both by the Chinese Government and by Chinese Companies, are subject to any political conditions, nor diplomatic impositions. All of them are developed jointly on the basis of Solidarity, Mutual Respect and Reciprocal Benefit, with the aim of supporting the Nicaraguan Government in its Fight against Poverty, and seeking the Well-being of the Nicaraguan People, who during the Sandinista Government and led by the Co-Presidents, Comandante Daniel Ortega and Compañera Rosario Murillo, have achieved historic and great successes.

Just a little over a week ago, the IV Ministerial Meeting of the China-Community of Latin US and Caribbean States (CELAC) Forum was successfully held in Beijing, the Capital of China.

During the Meeting, Chinese President Xi Jinping gave an important speech. President Xi pointed out that, at present, faced with the accelerated evolution of changes never seen in a century, and the multiple intertwined risks, only with Solidarity and Collaboration can Countries safeguard World Peace and Stability, and promote Global Development and Prosperity.

There are no winners in the tariff and trade wars; those practicing intimidating and arrogant behavior only serve to isolate themselves.

China and Latin America and also the Caribbean are a Community with a Shared Future that advances hand in hand. China is willing to work together with the Countries of Latin America and the Caribbean, including our beloved Nicaragua, to undertake Five Programs; namely: The Solidarity Program, the Development Program, the Civilizations Program, the Peace Program, and the Peoples Program, in order to plan together for development, revitalization and building together the China-Latin America and the Caribbean Community of Shared Future.

The Solidarity Program aims to consolidate the political base, because Solidarity is Strength; the Development Program seeks to promote common Prosperity, because Development is a necessary foundation; the Civilizations Program aims to deepen mutual learning among Civilizations, because the diversity of Civilizations is what makes the World the way it should be; the Peace Program seeks to maintain Regional Stability; and the Peoples Program aims to improve Friendship among Peoples.

The Five Programs proposed by President Xi Jinping have charted the course for the construction of the China-Latin America and the Caribbean Community of Shared Future.

At the instruction of the Co-Presidents, Comandante Daniel Ortega and Compañera Rosario Murillo, Compañero Laureano Ortega, Presidential Advisor for International Trade, Investments and Cooperation, led a High-level Delegation to attend this IV Ministerial Meeting of the China-CELAC Forum, in which Compañero Laureano addressed important words, reiterating the firmness of the Nicaraguan Government supporting the Principle of One China, adherence to all the Initiatives proposed by President Xi Jinping; the promotion for the construction of the China-Latin America and Caribbean Shared Future Community, and leading the Nicaraguan People to a brighter Future.

During his stay in China, in addition to fulfilling all the Agendas of the Ministerial Meeting, Compañero Laureano held, in just two days, intense meetings with more than a dozen Chinese companies to promote and sign several Cooperation Projects in important sectors, such as Transport, Communication, Science and Technology, etc. Projects that, without a doubt, will support the Socio-economic Development of Nicaragua and the Fraternal Friendship between China and Nicaragua.

Nicaragua is an important Partner in the construction of the Community of Shared Future between China and CELAC. China reaffirms its will to further consolidate high-quality mutual trust, deepen Cooperation and provide continuous support to Nicaragua, to carry out, as a matter of priority and on the basis of mutual respect, Projects that contribute to the autonomous development of Nicaragua, and to the well-being of the Nicaraguan People.

On the eve of Mother's Day, to conclude, I want to take this opportunity to convey my warm and sincere greeting to Compañera Rosario Murillo, Co-President of the Republic of Nicaragua, and to all the Heroic and Hardworking Nicaraguan Mothers, wishing them much Health, much Happiness, and Good Fortune for their Families.

Thank you very much to everyone.
