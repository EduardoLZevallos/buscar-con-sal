ROSARIO: "We the Peoples are the ones who are fighting and so we know that the New World is ours, that the Future belongs to us"

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 05/11/2024 - 18:15

Opening words of declarations by Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua,
in the midday edition of Multinoticias on Canal 4

November 5th 2024

A very good afternoon, Compañera, and very good afternoon, beloved families of this Nicaragua of Ours, Always full of Honor, Glory and Victories.

Today we have been watching the second session, the second day of the World Parliamentary Forum against Fascism being held in Caracas, Venezuela, where our Delegation has arrived and spoken on behalf of all of us, our Brother and Hero Orlando Tardencilla, who has spoken for us, speaking out as we always do, against all the criminal strategies trying to institutionalize the horrors, the attacks against the life and legitimate Rights of Peoples. And we have spoken out, once again, against hatred, human misery, discord, disunity, and everything that sows evil and death among Brothers and Sisters, among the Human Community.

Orlando said: "With horror and indignation we watch the multiplication of very sophisticated aggression against Peoples whose only crime is to live with dignity. These acts rejected by the vast majority of States and Peoples of the world require the adoption of urgent measures demanding an end to these criminal acts against people's lives.

We parliamentarians have particular challenges, as direct representatives of our Peoples, to maintain our firm position as an Anti-Imperialist and Anti-Fascist Forum in the face of the perversions of Imperialism and its lackeys, its sad and servile flunkeys.”

We have spoken out saying that the Victory over Fascism is coming and it will be True. We do not doubt it.

We speak with the undefeated Heart of our Heroes, from the Honor and Pride of a Combative Nicaragua, where we have always fought, and still fight, against hatred in all its forms, against interference, interventions, the brashness and brazenness, the impudence, of those who think they own the world. We discussed among Brothers and Sisters proclaiming Full Rights for all Humanity.

And by that uncompromising demand for Justice and Truth, we know we are making our way in Glory and Victories, among all the provocations of arrogance, supremacy, conceit, the vanity of all vanity, the falsity of all falsehood, the idols among all the phantasmagoric idols that fall away with the slightest breeze.

Winds of change are moving the world today and those changes are towards something better, a Better World . There's no going back. Always Further On is the Rallying Cry. The winds of change belong to Peoples,and we promote them through our Peoples: Our Dignity, Our Right to Freedom. We the Peoples are the ones who are fighting and so we know that the New World is ours, that the Future belongs to us, that Victories, in all the ongoing Struggles, are absolutely Certain.

These are the New Days, these are the New Times, and these are also Good Days and Good Times for Humanity: Struggles and Victories!
