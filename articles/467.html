Greetings to the Army of Nicaragua on its 43rd Anniversary

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 01/09/2022 - 16:15

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Managua, 1st September, 2022

Army General
Julio César Avilés Castillo
Commander in Chief
of the Nicaraguan Army

At 43 Years, in Patriotism and absolute Respect for our Constitution and Institutionality, the Nicaraguan Army is the Strength and Pride of the Nicaraguan Families.

At 43 Years and with the Force of Victories, this Sandinista Popular Army, today the Nicaraguan Army, Patriotic, Revolutionary, Rooted in Ideals and National Values, travels with our Heroes, Forefathers and Martyrs, upon Paths of Motherland, Peace and Freedom, in our Blessed Nicaragua... They are Paths that we continue to travel, Always Free, Dignified, Sovereign and Honorable.

After 43 Years of Fighting, the Victories continue to be Certain, because we created them, because we deserve them and because we defend them, as Inalienable Rights of our Working People, Undefeated and full of Hope.

After 43 Glorious, Brave, Battle-hardened Years, with a Noble Heart and Unwavering Love for the Motherland, for the Families, as The People in Uniform, the Nicaraguan Army is part of the Heroic Heritage of our History and has the esteem, appreciation and respect of the Nicaraguan Families.

We feel proud of that Army that cares for our National Sovereignty, that defends our Territorial Integrity and that is present at every moment of every day, caring for, defending, working together with the People and as a People, for that Nicaragua that we all dream of, that we all want, that we are all creating, of Justice, Peace and Unity for Prosperity.

We embrace the Families of each Superior Officer, Subordinate Officer, Noncommissioned Officer, Class, Soldier and Auxiliary Personnel of our Army, acknowledging the Merit and Value of all, and the full support they receive with that Affection, typical of our Culture of Homes and Communities, where our Families are the Center and Heart of our Lives.

We celebrate ourselves with Confidence, Security and Certainty, that together we are that People of our Nicaragua that advances in New Times, New Days, contributing to the New World, that is already being born, because we believe in the Good of All, in Sisterhood/ Brotherhood, and in the Work of each of us, in each space, for our Nicaragua, that deserves the best.

Congratulations, Sisters and Brothers, Comrades, Soldiers, on this 43rd Anniversary of forging Peace and Just Development, from that inviolable, immutable and immovable National Dignity, which in Sandino's Always and Beyond, manifests itself as Essential Value of our Idiosyncrasy, as Supreme and Foundational Ensign and Emblem of a People and a Country that has always known how to Fight and Win.

With the Conviction of the Future that in Light, Life and Truth we are creating, we say, loud and clear, Nicaraguans by Grace of God; Nicaragua will remain Free, Dignified, Sovereign, Christian and in Fraternal Solidarity, because it has sons and daughters that love her and that are inspired and enlightened, defending the Future.

Long live the Nicaraguan Army!
Long live the 43 Years of Glory and Victories!
Motherland and Freedom!
