Nicaragua responds to the COVID-19 Pandemic

Submitted bytortilla onVie, 03/04/2020 - 10:00

NSCAG News, April 2nd 2020
http://www.nscag.org/news/article/300/nicaragua-responds-to-the-covid-1…

As at the end of March, there were three confirmed cases of coronavirus in Nicaragua. One person out of the three has died due to complications from COVID-19 and another 14 people were under observation but had so far tested negative for the virus.*

Until now, Nicaragua has been successful in preventing the virus from spreading, largely due to a public health system which offers free, universal health sevices based on community-focussed preventative care.

For more about the steps being taken in Nicaragua to confront the pandemic and protect the population, see here

* Latest update as of 3 April 2020 indicates that there have now been 5 cases, of which one person has died, 1 has recovered and 3 are under hospital care. All of the cases have been among people from outside the country and there are no reports of community-based cases.
