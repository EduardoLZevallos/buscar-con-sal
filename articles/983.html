FSLN sends greetings to the delegates of the Foro Sao Paulo

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 13/12/2022 - 18:47

First of all, on behalf of our Secretary General, Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra, President of Nicaragua, and Comrade Rosario Murillo, Vice President, we would like to extend a fraternal greeting of solidarity to all the Delegations that are attending this Meeting of Political Parties of our Sao Paulo Forum.

Sisters and Brothers: We live in times of immense trials and challenges, which we are going through with the Force of Hope and a Heart, always willing to fight, to continue fighting and to continue winning.

With our indomitable Libertarian Character, we advance in our great, steadfast CaribbeanAmerica of Principles and Convictions that strengthen our Paths of Liberation.

Faced with the attacks that have been sponsored in each of Our Nations, because we are all a Nation, with the aim of erasing the resolute Courage and the certainties of Triumphs of our Peoples; we unite, in solidarity and in growing Brotherhood, sharing the Principles and Values that distinguish us, Sovereignty, Self-determination and non-interference in the affairs of each People and Country.

We salute in a very special way the Workers' Party, Lula, its Historic Leader and President Elect of Brazil; Cristina, the indisputable Leader of that Undefeated Peronism that her People support... Peru, its Social Movements, its People that reclaim the Right to maintain its Democratic Proposal.

We greet each other by recognizing that each People and Country chooses its own Cultural, Social, Political and Economic Models, based on that inviolable Right to Independence, for which we have fought so hard.

Our unwavering Solidarity and Union with Cuba, Venezuela, Bolivia, the Caribbean, and the Fighting Peoples of Chile, Ecuador, Colombia... With the Indigenous and Afro-descendant Peoples of our entire Continent, always in tune with the Ideals and Values that characterize us.

To the People of Colombia, our Embrace in these Times of defense of the Right to Live in Dialogue, Consensus and Peace.

To our Central American Region, always the commitment to assume Union and Integration to continue the primordial Battle against Poverty.

In Nicaragua, as is well known, we held successful Municipal Elections, which culminated in the Triumph of the People's President, and of the People's Mayor, in all the Mayor's Offices.

All these Battles and all these Glories are possible because the Peoples of Our CaribbeanAmerica work hard for the Unity that allows us to consolidate our Victories every day, among all the aggressions and actions harmful to our own Processes.

To the Families of our entire Great Homeland, to the Youth, to the Women, to the Indigenous and Afro-descendant Peoples, to Everyone, and with Everyone, Let's Move Forward, Everyone Together, building the Future.

Managua, 13th December, 2022
Sandinista National Liberation Front
