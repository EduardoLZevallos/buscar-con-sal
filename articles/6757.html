DANIEL: "Strengthening the Nicaraguan Army, strengthening Sandino's Army, strengthening the Army born of the Revolution. That's what this is about"

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 03/06/2025 - 15:58

Official Event Promoting Senior Commanders of the Nicaraguan Army
to the Military Rank of Colonel General
 
June 2nd 2025

Compañera Rosario, Co-President of Nicaragua,
reads Presidential Agreement No. 66-2025,
Promotion in Military Grades of Colonel General

A very good evening. Presidential Agreement No. 66-2005 states the following:

"The Presidency of the Republic of Nicaragua, Co-President Daniel Ortega and Co-President Rosario Murillo, Taking into Consideration :

    1. That the Political Constitution establishes the Presidency of the Republic as the Supreme Head of the Nicaraguan Army and, in accordance with Article 125, paragraph 9, that it is the responsibility of the Presidency of the Republic to approve the granting of the military rank of Colonel General at the proposal of the Military Council.

    2. That the Military Council of the Nicaraguan Army, proposed to the Presidency of the Republic and Supreme Head of the Nicaraguan Army, granting the Military Rank of Colonel General to Major Generals Bayardo Ramón Rodríguez Ruiz, Chief of the General Staff, and Marvin Elías Corrales Rodríguez, Inspector General.

Using the powers conferred by the Political Constitution and Law No. 181, "Code of Organization, Jurisdiction and Military Social Security".

Therefore, using the powers conferred on them by the Political Constitution, they agree :
Article 1. To promote to the Military Rank of Colonel General the Major Generals: Bayardo Ramón Rodríguez Ruíz, Chief of the General Staff, and Marvin Elías Corrales Rodríguez, Inspector General.

Article 2. This Agreement takes effect as of its publication in La Gaceta, Official Journal.

Given in Managua, Presidential Office, Republic of Nicaragua, on May 7, 2025.

Daniel Ortega Saavedra, Rosario Murillo”.

Many congratulations, Compañeros, many congratulations, and to your Families also.

Address by Daniel

Beloved Brothers, Compañeros of the Nicaraguan Army; Beloved Compañera Co-President Rosario Murillo Zambrana; Beloved Brother, Commander-in-Chief of the Military Forces of the Nicaraguan Army, Army General Julio César Avilés Castillo; Beloved Brother President of the National Assembly, Doctor Gustavo Porras Cortez;

Beloved Brother, Chief of the General Staff of the Army of Nicaragua, as of today, Colonel General Bayardo Ramón Rodríguez Ruiz. Congratulations, Beloved Brother, to You and your Family;

Beloved Brother Inspector General of the Nicaraguan Army, Colonel Marvin Elías Corrales Rodríguez, as of today Colonel General. Our greetings, our congratulations, our love, to you and your family.

Beloved Minister of Defense of the Republic of Nicaragua, Compañera Rosa Adelina Barahona Castro;

Beloved Brother, Chief of the Police Forces of the Republic of Nicaragua, First Commissioner General Francisco Javier Díaz Madriz;

Beloved Sister, Compañera Maria Amelia Colonel Kinloch, Minister of the Interior;

Brother Generals and Senior Officers, Members of the Military Council of the Army of Nicaragua who signed this Proposal in what is an advance in terms of the development required for our Institutions, to incorporate the Rank of Colonel General;

Beloved Wives of the Members of the High Command of the Nicaraguan Army; Children and Relatives, and Special Guests of the Chief of the General Staff and the Inspector General of the Nicaraguan Army;

As always, our greetings to the Brother Journalists who cover the most diverse activities, so as to inform the Nicaraguan people.

This is a day in which Nicaragua takes another step to strengthen the institutions of the Nicaraguan Army. Because we have to continue strengthening all our Institutions, from  Institutions, like the Army and the Police, that are for protecting the People, protecting and guaranteeing Security for Peace, but also advancing with all our Institutions in every field, both in the Fight against Poverty and in the activities of the Health System taking care of the Lives of Nicaraguans.

Likewise, the Hospitals, the Military Hospital, the Carlos Roberto Huembes Hospital, Hospitals that are at the service of Soldiers, Officers and their Families, but also with their doors open for the People.

We are also continuing to build more Hospitals, just as we can see how new buses are arriving, in the same way we are seeing new Hospitals multiplying even in the most remote areas, in the Caribbean area, in the area of El Rama.

Hospitals are multiplying all over the country, and this is accompanied by direct care to the population through Mobile Clinics, which go out to Neighborhoods, which go out to Communities, going there to provide care for Families, care for Children, care for those who are presenting different ailments. And they go with modern equipment.

That is to say, this is an attention that is fundamental and which we have prioritized, Health, because the first duty that we have as Revolutionaries, as Sandinistas, as Nicaraguans, is to take care of the lives of Nicaraguans, to watch over the lives of Children, to watch over the lives of Young People, to watch over the lives of Adults.

And we have to develop a Plan that may cause some discomfort to those who like to speed on the highways, but we have to make a Plan where you cannot speed on the roads; put a limit. Set a limit, which cannot be 90 kilometers, nor 100 kilometers; but setting a limit that the Compañeras and Compañeras will have to out well and make their proposal to us.

If the limit is 50 kilometers per hour or it is 60 kilometers per hour, they are going to argue that this is going to cause losses because it is slow. Ah, but what is worth more, a truck with merchandise or the life of a Citizen? We have to put life first.

We see accidents every day, every day, accidents in the world, terrible accidents, caused by acts of terrorism, like the train that was blown up there in Russia.
That I would say is a priority, for the Life, Health, of Nicaraguan Families, for the Life and Family of the Drivers themselves. Because also, at high speeds Drivers perish and run over pedestrians, or those who ride motorcycles, or those who ride bicycles, or those riding horses. All these things happen. Such that we have to make a Plan for the Life and Security of Nicaraguans, in terms of public and private transport.

In the same way, to continue developing the other Programs we have been developing throughout all these years, in every field, in the field of Education, in the field of Academic Training; that is, from the First Degree to the Postgraduate Degree, and the Courses that have been developed, and where we have also now managed to make progress via exchanges and cooperation with countries that have great scientific development, that have great development and that are now here.

They come to transfer knowledge in the Hospitals of the Health System, in the Military Hospital, in the Carlos Roberto Huembes Hospital; we see there the Seminars so that Doctors have greater mastery in order to face the challenges of preserving and saving Nicaraguan lives.

These are the great challenges, among others facing Nicaraguans which we have already noted, in that we are for Peace, for Peace in Nicaragua, where we have an enormous responsibility to preserve Peace and for Peace in the World.

They murdered children, old people, women, simply because they were of Jewish origin, and because they did not fit in the mind of Nazism, which did not recognize any race if it was not an Aryan, German race. It is a world, I would say, this is the most critical period that humanity is living after those other wars, the First World War, the Second World War, after the Napoleonic invasion, which was defeated in Russia; after the terrible wars that were fought in Vietnam where US power was defeated.

But, it’s as if they don't want to look any further in favor of the security of their own citizens, of their own families. They don't want to look further. Simply, well, it has its value of course, we are not going to underestimate it, they reached an agreement to deliver Ukrainian prisoners who are in Russia, and to deliver Russian prisoners who are in Ukraine, we are talking about thousands; and also to deliver the corpses of the thousands who have died in these battles.

Well, it's a step, but in the meantime, the missiles keep flying, the war goes on. Beloved Brothers and Sisters, Beloved Nicaraguan Families, Beloved Brothers and Sisters of the Army, of the Police, this day is really a day of great significance, because it is not merely inventing a new rank just for the sake of doing so, it is fundamentally about strengthening the Nicaraguan Army, strengthening Sandino's Army, strengthening the Army born of the Revolution. That's what this is about. And to what end? To guarantee Peace and Stability in the Country, and to guarantee continuing to strengthen our relations with the other Central American Armies, as they have already managed to do so well.
