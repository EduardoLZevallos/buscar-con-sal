ROSARIO: "United, cohesive, we are strong, with the courage to preserve Unity, since it requires, demands, a lot of courage"

Enviado por tortilla el Dom, 09/11/2025 - 10:51

Remarks by Rosario, Copresident of Nicaragua
after the Commemoration of the 46th Anniversary of the Ministry of the Interior
and the 49th Anniversary of the Transit to Immortality of Carlos Fonseca Amador

8 November 2025


Good evening, Compañeros and Compañeras. Here we are living in honor of Carlos, with Carlos... Living this Revolution which belongs to us and which has left us the assurance, the guarantee, that our Nicaraguan people will live the best Times possible... Times of well-being, Times representing the Bright Future, which is what belongs to and characterizes Revolutions.

Today, not only have we lived all we owe Carlos, but we have pledged, once again, to ensure his example, the example of permanent struggle for Unity, without which, as our Comandante Daniel explained, without Unity, and without Peace, there is nothing.

United, cohesive, we are strong, with the courage to preserve Unity, since it requires, demands, a lot of courage, to be able to see beyond selfishness, beyond our human faults, to think of and to live for the great work of Revolution.

How many years now? It will be 50 next year, but Carlos lives on and the struggles continue, as we build our future !

A warm embrace to all our families in every home, in every community where we all are, there too is Carlos and this Revolution that signifies Hope, Affection, Faith, and all that is our Future.

Thank you, Compañeras and Compañeros.
