Honoring Sandino, General of Dignified and Free Men and Women

Enviado por tortilla el Lun, 13/05/2024 - 18:18

HONORING OUR COURAGEOUS FATHER
SANDINO, GENERAL OF DIGNIFIED AND FREE MEN AND WOMEN

REASON NEEDS HEART

Let's all go onward with Sandino, Emblem, Symbol and Banner of our Sovereignty, Decorum and National Dignity. We celebrate his arrival in this World as the Courageous Visionary he came to be, who left us the obligation to live in our Nation, Patriotic and Free, to live honorably, building the Future.

With our Sandino, Eternal Guide along our Revolutionary and Evolutionary Way, we head towards the Sun of Freedom every day, and towards the Full, True, Just and Enlightened Life, irreplaceable Legacy and Triumphant Teaching of his daily Heroism, and that of Andrés, of Zeledón, of Carlos, of Tomás, of Daniel, and of our Families, of Women and Men of all Ages.

We are proud to be born, to grow up and to live the Fullness of an increasingly Dignified, Bright, Luminous Nicaragua, and its Noble Stories that inspire every generation of Nicaraguan Patriots, and Human Beings determined to continue waging all the Sublime Battles for Life, with Freedoms and Rights, and above all the Honor of knowing ourselves to be Permanent Combatants for Justice and Love.

With Darío, with Zeledón, with Sandino, we continue to present to the world the clean, radiant Face of a Nation that Generates Respect for every Good, Courageous Soul, for every growing Spirit, for every raised Hope, and for that permanent Strength of Spirit that makes us Triumphant Victors, in the cause of Humanism, and of that Christian and Solidarity based Socialism, which we proclaim and practice.

Our Faith in the Certainty of a Better World encourages us and strengthens all our daily efforts and victories, ignoring the cowardice, hesitations, servility, capitulations and betrayals that fill the Dustbin of History, while the Peoples of the World rise up and shout Onward! with a Giant's Voice...!

The People United Will Never Be Defeated... The People with their Weapons of Intelligence, Serenity, Wisdom, and Experience of all times; the People United in their Glory, their History and their Victories, Will Never Be Defeated!

The traitors and cowards, the sellouts, those who offer themselves to serve the Yankee, the Imperialists of the Earth, those who continue to believe that surrender will bring them dividends, are nothing but shysters, sinister villains, opportunists, promoters of false theatrical fears, of over-acted poses; they are lackeys, fake democrats, phonies, cynical, insolent, depraved and shameless parents of the terrorist coup, shamelessly reflecting themselves as the center of their own egomaniac mirror...

They are the unhappy usurpers of dreams; owners, yesterday and tomorrow, of the momentous failures, of great defeats, and of the absolute loss of honor and credibility, nor do they know Sandino in his Vigor, his Greatness, his Brilliance, his “We do not fear them”, nor do they know the Love Capable of Everything and which Overcomes Everything.

Light, Life and Truth, says Sandino... Always Further On, says Sandino. References to out of date tales, the faint-hearted, the false Prophets with cosy versions of ignoble Futures; the Renegades who aim always at betraying their own History, spitting on the Blood of our Heroes and Martyrs; those who sell their souls to the devil; the mediocre narrators of absurd fables and delusions of greatness, for sale to the worst of bidders... They will not remain, they will not stand, they will not sleep the sleep of the Righteous. On the contrary. They will be remembered for infamy, ignominy, ingratitude, ignorance, and the nefarious breath of infidelity and disloyalty to the flesh of their own.

Sandino Lives, the Struggle Continues, and as he, our Visionary and Prophetic Father, said in the immortal Manifesto of San Albino, “The last of my men, who are men of Freedom and Dignity, may die, but before that happens, blond invader, more than one of your battalions, will have bitten the dust of my rugged mountains...”

In the Sacred, Sacrosanct Flames of the Worthy Sandinista Struggles; in Life, Health and Fortitude; with all our Undefeated Strengths, we proclaim the absolute validity and value of our Battles, the fierce Combats of Love, Justice, Hope, Joy, Much-deserved Well-being, and Peace.

In all the moments and spaces of our Lives, the inspired and inspiring Daughters and Sons of Sandino know, that...

The People United
Will Never Be Defeated!

And too...

There Is No Reason
Without Heart!

And also...

like Sandino...

We Don't Sell Out
Nor Do We Surrender
Ever...!

We live in a Free Nation

We are a Patriotic Nation of Dignity and Freedom

Nicaraguans, by the Grace of God

Revolutionaries and Evolutionaries

like Sandino!

Managua, May 13, 2024

Rosario Murillo
 

ACTIVITIES

1. We celebrate our General with Keynote Lectures, Impromptu Seminars, Events to Impart Knowledge and Experience Events, in all the Educational Spaces, with broadcasts via our Communication Media, all over the Country.

2. We celebrate Sandino by activating all forms of Art, Culture and Tradition.

3. We celebrate Sandino from the Units of Production, Cooperatives and everywhere that Our Freedom Loving Father dreamed of so much, to see people Living Well with All Rights in our Beloved Nation.

4. We celebrate Sandino with his Blanquita, Linda Stela from San Rafael, uniting Lives, Passions, Dreams, and Certainties, surrendering to the Struggle for Our America, Free, United, Just and Redeemed by its Peoples.

5. We celebrate Sandino in all our Diplomatic Missions, transmitting the Events we hold in Nicaragua.

6. We celebrate Sandino in all his Dimensions, with Parades, Rhythmic Bands, Floral Offerings, with the Love and Presence of Women and Men of all Ages, in a heartfelt and profound National Tribute to the Extraordinary Human Being who gave us the Honorable Duty, the Extraordinary Privilege of Learning How to Fight and to Overcome.

7. We celebrate Sandino in his Deep Spiritual Dimension, that Invincible, Indomitable Nicaraguan Spirit of Diriangén, Nicarao, Adiact; Andrés, Estrada; Darío, Zeledón, the Army in Defense of National Sovereignty representing everyone, for the Future of everyone.

8. All the Institutions, all the Municipalities, the whole State and the Government, all our Units of Victory, all the Social and Labor Movements, all our Youth, Heir of the Values and Ideals contained in the Battles of yesteryear, of today and also all those to come, everyone absolutely, we celebrate, sing, and receive with Reverence, that Sandino who is born every day in our Hearts, and in our irrevocable, invincible, undefeated Conviction in all our Battles and Victories.

9. The Media will conduct Interviews, Reports which will reflect the Richness of our Patriotic, Darian, Sandinista, Proud, Revolutionary and Evolutionary Culture.

10. Everyone with Daniel,

with Reason and with Heart

WE ARE SANDINO

May of our Patriotic, Free Nation

May of our Nation and Freedom!

May 13th 2024

Rosario Murillo
