Serene and Strong

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 28/02/2025 - 18:06

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

SERENE AND STRONG

Virtue lies in being serene and strong;
for everything burns in that inner fire;
so one triumphs over rancor and death,
and to Bethlehem... The caravan goes, passing by!

Rubén Darío
Songs of Life and Hope, 1904

On the Day of the Journalist, Nicaragua pays tribute to all the Communicators who have fallen defending the Enlightened Ideal of True Truths that lights our Revolutionary and Evolutionary Skies.

We celebrate the Life of our Brothers and Sisters who, with their Thought, Action and Dedication to the Nation which sees us born and grow, a Nation of Everyone, constitute a clear and fruitful Example of the Love of Loves, the Love of Nicaragua, Blessed, Prosperous, Just, Dignified and Always Triumphant.

We commit ourselves to the steps of all our National Heroes and all Nicaraguans of Good Will, who have opened up Paths so that with the Strength of the Great Spirit of our Original Heroes, of the Legendary Anti-interventionist Warriors, of the Heroic Guerrillas, and from the Immortal Genius of Darío and the Burning Vigor of Sandino, General of Free Men and Women, we are proud to shine out in these other Times of Struggles and Certainties, of Integrity and Courage, of Honor and Nobility, of the Supreme Commitment and Duty to Be Free, and to make History with the Humility and Greatness of every Human Being of this Nicaragua of Ours.

We commit ourselves to working daily with the best of each one of us, so that the Infinite Light and the Great Truths, the True Life, of Darío and Sandino, shine like never before in the Blue-and-White Sky of this Nation of unlimited Consciousness and Sacred Flames.

With the Love which makes Everything Possible and Conquers Everything, we Continue, Always Further On, building the Future..

Managua, Nicaragua, March 1st 2025

Rosario Murillo
Co-President and
Coordinadator for Communications
and Citizen Affairs
