on the occasion of the 43rd Anniversary of the Triumph of the Sandinista Popular Revolution

July 19, 2022

Introductory address by Rosario

Here They Could Not Prevail, Nor Will they... Here They Could Not Prevail, Nor WIll They...!

Here is the People, and a warm embrace, first of all, to all the Families, in all corners of our Blessed and Free Nation, we are united, we embrace each other, we are full of Faith, and full of Certainty, because the Struggles are always there, the Struggles continue, but our Victories are Certain. That's why we say : Honor, Glory and Victory!

Long live that Sacred Blood sustaining us and redeeming us!

May  the vision Always Beyond of our Sandino, of our General, who marks the Path, the Destiny and the Victorious Struggles of this People, of our People!

Long live the Eternal and Courageous Nicaraguan Youth!

May the Young People of all our Nicaragua live long, who today are committed, as always, to the Ideology of the Revolution... a Free Nation, a Nation with Dignity, a Christian Nation, a Nation of Solidarity, which is the Ideology of our Revolution.

And here they are now. And they live on, because it is they who guide us by their example, their strength, and that is why we are a Force for Victories, a Force for Revolution, a Force for a Consciousness that renews itself and grows every day, a Force of Courage, an Anti-Imperialist Force, because we have suffered from them here, but we have also expelled them and defeated in every case.

Strength and Fortitude of Victories, Strength and Fortitude of a Revolution of Love, of Concord, which promotes the Rights of All,  above all the Right to Live in Peace, the Right to build from Peace, the Right to live with Affection, and to reject and repudiate hatred, forever.

Address by Ralph Gonsalves

Comrade President, my Beloved Friend, Daniel Ortega; your Beloved Lady Wife, Vice-President of Nicaragua, Compañera Rosario Murillo; Leaders of the different Institutions of the Government of the Republic of Nicaragua; Excellencies and Friends, Distinguished guests from around the World attending this historic event of the 43rd Anniversary of the Sandinista Revolution; Beloved People of Nicaragua and all the Warriors of the Nicaraguan People, good afternoon.

I accept with great humility this Distinction and on behalf of all the Brothers and Sisters of Latin America and the Caribbean with whom I have been working for more than 50 years.

The struggle has been one, for Independence, for National Sovereignty, for the Freedom and Dignity of our Peoples, to be able to live our own Life according to our own rules and our own experience; for Peace, Justice and Prosperity for all.

I come from a small Country in our Hemisphere, but this small Country believes and subscribes to large Principles: The Defense of Sovereignty and Independence, non-interference and non-Intervention in our own affairs; so as to able to lead ourselves and our Civilizations onward, and to be able to walk together with all the Peoples around the World, in friendship but not in subordination. In that sense, we are friends with everyone and aspire for a Better World.

Based on my own experience and my understanding of the World and in the light of all those Principles that we defend, I can unequivocally say that no weapon pointed against Nicaragua will prosper.

On July 19, 1979 I had not yet met Daniel Ortega, but I knew him even before I met him, but from my understanding, my knowledge of him and of the People of Nicaragua, when the Revolution succeeded, I celebrated in St. Vincent and the Grenadines, because the Nicaraguan Revolution is our Revolution.

And I will tell you this, yesterday I was with the Garifuna People in Orinoco, I was with the Garifuna People and other Afro-Caribbean Peoples in Bluefields, and found another reason for me, for the People of San Vicente and the Grenadines and for the Greater Caribbean, to give our unequivocal support to the Sandinista Front and the Revolution.

An additional reason is because the Garifuna People, who were born in San Vicente and the Grenadines, and the Caribbean Peoples told me wherever I went, the Caribbean People said that they are in support of the Sandinista Revolution and they live it and know it daily, and if they tell me that it is so, I believe it, and I behave that way and act that way.

And I want to thank the Sandinista Revolution for helping so much in integrating the Garifuna People and the Afro-Caribbean Peoples, completely integrated into the development of Nicaragua and at the same time also allowing them to enjoy Autonomy and to be able to advance in this Multi-Ethnic and Multicultural Nation, and develop with Justice, Peace and Prosperity.

Those who want to lie about the Sandinista Revolution must first learn from that example, because if they learn from it, instead of trying to lie about it, they would work to elevate it.

Nicaragua and Saint Vincent and the Grenadines work very closely at the United Nations; we are Founding Members of the Bolivarian Alternative for Our America; we are members of the Community of Latin American and Caribbean States; we work very closely in the Association of Caribbean States.

And now the Empire does not understand that, it has a country of 350 million people, it has the largest economy in the World, it is said that they have the largest army in the World; Nicaragua has about 6.2 million people, a Central American country that only aspires to develop its country and its People.

Why in the name of God, such a big country, with so many resources, with so much military force, why attack a country like Nicaragua? I ask myself that question daily, and it is difficult for everyday understanding to understand something like that. But even that which one cannot understand, we know what they are doing and we know that it is wrong.

I call on the United States of America, a country of great achievements, to come closer in friendship with the People and Government of Nicaragua. Is that so hard? Instead, what have we been seeing? The Summit of the Americas, at the beginning of this month, in Los Angeles, the United States of America unilaterally and wrongly arrogated to itself the right to exclude Cuba, Venezuela and Nicaragua from attending that Summit. Saint Vincent and the Grenadines for reasons of principle refused to attend.

There are some countries, no, the whole of Latin America and the Caribbean came to the conclusion that the United States was wrong, there was unity on this issue, but some Countries decided simply not to attend at the level of Head of State or Government. I decided not to attend at all.

My American friends begged me to at least send someone else, but if you invite me to me to your house and for reasons of Principle can not attend, it would be a bad attitude for me to send someone else, and in my civilization good manners have weight.

If you invited me to come and then sent me the invitation in writing, if you wanted me to invite someone else, you also had to write to me and say, "if you can't attend for reasons of Principle, I wouldn't consider it bad behavior if you sent someone else". I did not receive that second letter, and Saint Vincent and the Grenadines did not attend for reasons of Principle, and I did not send anyone, Comrade Daniel, out of good manners.

For those who wanted an explanation, they received it here in Managua. I reiterate my gratitude for this great honor. For this great honor I commit myself to live the rest of my life trying to meet your expectations, but it is not only for me, it is for our Peoples, the Peoples of our Region, to continue advancing and fighting for the great Ideals and Principles of Sandino.

As I said yesterday at Bluefields, what is required of us as Leaders? Thousands of years ago the Prophet Micah gave a simple explanation of what is required of a Leader: To act justly, to love Compassion, and to walk humbly with our God.

Happy Anniversary, Nicaragua!

Young People, remember that the condition of Youth is Heaven, with your Heart full of Love, that is a fixed mark that doesn't care about the difficulties, and always know that with Solidarity among the People and all around Latin America and the Caribbean and around the World, there is no power, there is no weapon that can be pointed against the People of Nicaragua and be able to prosper.

Thank you very much. Good night.

Daniel's Address

Beloved Nicaraguan Brothers, Beloved Families, all Children of this Blessed Land. It's 5 minutes to 9 at night and the truth is that we haven't felt the time go by. With the Energy of this Youth, with the Strength of this Youth, with the Singing of this Youth, with the Heart of this Youth, with the radiance of this Youth, the time has passed and we have not bothered to be watching the time, while it may already be late, I expected a bit more music right now, a little more singing, and that is the Strength of Nicaragua, that is Nicaragua's Strength. You represent the Strength, the vitality of this Country, of this Nation.

For in you, Beloved Young People, runs the blood of our Heroes, the blood of Diriangén, the blood of Nicarao, the blood of the Caciques who faced and died confronting the Spanish invaders. That blood is alive, they put them to death, but they did not kill their Spirit, they did not kill their Soul, their Soul and Spirit is here, in you and in all of us.

Our Brother, Ralph, asked why is the United States behaving that way, and when we say the United States we are talking about the United States leadership, because when they launched the atomic bomb on Hiroshima, the United States people was not consulted. When the  United States People if they got the nuclear bomb, that was a subject of discussion and analysis, among a few scientists with the US Authorities and the President, and rather the concern that they had was that if the bomb was going to explode or not going to explode, that's what they were worried about and they realized how many thousands the bomb could kill, and the higher the number they calculated that the bomb could kill the happier they were and the more animated they became.

And so they went with the bomb, transporting it, and they called it "La Niña“ or was it ”El Niño“, imagine the name they put on it, ”La Niña“ or ”El Niño“; that is how far the perversity of Empires and Yankee Imperialism goes, naming a weapon as deadly, as brutal as that, ”La Niña“ or ”El Niño".

And they went and launched it, and killed hundreds of thousands of citizens, Children, Adults, with a single blow, because they launched it on a city where there were no Japanese troops, there were no troops, it was not a military objective.

Right there they killed, they murdered many more citizens than all those who may have died right now in this war that the Empires have launched to try to destroy the struggle Humanity is waging to achieve the end of hegemony, and install Multipolarity on our Planet. That is the battle that is being fought there in Ukraine, where Europe and the United States do not want to see China growing economically.

Already from there, one can see the evil that some Human Beings have and that unfortunately is the evil the great powers have carried throughout history, colonizing Africa, Asia, Latin America, the great powers brought slaves from Africa to these Regions to sell them as if they were animals ,there in the slave markets. There is no God in the Soul of those people, there is no feeling in the Soul of those people; and that behavior is not just recent.

When you asked and wondered why they do not seek understanding, it is that they are not prepared to seek understanding, they are prepared only to impose, occupy, bomb and murder, as they have done throughout history.

Let me summarize what the history of Nicaragua is, if you don't mind. Here the Spanish came, and well, there was indigenous resistance, many Caciques fell facing the Spaniards who came, like true hypocrites, with the Cross and with the Sword. And what did they say to our ancestors? Either submit or we'll put you to the sword. Yes, they had to renounce their gods, because they had their gods; they were told, Either you renounce your gods and get baptized, or we put you to the sword.

And in that way they exterminated millions of Human Beings throughout America, from Canada all the way to there in the area of Chile; they exterminated millions, in the name of God, sanctified by the High Authorities of the Church. There was no Pope there protesting against these barbarities, against these crimes, and of course there was the complicity of all the Powers who were quarreling among themselves and waging wars were among themselves so as to take over the World.

It was a war between the European Powers when there was still no European Union, you know very well, beloved Brother, England, France, Spain, wanting to dominate all of Europe and after coming to dominate all of Europe then going on to take over Africa, Asia, all of America. A hegemonic mentality, an egoistic mentality, a mentality that has nothing Christian about it, and all this they did with the blessing of the different Churches that existed at that time.
