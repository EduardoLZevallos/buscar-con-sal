Nicaragua - Anniversary of a Revolution

This webinar had been postpone and is now rescheduled for Tuesday, August 16 as indicated below

Join the UNAC webinar

Tuesday, August 16, 5 PM Eastern US and Canada

Click here or the image below to register

Speakers:

     Margaret Kimberley -

          Executive Editor and Senior Columnist for Black Agenda Report.

          Member of Black Alliance for Peace Coordinating Committee and

          Administrative Committee of UNAC


       Dan Kovalik -

          Human rights lawyer and peace activist. He teaches Human Rights at

          the University of Pittsburgh School of Law

On July 19, Nicaragua celebrated the anniversary of its revolution that overthrew the US supported dictator Anastasio Somoza. A delegation of US solidarity organizations were in Nicaragua to commemorate the anniversary. Hear about the events, the progress and struggles of the Nicaraguans and their role today in Latin American and international politics.
