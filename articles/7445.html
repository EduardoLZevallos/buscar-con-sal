ROSARIO: "Day by day, the Certainty of the Security is being built that we all deserve, the Security we will never stop defending, the Security that is Peace"

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 04/09/2025 - 10:26

Remarks by Rosario, Co-President of Nicaragua
after the Military Parade of the Nicaraguan Army

September 3rd 2025

I want to emphasize the intense, beautiful Words of our Comandante Daniel, the Chief of our Army, when he says: Here with us are the People and here are our Heroes. Here are all those Women and Men who have given their lives for the Fatherland. Here are their families. Here we are all full of the Pride of being ALL OF US SAN JACINTO and of being All of Us Nicaraguans, by the Grace of God!

How the country we dream of is being built day by day! How, day by day, the Certainty of the Security is being built that we all deserve, the Security we will never stop defending, the Security that is Peace, Peace that is development and progress against Poverty.

On a day like today, in this Victorious September, we called it Victorious September before the Triumph of the Revolution, and we say Victorious September today because we are building More New Victories, making advances in all the Programs our People deserve: Housing, Highways, Streets... Hospitals!

What a beautiful hospital, there in Ocotal, the Heroes of Las Segovias...! What a beautiful hospital we are also going to inaugurate on the 21st, there in León, the Oscar Danilo Rosales!

So many hospitals, in Chinandega, all over the country...! So many health centers! So many Health Fairs, so many Campaigns to guarantee the Health of our People, Women, Men and Children!

We can't help but feel excited and proud, because we know we are doing what we should be doing. 

In this we are paying Tribute, Homage, Honor and Glory to all the Nicaraguans who have wanted, who have dreamed, who have fought and struggled and even given their lives for this Nicaragua that we have today, with Health, with Education, with Universities, with Roads, Streets, Highways, with Ports, with so much also Victorious Cooperation with those Countries of the World committed alongside us to defend Peace with Social Justice.

Social justice for Everyone is what we all deserve, is what we want and is what we defend, working every day, to serve our People better and better.

We know that We are Going Onward... Another world is not just possible. Another world is a reality now. Another world is forming. Another world is dawning which is the Golden Dawn of the Peoples!

Warm embraces, Compañeras and Compañeros.
