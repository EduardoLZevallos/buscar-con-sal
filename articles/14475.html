MESSAGE FROM THE SANDINISTA NATIONAL LIBERATION FRONT IN GREETINGS
TO THE CENTENARY OF THE COMMUNIST PARTY OF URUGUAY

FRIDAY 27TH MAY, 2022

Dear Comrade and Brother, Juan Castillo, Secretary General of the Communist Party of Uruguay; Sisters and Brothers of the PCU Directorate, Sisters and Brothers Members, who gather today to commemorate one hundred years of brave struggles against capitalist exploitation, imperialism, discrimination, social injustice and inequality; artifices created and promoted by those who have always sought to dominate and subjugate our Peoples that clamor for Peace, Happiness, Freedom, Justice, Development and Good Living.

Receive the Fraternal Embrace of the Directorate and Membership of the Sandinista National Liberation Front, from Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra and Comrade Rosario Murillo Zambrana, with the recognition of the Heroic People of Sandino, who embrace and salute the Glorious Centenary of the Communist Party of Uruguay.

Upon reaching one hundred years of struggle in favor of the Uruguayan People, the People of Nicaragua and the FSLN, are proud to recognize and celebrate the memory and historical legacy of all the Heroes, Heroines and Martyrs of the PCU, who made the maximum sacrifice for their People and their Country. Honor and Glory to each of them!

In this Centenary we face the new aggressions of Imperialism, which tries to impose its hegemony, applying all forms of aggression, attempting to deny the reality of the sovereign will of our Peoples who have been establishing a New Multilateral World Order.

The Sandinista Popular Revolution wages this new stage of struggles, strengthening Popular and Participatory Democracy, Latin American and Caribbean Unity, the defense of the Cuban Revolution, of the Bolivarian Revolution, and strengthening our relations with all Peoples in defense of Peace, National Sovereignty, Self-determination and Independence; Human Development, with a focus on eradicating poverty and ensuring economic growth with Social Justice and in harmony with Nature.

Dear Brothers and Sisters of the Communist Party of Uruguay, in this Centennial Commemoration, we Sandinistas feel united in your struggles, defending our Principles and convinced that We Never Sell Out nor Give Up, and that we will achieve New Victories.

Always and Beyond in the Struggle for Humanity!

Sandinista National Liberation Front
