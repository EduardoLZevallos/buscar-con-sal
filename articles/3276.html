ROSARIO: "Evil does not overcome Good. Love can do anything, as can the Love of God infinitely, powerfully, potently!"

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 04/01/2024 - 19:22

Comments by Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua,
in the Midday Edition of Multinoticias Channel 4

January 4th 2024
 

Good afternoon, Compañera. Here we are, greeting each other. Our Fraternal Embrace to all the Families of our Blessed Nicaragua, in Peace, in Peace, in Peace...! Our Nicaragua gives thanks to God for Concord and Common Good, that Concord and that Common Good on which we work on every day, with Love, Hope and Trust in our Heavenly Father.

 

I have received a Poem from the Writer, Brother Edwin Moncada, which I want to read because it is important to know how we share Thoughts, Reflections, Truths, Real Truths. It begins with a Psalm of David, which says: “For thou art not a God that hath pleasure in wickedness: neither shall evil dwell with thee”; it is from a Psalm of David.


"Walt Whitman made silence burn:
'Don't let anyone take away your right to express yourself, which is almost a duty.'

And in life, faith or art, the norm
is that individuals must respond
for their actions before the law. So be it in Rome
or Spain (yes, in the Spain of loving
There are 33 priests, not of the beautiful, who appear
in prison, and not by praying the Angelus at dawn),
Either in the US or in Chile, and no one is “astonished”.

Ah, but if it's in Nicaragua...

Garments are torn apart.
It always happens like this. Notorious or anonymous.
With dogmas or pure phylacteries.
Since the last
Sanhedrin
even the most incensed cluster
scoundrel.

The first to rip off is the lie
facing the Truth.
The lie that has in the center of its sight
anger and evil for
everything inspired by the celestial Light.

False, there is no religious persecution.

If a priest is arrested, is for a reason.
That is not religious persecution.


For centuries and for open coffers,
they have believed themselves are above the laws,
and some hierarchs
even above the King of Kings.

If they capture
a hammock weaver because of something,
due to lack of sloop
and luxurious life
they will not bark
they will not say:
“There is persecution of precious craftsmanship.”

Weavers don't believe they are above
anyone, not even below: they are workers,
they do not ask for nor live on gifts.

That's why they weave
hammocks, they do not attack
the country we love.

If they put a doctor of the Republic
in a prison for some reason:
they do not say either:
“There is persecution of medical sciences.”

A good doctor or a decent doctor,
does not believe himself more than the patient,
nor of the governing Constitution.
They heal, and they really heal people.

Because they really assume
the hippocratic oath
in their personal and professional life.
Ergo, they are neither hypocritical nor cynical.

If a guitarist
goes to jail for some criminal act,
no one says:
“He harasses artists for no reason.”

Musicians don't feel they are in the heights.
They relax themselves in the chords.
His part is not with the roar or the bitterness.
They were not born to make noise or disorder.
His job is harmony that lasts.

If they put a teacher
in prison for violating
the law, they don't say that's why
“we must escape from magisterial persecution.”

Teachers don't believe
they are predestined of no sign
to say Amen and say what Jerusalem
heard from the Divine Rabbi:
"Love each other; do good..."

That is why teachers give classes, to nurture good
citizens, technicians, workers, doctors...,
—and those who were born full of art—
without post-truth 2.0, without disinformation
newspapers or the massive corporate poison
of media sludge.

We want Peace and Development, not hostility
from those who hate who cries “Repent!”
in order to rhythm their villainy with the doctrine
of pity, there they go empty of love
how dark they are in weaving a crown of thorns
against the Great Homeland of the titan Dario.

Paranoia and more paranoia, just paranoia...
And nothing, nothing, absolutely
of Metanoia (*).

What Repentance means, it means beyond the obtuse, anachronistic, traditional mind, active in evil. Metanonia, which is before and after. Change of thought, direction, focus, perspective, meaning in Life. It encompasses leaving behind moral or spiritual collapse for a clean change of course.

Paranoia? No ! Metanonia, which is transcending? Not that ! It's paranoia. And never go back. Never descend. It is not an option, not for a Christian at least.

This is how we feel, always capable of strengthening ourselves, of transcending, of learning, always with the humility of our human condition. It is in humility that we build. It is by recognizing the Great Power of God that we can build. It is not by feeling

or believing ourselves to be God, because we are not God. And we cannot consider ourselves more than God.

In this Plane of Life, it is up to us, Human Beings, to be humble and recognize that Great Power of God, transformative, evolutionary, that Great Power of God that illuminates us, to always grow, to always learn, to transcend.

Evil does not defeat Good. Love can do everything, and the Love of God can infinitely, powerfully and strongly do everything!

Compañer@s, today we are also with a lot of Good News... Good News, Good Days, Good Times, Thanks to God!

We are announcing that next Monday, the Ministry of Finance has informed Comandante Daniel that all Public Servants on the Tax Payroll will receive their salary on Monday, January 8, well, in order to compensate for the necessary expenses of the Christmas, New Year's, Holidays, and to always have in our houses, in our homes, for our Families, Our Bread of every day, that Bread that God blesses.

We also have information about the “Comandante Pablo Úbeda” Hospital, who is one of the Founders of the Sandinista National Liberation Front; inaugurated in Juigalpa, Chontales, which will be attending 54,000 Insured, Retired, of Boaco, Chontales, Río San Juan, Zelaya Central and Bluefields. Good News in these Good Times that God gives us!

38,000 Families were attended by the Mobile Clinics in 348 Neighborhoods and Communities. And Health Fairs, 1,110 for 1,700 Neighborhoods and Communities, attending 118,214 Sisters and Brothers.

And we are also developing all the Programs to Recognize the Glory, the Honor, of having the Great Dario in our Blessed Homeland. We will be in Ciudad Darío on the 18th with a number of activities and a Solemn Session of the National Assembly. And on February 6, the day that he passed to another Plane of Life, to Eternity, from where he illuminates us, like the Sun that does not decline; on the 6th in the City of León, we have Floral Offerings to the Great Darío, at the place where the remains of his physical body lay, because Dario is everywhere, in spiritual and cultural terms, in terms of our National Honor and Glory.
We also celebrate Leonel Rugama, Poet, Guerrillero, who left us that undefeated phrase that marks us: “May your Mother surrender!” Nobody gives up here, and all those who wanted to use Rubén, all those who wanted to use Leonel, we already know where they are, we already know how they are, and we already know that here, in this Undefeated and Victorious Nicaragua, no one gives up. Honor and Glory to the Great Leonel Rugama!

Our Compañero Carlos Eduardo Díaz Moreira is in Algeria presenting today the Copies of Credentials before the Foreign Minister, Brother Ahmed Attaf. He is officially accredited as Ambassador of our Blessed, Glorious, Always, Always, Dignified, Sovereign, Free Nicaragua!

The Regional Electoral Councils also took office this January 3 and have been sworn in for the Victorious Caribbean 2024 Elections that will be held on March 3. Sovereign, Victorious Elections in our Autonomous Regions of the Caribbean of Nicaragua.

We also delivered Houses in Jinotega, Boaco, Nueva Guinea, El Coral; Cobblestone streets and roads in different Municipalities.

We have the entire Program of Streets for the People that we will be executing this year, always with Unconquered Hope and always trusting in God, who guides us, who illuminates us, who covers us, who blesses us, who tells us Always Beyond, Always Forward, by His Hand and for His Glory!

4,027 Streets for the People; 5,152 kilometers of Productive Roads; more than 7,000 Homes and more than 5,000 Lots in different modalities. All this is in the Municipalities, from INIFOM, National Government-Local Government Alliance or Mayor's Office.

2,000 Recreational Spaces permanently attended; 480 Parks, Viewpoints, Plazas, Walks and Barriers.

We have 600 Stadiums, Sports Centers, Gyms, Playing Fields and Grounds... Sports Spaces.

153 Fair Parks and we are going to expand to 52 more this year.

Markets: A number of sections and improvements in all the Markets of the Country.

Transportation: Expansion of 11 Terminals.

Culture and Creativity Houses: We have installed 186 and we are going for 40 more for this Year of Blessing and Victories!
Museums: We have 23 new Museums being  established between Community Museums and Museums of our Glorious Revolutionary Deeds, Revolutionary Epic; 191 installed.

We also have Healthcare and Schools Spaces, 856 and 368, with the Mayor's Offices and MINSA.

Water and Sanitation: 550 Projects, 480 Drainage and Sanitation also, in coordination with FISE, ENACAL, Mayor's Offices.

Land for Fire Stations: We will be completing more than 200 Fire Stations this year.

Holy Fields; Nurseries...

Arts Schools in the Countryside: 1,100 Countryside Communities, Rural Areas, will have their Arts Schools, 600 new. Last year we installed 500.

Trade Schools: More than 40,000 Protagonists guaranteed in 1,400 Courses this Year of Blessing and Victories. Trade Schools throughout the Country.

We give God thanks for so much Blessings and so much Strength to walk, in Peace and Good, in Fraternity, in Brotherhood, in Family, in Faith, in Community.

ENACAL informs us we have one more Well on the Masaya Highway to improve the service to 15,000 more Sisters and Brothers, from here in Managua.

We inaugurated today, Thursday, January 4, the INSS Delegation in Bluefields, another Delegation; named after Enrique Campbell Hooker. Better service to Insured and Pensioners of the Autonomous Region of the Southern Caribbean.

We also have the release of the School Meals, the first delivery. This Friday the 5th, the first caravan will be leaving to the Departments of Matagalpa, Zelaya Central, Chontales, Autonomous Region of the Southern Caribbean. We will be there witnessing the departure and reporting it to the Families. There are 180,000 quintals of various foods.

We are also, from the Ministry of Education, with the entire Dariana Day joining all the Celebration Activities, of Festivity, for the Joy of having our Great Rubén. We also have Infrastructure and School Equipment Works in La Cruz de Río Grande, in Laguna de Perlas.

Then we have, Compañer@s, INATEC with the construction and equipment of the Hospitality Teaching Classroom in the Bluefields Technical Center, with an investment of 15 million córdobas. We will be opening for 600 Protagonists, in the coming weeks.

Family Economy... We have Fairs and Festivals, Farmer's Markets, this coming Weekend, for Joy, Enjoyment, and Profit, for Everyone.

We have 850 Property Titles, today, in Posoltega, Managua, San Carlos, El Coral, Santo Tomás, Granada, Altagracia, Buenos Aires, Wiwilí, Santa María de Pantasma, Estelí, La Trinidad, Rancho Grande, El Jicaral, Santa Rosa del Peñón, Larreynaga, El Sauce, Río Blanco, Matagalpa, Muy Muy, Matiguás, Palacagüina, Telpaneca, San Juan de Río Coco, San Fernando, El Jícaro, Ocotal, Macuelizo; Northern Caribbean, Rosita; Southern Caribbean, Corn Island.

850 Families certified as Homeowners. And 2,500 more, between the 8th and 12th of this Month of Blessings, the beginning of a Year that God Willing will be of Success, of Work to prosper, of more Security, of more Stability, of more Future.

Today we also have the inauguration of Electric Power in San José del Paraíso, in Río Blanco, benefiting 150 Sisters and Brothers.

And we conclude with a Mourning Note, but also a Note of Hope for Eternal Life for us Believers. Compañero Róger Antonio Cajina Vásquez, 63 years old, died in Chiquilistagua. He was the General Secretary of the Unions of the Mayor's Office of Managua, of the Bismarck Martínez Municipal Confederation, and Secretary of the National Organization of UNE-FNT.

We want to give a Fraternal and Solidarity Hug to his wife Martha Faustina Ocampo, to his children, Dany Luis Cajina López, Ana Georgina Cajina López, María de los Ángeles Cajina López and Róger Antonio Cajina López. To all of them our Affection, our Recognition, and we know that they inherit, that they follow the Legacy of their father, Exemplary Militant of the Sandinista National Liberation Front, loyal to his People, to the Nicaraguan Families and to the Nicaraguan Workers until his last breath.

We say goodbye to Róger and tell him: Always Beyond! We are Together, We Continue Together, Working Together, as Beings on this Plane of Life, and as a Spirit that is always inspiring us and filling us with Strength.

Big Hugs to all Nicaraguan Families on this special day, a day to think big about everything we are, about the Real Truths, about the Force of Truth; to think Big and do Big, from the Infinite Truth that is Justice, that is Concord, and that is Love. We believe and are Beings of Struggle, of Strength and of Big Love.

Big Hugs, Compañer@s, from our Comandante Daniel. And Let's Go Forward, it is ours, of the People and Families of Good Will, who live and work honestly in this Nicaragua of so much Blessings, the Future is ours! Big hugs.
