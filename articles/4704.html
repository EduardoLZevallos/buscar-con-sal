Long Live Venezuela !

Enviado por tortilla el Lun, 29/07/2024 - 17:58

Operning remarks by Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua
in the Midday Edition of Multinoticias on Channel 4

July 29th 2024

Good afternoon, Compañera, and a very good afternoon, Beloved Families of our Nicaragua, Blessed, Sovereign, Always Full of Dignity, Always Strong of Soul, of Life, of Heart... And Always, Forever Free!
 

And here we are, celebrating the Great Victory of our Venezuelan Brothers and Sisters. Yesterday, throughout the day we were following their impeccable Electoral Process... Impeccable, given all the experience our Compañeras and Compañeros have there in Venezuela.

 

So Many Elections! One of the countries with the most democratic capacity in the world as regards elections. So many Elections have been held in that Country, with so many Results validating the connection between Venezuela's Families, the Venezuelan People, and the Revolutionary, Evolutionary Project begun by Comandante Hugo Chávez Frías, Comandante Eterno, yesterday was his Birthday, and what a Gift he received from his People.

 

Now today, with Nicolás reaffirmed at the helm as President, confirmed by Venezuela's Families, that is the guarantee that this People is advancing in Light, Life and Truth, advancing in absolute Fidelity to itself, because they are the People of the Liberator, the People of Bolívar, the People of Chávez, the People of Nicolás, of Cilia, of Diosdado, of so many Comrades who have done everything that possibly can be done for the People to advance as Protagonists and, above all, with all the Rights that they deserve: The Right of Venezuela's People to live in Peace, and the Right of the Venezuela's People to use their own resources, their abundant natural resources, to work and prosper.

 

But, after all, we know very well what Empires are, and how they strive to destroy those Processes, our Processes, of Liberation. We have lived it in our own flesh, we have lived it over Centuries, we have lived it recently, and we know how the hordes of the International Right respond to the Triumphs of the world’s Peoples.

 

Because, as we say day after day, for them the People should never be in Power, for them, the world's Peoples must continue to be subjugated, but above all they must continue to be swindled, stolen, fleeced, robbed of their own resources. And that is the reason for the furious attacks, since all of them say the same things, they all have the same postures and body language, which are also the postures and gestures of ignoble Souls.

 

They are all the same, cut from the same pattern, with the same scissors, and they want to apply their patterns, their scissors, to us, to our necks, to our throats, as our people might say. But They Can't Prevail, They Haven't Prevailed, They Could Not Prevail, Nor Will They, because we are Peoples Conscious, in the first place, of our Rights and of our Power, and, too, we are Peoples who know what we need, what we have now, and what we can achieve.

 

Here we are, then, embraced with that Great People, united with that Great People, celebrating their Great Victory of yesterday, and celebrating the Character with which they have faced the harshest moments of the war they impose on us, seeking to continue stealing from us, or to continue dominating us, by their lights.

 

But in fact, we the Peoples fight to overcome, and we say to the Imperialists of the Earth: You will not pass...! We say it from the Soul, we say it full of the Power and Strength of our Great Forebears, our Heroes, our Teachers, our Guides.

 

Let's Go Onward, we tell the Venezuelan People, for We Are Together as Sisters and Brothers. To Nicolás we proudly say: A Great Victory, Nicolás...! A Great victory for you, for all your People, for the Families who know where Peace resides and where the Best Things reside: Peace which is Love, Love which is Harmony, which is Unity, which is Union. That is why we always know Bolivar's Supreme Dream of Union means Love and the Just Development of our Peoples in Prosperity, Happiness and Peace.

 

Many, many Congratulations to everyone there, and Together, Everyone Together, Let us indeed Go Onward. The Venezuelan People, United, will never be Defeated! We are Together, Together, walking the Paths of Faith, of Hope, along Ways of Enlightenment, along Ways of Security, of Our American Unity... Unity for the Common Good, Unity for Our Rights, Unity for our Joy, and Unity to grow, to prosper, to get ahead.

 

Warm Embraces to you Nicolás, from Daniel, from me, from all of us; to Cilia, to Diosdado, to all your Great People, to Delcy, beloved compañera, Warm Embraces, Congratulations! And let us live the Joy of consolidating Peace and Goodness. Again, Warm Embraces.
