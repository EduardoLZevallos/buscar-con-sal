Rubén Darío Selected Poems - Book launch

Submitted bytortilla onJue, 21/01/2021 - 09:25
Register here for tonight's event with the Cervantes Institute of Manchester and Leeds, with world-renowned hispanist Professor Adam Feinstein, who will read from and discuss his book "Rubén Darío: Selected Poems", the first English-language rhyming translation of the poetry of Nicaragua's National Poet, the "Prince of Castillian Letters" Rubén Darío, whose 154th birth anniversary was commemorated this month.
 
Professor Feinstein will be joined by acclaimed academics Professor Antonio Martínez-Arboleda (University of Leeds) and Nicaraguan-born Professor Carlos Fonseca Grigsby (University of Oxford) who will discuss the new book and Darío's poetry, as well as Spanish actress María Estévez Serrano, who will perform poetry readings in Spanish (while Professor Feinstein will do the English readings).
