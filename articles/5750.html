Ceremony of the XXVII Graduation of Cadets of the Leonel Rugama Academy

"Surrender your mother!” of the National Police

December 16th 2024

Address by Inspector Ledys Imara Maradiaga Mejía
Leading student.

Compañero President of the Republic and Supreme Chief of the National Police, Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra; Compañera Vice President of the Republic, Rosario Murillo Zambrana; Director General of the National Police, First Commissioner Francisco Javier Díaz Madriz; Comandante-in-Chief of our Nicaraguan Army, Army General Julio César Avilés Castillo.

Minister of the Interior, compañera María Amelia Coronel Kinloch; Minister Adviser to the President of the Republic for Security Affairs, Commissioner General Horacio Sebastián Rocha López; Deputy Director General of the National Police, Commissioner General Zhukov Serrano Pérez; Chief of the General Staff, Major General Bayardo Ramón Rodríguez Ruiz; Inspector General, Major General Marvin Elías Corrales Rodríguez; Rector of the University of Police Sciences “Leonel Rugama" Surrender your mother! Commissioner General Zaida Matilde Pineda Arauz.

High Command of the National Police, Special Guests, beloved Teachers and Instructors; Graduate Officers and relatives who are accompanying us on this Twenty-seventh Graduation of Cadets of the Baccalaureate of Police Sciences.

Good evening, one and all.

On this special day, it is an honor to address these Words of Gratitude on behalf of my Compañeros and Compañeras of the Twenty-Seventh Graduation of Police Cadets.

Our Graduation is dedicated to the Legacy of Comandante Eduardo Contreras Escobar "Marcos”, a member of the Historic Directorate of the Sandinista National Liberation Front, brave, bold, loyal to the Principles of the Sandinista Cause, Head of the Guerrilla Commando Juan José Quezada, who on December 27th 1974, stormed the house of the Somoza minister Chema Castillo, freeing prominent FSLN militants and members of the Historic National Directorate, among them our Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra and José Benito Escobar.The example of Comandante Eduardo Contreras Escobar "Marcos" inspires us to be loyal, obedient, committed to our Heroes and Martyrs in serving Individuals, Families and Communities, defending Peace and Prosperity.

We give thanks God, we thank our Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra, President of the Republic and Supreme Chief of the National Police, and our Vice-President of the Republic, Compañera Rosario Murillo Zambrana, for her determined support to our Leonel Rugama University of Police Sciences “Surrender your mother!”, where we were provided with academic training in the areas of Crime Prevention, Crime Investigation, Police Intervention Techniques, Criminology, Traffic Accidents, Human Talent Management, material and technological resources.

We are a group of Police Officers committed to Sandinista Principles and Values.

Similarly, we thank our Relatives, for without their support it would not have been possible to achieve our proposed objectives and goals.

The 33 Officers who graduated today (9 Compañeras and 24 Compañeros) were forged in the Principles and Values of the Legacy of our National Heroes and Sandinista Martyrs, to strengthen and defend Peace and the Well-being of Families and Communities. As our Compañera Rosario Murillo Zambrana, Vice President of the Republic, has said: Without Peace there is no Progress, the Peace that is the Essential Right of Peoples, of Individuals, of Families in the World.

Our gratitude to our Chiefs, our Superiors, Teachers and Instructors for their perseverance and professionalism.

We Cadets of the Twenty-Seventh Graduation 2024, ratify our Loyalty and Obedience, to our Supreme Leadership, Compañero Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra, President of the Republic and Supreme Chief of the National Police, and Compañera Rosario Murillo Zambrana, Vice President of the Republic.

Honor and Glory to Comandante Eduardo Contreras Escobar. Honor and Glory! Honor and Glory!

Long live the National Police! Viva, Viva, Viva!

Long live the Volunteer Police! Viva, Viva, Viva!

Thank you very much for your attention.

Address by First Commissioner General
Francisco Javier Díaz Madriz
Director General of the National Police

Good evening everyone; Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra, President of the Republic and Supreme Chief of the National Police; Compañera Rosario Murillo Zambrana, Vice President of the Republic.

Comandante-in-Chief of our Nicaraguan Army, Army General Julio César Avilés Castillo; Commissioner General Horacio Rocha López, Minister Advisor to the President of the Republic, Compañera Amelia Coronel Kinloch, Minister of the Interior.

Brothers and Sisters of the High Command, Major General Bayardo Ramón Rodríguez Ruiz, Chief of Staff, Major General Marvin Elías Corrales Rodríguez, Inspector General of our Nicaraguan Army and Members of the Military Council.

Commissioner General Zhukov Serrano Pérez Deputy Director General; Commissioner General Zaida Matilde Pineda Arauz, Rector of the University of Police Sciences “Leonel Rugama" “Surrender your mother!”

Brothers and Sisters, General, Senior and Junior Officers of the National Police; Military and Police Attachés accredited in our Country; Officers graduated from this Twenty-Seventh Promotion and your relatives; Guests of the Ministerial Cabinet of Our Good Government, Friends of the Media.

We thank you for joining us on this Twenty-Seventh University of Police Sciences “Leonel Rugama" “Surrender your mother!”, dedicated to the legacy of Comandante Eduardo Contreras Escobar "Marcos" who joined the Sandinista National Liberation Front at a very young age. Comandante Eduardo Contreras Escobar, was Head of the Guerrilla Commando "Juan José Quezada”, which, on December 27th 1974, stormed the house of the Somoza Minister Chema Castillo, a historic operation of the FSLN, that broke with the stage of silent accumulation of forces. This action resulted in the release of prominent Sandinista militants and Members of the FSLN's Historic National Directorate, including Comandante President Daniel Ortega Saavedra and José Benito Escobar.

The police officers graduated in this promotion honor his legacy and make his feelings our own, when he said: ”We Nicaraguans can now look anyone in the face, because now we can proudly say that we are fighting imperialism with weapons in hand, thus helping achieve the final liberation of all the Peoples of Latin America." Eduardo Contreras Escobar

To refer to Comandante Eduardo Contreras Escobar, "Marcos", is to speak of Revolutionary Love, of the Just Cause, he bequeathed us his heroism, his Sandinista firmness, anti-imperialism and his loyalty to the Sandinista National Liberation Front. The motto of the Commando "Juan José Quezada" was: All orders will be fulfilled except one: surrender. A Free Nation or Death!

In this Twenty-Seventh Graduation of Cadets, 33 Compañeros and Compañeras join the police family at the service of our people (9 compañeras and 24 compañeros), assuminf the commitment to defend Peace, protect the Life and Physical Integrity of Individuals, families and communities. Of these, 25 completed their studies at the “Leonel Rugama” University of Police Sciences and 8 at Universities of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Russian Federation, also 9 Compañeras are part of this promotion, Worthy representatives of Nicaraguan women, of our Women Police Officers.

The training of the graduates of the Bachelor's Degree in Police Sciences, from 2021 to 2024, was focused on the topics of Crime Prevention, Investigation of Common and Organized Crime, Traffic Accidents, Criminology, Police Intervention Techniques, Human Talent Management, Material and Technological Resources.

They were trained in the revolutionary principles and the legacy of our Heroes and Martyrs, prepared to give their Lives out of Love for this Blessed, Forever Free Nicaragua and committed to defending Peace and Prosperity.

Beloved Graduates, we will continue to work in coordination, articulation and communication with the Institutions of our Good Government, Municipal Authorities, Political Secretaries and the territorial structure of the FSLN, so that Nicaragua continues to be one of the safest countries in Central and Latin America.

You gave your best effort during these 4 years of studies, which have concluded with discipline. In this Promotion we offer recognition to the Graduates deserving of the Titles and Degrees they hold today; we recognize those who obtained the best grades:

Inspector Ledys Imara Maradiaga Mejía, Leading Student. Inspector Estiven Amaro Orozco Cano, Second Leading Student. Inspector Alexis Alfredo Rocha Suárez, Third Leading Student.

We especially congratulate the nine female compañeras who graduated in this Twenty-Seventh Graduation of Cadets, making special mention of Leading Student, Inspector Ledys Imara Maradiaga Mejía, for her our respect and admiration.

Beloved Graduates, on behalf of the National Police High Command, we congratulate you on your graduation and your promotion to the Rank of Inspector, we urge you to continue achieving success and continue setting an example, serving with Love, committed to the security of individuals, families and communities.

You are the next generation and you carry in your hearts the Principles and Values bequeathed to us by our Heroes and Martyrs, the imperishable example of Comandante Eduardo Contreras Escobar “Marcos”.

In this Celebration we recognize the Teachers and Instructors, the Chiefs and Officers of the University of Police Sciences “Leonel Rugama" “Surrender your mother!”, for all your contributions to the training of these new Officers over these four years.

Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra, President of the Republic and Supreme Chief of the National Police; Compañera Rosario Murillo Zambrana, Vice President of the Republic, we police women and men express our gratitude for your guidance and successful leadership of the National Police, in addition to your unconditional support for us in fulfillment of our Constitutional Mission defending Peace and Security.

I want to express my congratulations to the families who have supported our graduates in their university preparation. Your support was essential for the completion of their studies.

Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra, President of the Republic and Supreme Chief of the National Police, Compañera Rosario Murillo Zambrana, Vice President of the Republic, tonight, we the Police reaffirm our Loyalty and Obedience, Loyalty and Obedience by which we reiterate our willingness to continue working tso as to strengthen the Peace, Security, Tranquility of individuals, families and communities.

As our Compañera Vice President Rosario Murillo Zambrana said: "How we thank God for Peace, Peace and the Right to Well-Being! Without peace we cannot advance, that is why we defend it so much, with our Heart, with our Soul. Our fundamental heritage is peace”. Rosario Murillo.

To conclude, we wish all Nicaragua's families a Merry Christmas and a Prosperous Year 2025 of Well Being and Joy.

Long live Comandante Eduardo Contreras Escobar!
Honor and Glory to our Heroes and Martyrs!
Long live the National Police!
Long live the Volunteer Police “Comandante Julio Buitrago”!

Good evening.

Address by Daniel

Nicaraguan brothers and sisters, Families of these Lands, of these Heroic Peoples, who have always fought for the Sovereignty of the Nation, for Peace.

Compañera Rosario Murillo Zambrana, Vice President of the Republic of Nicaragua; First Commissioner Francisco Javier Díez Madriz, Director General of the National Police; Army General Julio César Avilés Castillo, Comandante-in-Chief of our Nicaraguan Army; Commissioner General Horacio Sebastián Rocha López, Minister of Public Security; Compañera María Amelia Coronel Kinloch, Minister of the Interior.

Commissioner General Zhukov Serrano Pérez, Deputy Director General of the National Police; Major General Bayardo Ramón Rodríguez Ruiz, Chief of the General Staff of the Nicaraguan Army; Major General Marvin Elías Corrales Rodríguez, Inspector General of the Nicaraguan Army; Commissioner General Zaida Matilde Pineda Arauz, Rector of the Leonel Rugama University of Police Sciences, with the motto: “Surrender your mother!”

Nicaraguan brothers and sisters, Nicaraguan families, Families of these Young People who are graduating today, who accompany us here and who also preside over this Event, honoring us with their presence. They come from some of the most distant places in our country; others will be from places not so distant.

The Compañera Leading Student is from Río San Juan, she comes from those Heroic Lands, where they fought in the Insurrection, in the month of October, in the Insurrectional October Offensive, when the columns of the Sandinista Front managed to take the main city of that Municipality, of that Department, San Carlos. The National Guad garrison there was well prepared, having combat units trained by the Yankees, and they were alert because we were already entering the Final Offensive, which would lead us to Victory in a short time. So also, on a day like today, in the battles that were being waged in the years leading up to the Victory of July 19, in 1976, in the Department of Matagalpa, in the mountains of Matagalpa, a Heroic Compañera, Adelaida García, originally from the Cerro Colorado Community, in the Municipality of Matiguás, fell in combat. That was a guerrilla zone, a combat zone, of the Sandinista Front.

And also in 1978, on a day like today, the combat took place there in Peñas Blancas, where there was a combat in which the National Guard was accompanied by their aviation, with mortars, and the guerrilla units first with their rifles, later the mortars would arrive, then even artillery would arrive, thanks to the solidarity of Comandante Fidel Castro.

And indeed, in combat on that day, in the month of December, on December 16th, the Combat Unit of the Sandinista Front in action was the Benjamín Zeledón Combat Unit, that was the name of the Combat Unit.

And the combat, as I was telling you, was there on the border, near the River El Naranjo and near the town of Los Mojones in Rivas, and of course, Somoza Guards fell, and our Sandinista compañeros and compañeras also fell, including compañeros Roberto Lara Pineda, Ariel Sánchez, Danilo Ponce, José Francisco Gaitán Muñoz and Ramón Escobar.

There too fell compañeros originally from Panama and other compañeros and compañeras originally from South America, because there were many Internationalist fighters on the Southern Front.

And today we salute the Supreme High Command of the Nicaraguan Army, the Ministry of the Interior, the National Police; the Members of the Government Cabinet, the National Council of Universities, the National Evaluation and Accreditation Council; the Compañera Mayor of Managua; the Naval, Air and Police Military Attaches who are accompanying us; the Professors, Students, Cadets, of the University of Police Sciences, and we have already greeted the relatives who are here presiding over this Event.

We also greet the Brothers and Sisters who are the Combatants of the Volunteer Units of the National Police.

Here before the People of Nicaragua, before the Civil and Military Authorities, before the Families present, we are going to proceed to swear in these Compañeros and Compañeras who come from the People, their Roots are in the People, and now as Graduates they have become Police Officers to serve the People.

And thus the Great Unity decisive for the defense of Peace is being consolidated; both the Police and the Army come from the People, these are their Roots, and thus, the Battle for Peace is being waged by All of Us Together, the Army, the Police, and the People... The People, who are organized neither in the Army nor in the Police, are a determining force to defend peace.

The Coast, the Peoples of the Caribbean showing proof and example of their commitment to Peace, incorporated into the National Police, just as they are also integrated into the Nicaraguan Army.

Inspector César Alexander Torres Hernández, originally from the Department of León... León, of Rigoberto Lopez Perez! León, where Rubén Darío lies! Inspector Dominic Leonel Medina Reyes, originally from the Department of Carazo.

Remarks by Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua, after the XXVII Graduation
of Cadets from the Leonel Rugama National Police Academy “Surrender your mother!”

December 16th 2024

A very good evening, and very good evening too, Beloved Families of our Blessed Nicaragua.

Today we are here with these Young Women and Men, who are completing a stage of training, learning, of gathering Knowledge so as to apply that Knowledge, all the while working for the Security of our Families, the Security of our Youth, the Security of Nicaragua's People, which is the priority.

For us to live securely is to live in Peace. To live in Peace is to live in calm and security. And so this Month of December is especially beautiful, not only for all that we celebrate as Christians, as the People of God, but also for all these Graduations and Promotions, be it from Preschool or High School, in Universities, in Technical Schools, or in the Army and Police Academies of Police Sciences or Military Sciences, leading us not only to think, but to assure us all of the certainty that We are Moving Forward and that Education is a Priority, precisely so as to prepare ourselves, to be able to serve better, and to better realize ourselves as Human Beings.

Because the fulfillment of each one of us depends on how we feel integrated into the Community, into Society, in this Country that we love so much. Integrating so as to move onward! That is why I say that all these learning processes are a significant advance.

And today I was also watching on Russian Federation television a very beautiful report about the Universities in our Countryside, I always insist: the Universities, the Culture Centers, the Trade Schools, everything that we are managing to carry forward , is because we have Peace...! Universities, Technical Schools, Technical Centers, Centers for the Development of all our faculties, all our capabilities.

And this is possible because we have Peace, and because Nicaragua's Families are full of confidence, grateful to the Heavenly Father and with full confidence that We Are Going Forward United. That is why we want to learn, that is why we want to work more and more, that is why our Vocation to make progress becomes a reality every day at all these levels, in all these spaces where we are realizing ourselves as the People of God.

This is a Month for celebrating that we are one Big Family, a Month to tell us, each one of us, that we are consolidating the Future of Everyone in our Homes and our Families where, because we hold to our Values, there is our Heroism, there is our Strength, there is our Spirit, in our Homes, with our Families.

From there we set out every day on the path of advancement in our Blessed Nicaragua, from our Values; our Ideals and our Values, because we have faith, and also, because every Dawn also represents, for all of us, the Sun, that Sun which illuminates us and never sets... The Sun of a Hardworking People, a People Victorious by virtue of being Hardworking, being Heroic, being Brave, being Noble, a People full of Faith, a Christian People.

Nicaragua is made of Vigor, of Glory, of Faith, of Hope, of Trust, of Certainty. We are made to be Free and we are made to advance and make our way in that Always Further On which our General of Free Men and Women, Augusto Sandino, left us as a Magnificent Motto.

An embrace to all the families. An embrace to the Families of the Graduates, to the Graduates, and above all, for the assurance all of us have that each one of them, each one of their Families represent pillars for the Secure, Fullfilling Future of Nicaragua's Families.

Warm embraces, Compañeras and Compañeros.
