Enviado por tortilla el Mié, 31/05/2023 - 09:56

Important Nicaragua Hearing of the International People's Tribunal:
Saturday, June 3

 Join us to hear the effects of U.S. imperialism on the people of Nicaragua.

11am ET, 10am Central, 9am Nicaragua and 8am Pacific.  Interpretation provided.

REGISTER HERE

 

 This hearing will feature expert testimony about the following:

1.    Nicaragua’s consistent use of international law to resolve conflicts, and the efforts of Fr. Miguel d’Escoto to promote a more just United Nations.

2.    Individual victims of US aggression

3.    The long history of aggressions against Nicaragua by the United States

4.    Effects of illegal coercive measures on the economy and development

5.    Effects of illegal coercive measures on the health sector, especially during a pandemic

6.    Aggressions financed by the US to foment the failed coup d'état (2018) as well as to finance fake news through networks and written media
