BADIL’s International Mobilization Course in the Inalienable Rights of the Palestinian People

Submitted bytortilla onLun, 05/07/2021 - 10:02

BADIL, 5 de julio 2021
http://www.badil.org/en/

BADIL’s second inaugural International Mobilization Course for the Inalienable Rights of the Palestinian People will be held online between 31 July 2021 and 13 August 2021. Please note that the course is free.

Designed to raise awareness on issues, both current and historical, that impede on the Palestinian peoples’ access to and realization of their rights, the program is a unique opportunity for international advocates, activists, mobilizers, policy officers and other interested individuals to gain a deeper understanding of a human rights-based approach to advance the international solidarity movement. he program includes in-depth lectures and group discussions, complemented by virtual field visits to refugee camps and areas under threat of displacement. These discussions and virtual visits will allow participants to witness the mechanisms of Israeli policies of forced displacement, colonization and apartheid and how Palestinians endure such violations and how, in some cases, they develop ways to resist these measures.           
 
Applications are open until 12 July 2021. We are asking all willing partners to share this information and encourage available international contacts and colleagues to apply. For more information, please visit here or see BADIL’s homepage. To apply, the form can be found here or within the link above.
