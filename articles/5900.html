Foreign Affairs Ministry welcomes Cuba's new Ambassador to Nicaragua

Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 11/01/2025 - 18:03

MINISTERIO
DE
RELACIONES EXTERIORES
Managua, Nicaragua


THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS INFORMS

Today, Saturday, January 11th at one o'clock in the afternoon, Compañera Iliana Teresa Fonseca Lorente, the new Ambassador of the Heroic People and Government of the Republic of Cuba in Nicaragua, arrives in Nicaragua.
 
We welcome you with great joy, in our Blessed and Forever Free Nicaragua, always with the invariable Admiration of this Nicaragua, where we have shared, and we continue to share, so many Struggles and so many Hopes.
 
We send an embrace to the Fraternal People of Cuba, Land of the Immortal Leader of the Cuban Revolution, our Comandante Fidel Castro; embrace to Compañero Miguel Díaz-Canel, President of the Heroic Cuban People and to Compañero Comandante and Historic Leader of the Cuban Revolution, Army General Raúl Castro Ruz.


Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Republic of Nicaragua
