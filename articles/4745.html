Government announces measures to improve public sector effectiveness

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 02/08/2024 - 18:07

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

PRESS RELEASE

The Government of Reconciliation and National Unity, Government of the People-President, informs Nicaragua's Families that, after evaluating the Programs and their successes and challenges at the end of the First Semester of this Year 2024, it has decided :

1. To proceed to assess the work of each Institution, its Directions and Projects.

2. This Evaluation involves re-ordering, restructuring and redesign in order to achieve savings and efficiency in public sector expenditure and management.

3. The Measure is aimed at generating greater availability of resources from the General Budget of the Republic,  strengthening direct attention and promoting greater progress in each of the Proposals under development, so as to address and resolve outstanding challenges.

4. This revision will allow us to mobilize more resources from the savings obtained, in order to expedite responses relating to all the processes pending.

5. Likewise, it will optimize our human capital in terms of stability, quality and professionalism, bringing better results in public service.

The Government of Reconciliation and National Unity will undertake the Second Semester of this year 2024 with more strength and capacity leading to timely action addressing those tasks that demand greater promptness and effectiveness.

Managua, August 2nd 2024
Council of Communication and Citizenship
Presidency of the Republic
