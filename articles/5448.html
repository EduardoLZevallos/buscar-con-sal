Everyone Together, Let Us All Go Onward!

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 05/11/2024 - 16:41

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

EVERYONE TOGETHER, LET US ALL GO ONWARD!

Message from Nicaragua to the World Parliamentary Forum against Fascism, Neo-Fascism and Similar Ideas

Caracas, Venezuela

Presented by the Head of Delegation,
Compañero Orlando Tardencilla.

Beloved Compañero Nicolás Maduro Moros
President of the Bolivarian Government of Venezuela.

Beloved Compañero Yvan Gil
Foreign Minister of the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela.

Beloved Compañero Jorge Rodriguez
President of Venezuela's Legislature, the National Assembly.

Beloved Compañeros and Compañeras Leaders of the PSUV.

Beloved Compañeros and Compañeras participants of this World Parliamentary Forum Against Fascism, Neo-Fascism and Similar Ideas.

We convey the Greetings and Fraternal Embrace of Comandante Daniel Ortega and Compañera Rosario Murillo, President and Vice President of our Blessed and Forever Free Nicaragua.

The Sandinista National Liberation Front, its Militancy, along with the People of Nicaragua and its Revolutionary Government, welcome this Forum for Discussion and Joint Adoption of Tasks so as to confront with greater courage and strength, this Struggle and all the Struggles against our universal Enemy: North American Imperialism in all its violent, genocidal facets.

Caracas and all of Venezuela, is one of the victorious fronts that welcomes us over these days, with the Love of the Bolivarian People, with the Fraternal Embrace of Chavismo and its Vanguard, the PSUV, of its Leaders,  Nicolás, Delcy, Diosdado, Jorge and the entire Leadership of the Party.

We hold this Working Meeting at a time when the brutal genocide carried out by the Government of Israel against the Palestinian People continues in practice and is now spreading via the cowardly, bestial terrorist attacks against the People of Lebanon. But they have not been able, and will not be able, to break the Fighting Spirit of the Palestinian People.

We denounce the way Fascism adjusts and manages its lackeys, reformulating its criminal strategies and trying to institutionalize its horrors and attacks on the Life and Integrity of Peoples. Fascism also works to sow hatred, discord and disunity among our peoples. Look to Christ! As our peasants would say, when they noticed the lies, deceits, and other forms of sowing evil among Sisters and Brothers.

With horror and indignation we watch the multiplication of very sophisticated aggression against Peoples whose only crime is to live with dignity. These acts rejected by the vast majority of States and Peoples of the world demand the adoption of urgent measures demanding an end to these criminal acts against people's lives.

We parliamentarians have particular challenges, as direct representatives of our Peoples, to maintain our firm position as an Anti-Imperialist and Anti-Fascist Forum in the face of the perversions of Imperialism and its lackeys, its sad and servile flunkeys

Our Peoples demand actions in every part of the world where the criminal hand of Fascism is seen. The combat should not be allowed to be disunited and sporadic but one Always With The People! May Victory be Certain and achieving Peace the final prize!!!

Faithful to the Principle of Sovereignty, Nicaragua rejects all those who  align with the imperial decision not to recognize the democratic will of the Venezuelan people.

Given the overwhelming, well supported proofs of the absolute Bolivarian Victory on July 28th 2024, it is not enough merely to extend our Heart of Solidarity to the Glorious People of Bolívar, Chávez and Nicolás, to that Great Bolivarian Community, united in Ideals, Values, Struggles and Horizons of Peace and Well-being, for we are also obliged to demand full Respect from our Governments for the Sovereign and unimpeachable Victory of the PSUV and Brother Nicolás.

We must leave no room for doubt about the Principles of Sovereignty and Self-Determination that inspire our Peoples, Values which are at the same time the sustenance of real Revolutionary anti-imperialism.

From Our Free, Blessed and Victorious Nicaragua, wrapped in the Spirit of Sandino, the Dignity of Zeledón, the Heart of Darío, the Firmness of Carlos and the endearing Love of Tomás, we greet the Governments and especially the Peoples who, with their Hearts in their hands, fight, United, for a Better World and we are also convinced that the Imperial Demon is in retreat  every day and too that a New World has already begun to grow.... Let us unite our dispersed energies, looking to the Sun of Freedom to illuminate our victories.

Long live Anti-Imperialism!
Long Live the Bolivarian Revolution!
Long Live the Peoples of the World!
A Free Patriotic Nation or Death!

"Let the brothers and sisters be united because that is the first law. Ensure true union at all times, because if they fight among themselves, outsiders will devour them.”

Fragment of the Poem “Martín Fierro” by José Hernández.

Tuesday, November 5th, 2024
