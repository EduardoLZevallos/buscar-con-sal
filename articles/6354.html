Government insists on joint appeal against sanctions

Submitted bytortilla onLun, 13/05/2019 - 10:07

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS


Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE DELEGATION OF GOVERNMENT OF
RECONCILIATION AND NATIONAL UNITY (GRUN)
TO THE NEGOTIATION TABLE
 

Monday, May 13, 2019

The Delegation of the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity at the Negotiation Table greets the Nicaraguan families and the International Community, as a new week of work for Understanding, Justice, Reconciliation and Peace initiates in Nicaragua.

We welcome these new days with Faith and Hope and with an important Agenda for the Nicaraguan People that we must address, tirelessly, in order to provide good news from the Negotiation Table.
 
The Delegation of the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity reiterates our willingness to comply with all the commitments, within the framework of our Constitution and Laws. We insist to the counterpart, as this new week opens, on the need to work with sincerity and good faith, in order to renew confidence and ensure progress.

As we have repeated every day, it is indispensable to address all the issues thinking of Nicaragua, as Nicaraguans, and thinking about the Nicaraguan families; As Christians, we must reflect, especially among the poorest, who have been the most affected by the foolishness, selfishness and violence that has imposed on us all, and that, as we know, has destroyed much of the progress that had been made in the just and indispensable fight against poverty.

They riled against Nicaragua, but as we also know, they were not able and they will never be able to destroy the Faith and Hope, the Encouragement, the Nicaraguan Soul, which “knows of struggle and honor”, and which advances, in spite of so much Animosity, with Christian Victory and Solidarity, Victories of Love and Common Good.

Once again we call for reflection and respect, in order to recognize the damages caused against Nicaragua by the terrorist coup, and we seek Justice, Reparation and Commitment toward Non-Repetition.

We insist on the need to seriously and consequently issue a Joint Call for the suspension and cessation of Sanctions (not against individuals, but against the country); sanctions which are unilateral and illegitimate and violate international law, as well as the human rights of all Nicaraguans, mindful of the Homeland and of all Nicaraguans.

This Joint Appeal constitutes an indispensable step towards Reparation.

We trust in God and from the Sane Optimism and tireless spirit of each Nicaraguan, we reaffirm that Nicaragua deserves Justice, Understanding and Peace.

With our historic Strength of Will, firmness of character and irrevocable decision of Sovereign Freedom and Democracy, We will Overcome...!

The Government of Reconciliation and National Unity, assumes the Victorious Spiritual Legacy of Sandino, and Patriotically subscribes with Honor, Always and Beyond...!

Hope, will never tire...!

Monday, May 13, 2019
Delegation of the GRUN
at the Negotiation Table
