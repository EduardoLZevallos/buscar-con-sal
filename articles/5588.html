ROSARIO: "Fidel, an Extraordinary Visionary who lives and animates the Strength of our Dignities and the Spirit of Victory at all times"

Enviado por tortilla el Lun, 25/11/2024 - 18:16

Opening remarks of declarations by Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua,

in the Midday Edition of Multinoticias on Canal 4

 

November 25th 2024

A very good afternoon, Compañera and good afternoon, Beloved Families of this Blessed Nation, Always Dignified, Always Sovereign. Made strong by Faith and Affection, strengthening and safeguarding Peace every day.

 

Today Monday marks the beginning of another Week, another Great Week of Continuity in all the Battles, with the Joy and Pride of being, of vibrating with and living in the New Times; Joy, Pride and Thanks to God ! Times of True Nicaraguan Patriotism, Nicaraguan by the Grace of the Lord; Times for advancing, making progress in the Fight against Poverty, which is our Mandate and our Principal Commitment.

 

We have also seen, through communications media, various expressions of the Women of the World, commemorating, because we cannot say celebrating, but commemorating today, the Day the United Nations established against Violence against Women, a Struggle that should be waged every day... Why? Because such violence takes away, in any of its expressions, the valuable Lives of Women Human Beings.

 

And so from here, from this Blessed Nation, we express our deep indignation and condemnation at this injustice, this unjustifiable feeling of vulnerability and insecurity among women in so many parts of the world; this claim, this demand for Justice. Vulnerability and insecurity constitutes such absurd injustice that nothing and no one can justify, and even more so in these Times of what we call Civilization.

 

We are resolutely speaking out against the Violence being carried out all over the world, even in the First World. That's where the demonstrations came out yesterday, demanding, and very justly demanding, respect for Women's Lives so that we don't have to feel that indignation, although we no longer live it, in the face of the insecurity, the vulnerability to which women are subjected in so many parts of the world.

 

Thank God, in our Nicaragua, which is a country of Revolution and Evolution, women are living the New Times of the People, aware of our History, our Struggles, our Victories, and the institutional and daily progress of Gender Equality, which is a Constitutional Mandate.

 

But we must also be aware that these Battles must be fought every day, so that what is Constitutional, Institutional, just, necessary and essential is in fact truly practiced every day in all aspects of our Lives, and especially at the level of the Couple, the Family and the Community.

 

May what we have established constitutionally, institutionally, become a reality at Home, in the Family, in Education, so that Children grow up with an Awareness of Rights, Respect, Harmony; and that every day we fight the Battles that may be necessary.

 

We live the Vigor and Glory of being Free, Sovereign, Honorable and Glorious, which signifies and dignifies the commitment to continue all the Battles in this very complex World, where Solidarity and Brother and Sisterhood among Peoples, in the Peace that of course we demand, for which we cry every day, our Peoples fight, so that this Unity is strengthened every day building the Future in the Always Further On, one of all the Truths, Merits, Freedoms, Dignities, of Justice, Security, and the Fundamental Right to Well-being.

 

Today we also celebrate the Life and Miracle of Fidel who belongs to the World... Fidel, Comandante of our Revolutions... Fidel, an Extraordinary Visionary who lives and animates the Strength of our Dignities and the Spirit of Victory at all times, above all by seeking via Fraternity and Solidarity, the Right of our Peoples to Live Well.
 

And with Fidel, prodigiously, in tremendous Harmony, the Great Diego of the World and of Our America-Caribbean, who honored us, our Blessed Nicaragua, with his Friendship, Comradeship, Solidarity and Affection. Maradona, the Great, the Immense, Always Great and Always Heroic and Victorious, who has the Admiration, Respect and Love of the whole World, and of the Youth, the Young People of the Planet.
 

Compañeras and Compañeros, also we have sent greetings today for the Victory of the Frente Amplio in Fraternal Uruguay. And our government has pronounced its greeting congratulating on their Victory, the Brothers and Sisters of the Frente Amplio of Uruguay, and the President and Vice President elected yesterday, Sunday, November 24th, Compañero Yamandú Orsi and Compañera Carolina Cosse.


 

We greet the Fraternal and Supportive Uruguayan People, with whom we have been united by bonds of Affection and Solidarity in Struggles for Justice.

In this New Period of the Frente Amplio, we wish the very Best for the Homes and Families of that Fraternal Country, as well as renewed Hopes for all its Young People, who legitimately aspire to realize their Dreams.

From this Nicaragua, Forever Blessed, Sovereign and Free, we reiterate the Commitment to work together for a Latin America and Caribbean strengthened in Union, given our historical ties in the Battles for Sovereign Dignity and a Full Life.

May the Times we are living in today open New Paths to continue forging the Future of Peace, Security, Rights, Well-being and Joy for Everyone.

Much Health and Success in your endeavors, and as always, our Respect and Admiration for the People of Uruguay.

Also on this Day of Anniversary we have sent our Greeting, always full of Love to the Historic Leader of the Cuban Revolution Comandante and Army General Raúl Castro Ruz, to the President of Cuba's Heroic People, Brother and Compañero Miguel Díaz-Canel, and we have said :

 

 

... Here is the clear
the endearing transparency
of their beloved presence
Comandante Che Guevara
Comandante Fidel!

Beloved Raul and Miguel :

To all of you, to all that Vibrant and Heroic People, to all of Cuba that we admire and love so much, both you Raúl, Comandante, you Miguel, President, and all Cuba's Families, Fidel's Family, Children, Grandchildren, Great-Grandchildren, our Love that is the Strength of our Revolutions, always giving thanks in recognizing Fidel's Vision and Light.

Here, eight years after that painful day, we meet him in all the Battles and all the Banners that we hold high, in the continuity of our Struggles.

What is it Fidel has,
that the Imperialists
can't cope with him?

Celebrating Fidel is to honor that Legacy of incredible Dignity, to honor his Optimism and to celebrate his Conviction of Victories.

Every day more and more Commitment and more and more Resolve in all the Ways that strengthen our Sovereign Honor. This is how Fidel lived!
