Reproductive Rights & Health:  Comparing the US and Nicaragua
 

Alliance for Global Justice

invite you to a

NICARAGUA WEBINAR

Reproductive Rights & Health:

Comparing the US and Nicaragua


Sunday, August 21st - 3pm Eastern, 2pm Central, 1pm Nicaragua, noon Pacific, 8pm Greenwich/UK

 

REGISTER

Please join us for a 75-minute dialogue with our featured speakers. 

 Camille Landry will depict current conditions in the US, where reproductive rights and healthcare are facing serious challenges, especially after the recent overturn of Roe v. Wade.  

Becca Renk will describe Nicaragua's law, public opinion and religious influence in that country.  Then she will discuss improvements in women's lives and gender equality in Nicaragua over the past 15 years.  The audience will have ample time to ask questions and offer comments!

Becca Renk has been a community development worker for over 20 years in Ciudad Sandino, Nicaragua, and a delegation coordinator through Casa Ben Linder, a solidarity project of the Jubilee House Community. 

Camille Landry is Outreach Director for Alliance for Global Justice's Lucy Parsons Popular Human Rights School.

Special thanks to Massachusetts Peace Action (MAPA) for hosting this webinar and providing technical assistance.

This event is part of a monthly webinar series on Nicaragua.  Additional organizational sponsors are welcome. Contact madeline@afgj.org to sign on
