Chomsky, Walker & Ellsberg: Defending Julian Assange

Submitted bytortilla onJue, 03/09/2020 - 19:45

Noam Chomsky, Alice Walker, and Daniel Ellsberg, preeminent experts on free speech and the fight to protect it, will discuss the persecution of Julian Assange just 2 days before his extradition hearing resumes in London. They will examine the implications of his indictment for journalism, etc. and explain why they have joined up as co-chairs of a new U.S. support group for Assange.

The event will be livestreamed at:
https://is.gd/CourageYouTube

Time: Saturday 5 September, 1:00 PM
Eastern Time (North America)


Background information : https://www.julian-assange.se/english/appeal.htm
