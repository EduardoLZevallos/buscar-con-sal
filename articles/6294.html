Nicaraguan government insists : Justice, Reparation, No Repetition

Submitted bytortilla onMar, 07/05/2019 - 14:25

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS


Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE DELEGATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF
RECONCILIATION AND NATIONAL UNITY (GRUN)
TO THE NEGOTIATION TABLE

TUESDAY, MAY 7, 2019

The Delegation of the GRUN to the Negotiation Table, in greeting all the Nicaraguan Families in this Month of Celebrations, Traditions and growing Identity and Dignity, reiterates to our People and to the International Community its Commitment to continue working for Justice, Reparation, and Non-Repetition, as a basis for Understanding, Reconciliation and Peace.

The Delegation of the GRUN reminds the Nicaraguan Families that based on their own experiences of conflict and suffering imposed on Nicaragua in the Past, experiences that, God willing, WILL NEVER RETURN, we are insistently demanding that justice be done for the Good People of Nicaragua. We are demanding that the damages inflicted on the Families, the Life, the National Economy and the Christian Commitment to Fight Against Poverty be recognized. We are calling for voices and efforts to unite in demanding the suspension and cessation of Unilateral, Arbitrary, Illegal and Unjust Sanctions, requested by a few wealthy and bad sons and daughters of Nicaragua, whom lacking any sense of Sensibility or Solidarity with their underprivileged brothers and sisters,  continue to invoke the economic destruction of Nicaragua.

We are not referring to Measures against Individuals, but to those that have been arbitrarily dictated, against the Nicaraguan people.

The Delegation of the GRUN to the Negotiation Table, reiterates to the Nicaraguan Families and the International Community, that the GRUN has ensured its Commitment to Peace, by its own will and privileging the Supreme Interest of Family Harmony and the Tranquility of the Nicaraguans, ensuring:

1. Release of 236 people linked to terrorist acts, that today are under the Family Coexistence Regime.

2. Commitment to release others, in coordination with the ICRC, in accordance with our Laws, and in compliance with the June 18 deadline.

3. Commitment on Constitutional Rights and Guarantees for all Nicaraguans.

4. Commitment on the Free and Safe Return of those who left the country, due to their involvement in violent coup actions.

5. Commitment to continue discussing in accordance with our Constitution, Legal Order and Laws, all the points agreed upon in the Agenda.

The Delegation of the GRUN at the Negotiation Table ratifies to the Nicaraguan Families and to the International Community that Justice, the Commitment on Reparation and Non-Repetition, are indispensable and unavoidable elements in any Negotiation Process.

It also reiterates that any Discussion, Agreement or Consensus must respect our National Dignity and be achieved through our Sovereignty, with the thoughts, acts and commitments of Nicaraguans.

Today, Tuesday, May 7, we are again at INCAE, as of 10 am, insisting on the Rights of the brave people of Nicaragua who want Peace, and demand the recognition of the universal pillars of Reconciliation, which comes through Justice, with Reparation and Commitment on Non-Repetition.

It is impossible to Forget. Forgiveness belongs to all Christians. To recognize is a sign of Human Greatness. To advance in Restoration and Union we must be able to put ourselves in the place of those who have lost so much, and recover the Confidence of all, crying out against the arbitrariness of the so-called Sanctions, in a single and dignified Voice, Nicaraguans by the Grace of God.

The Past will not return !
We have buried Hate !
Nicaragua Lives in Blessings and in Victories !

Managua, Tuesday, May 7, 2019

Delegation of the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
to the Negotiation Table
