Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 10/08/2024 - 14:13
NICARAGUA WEBINAR, SUNDAY AUGUST 11
Noon Pacific time / 1 pm Nicaragua /
3 pm Eastern / 8 pm Greenwich UK
 
REGISTER:  bit.ly/NicaAug11
 
 
Confirmed speakers:  Becca Renk, Natalia Burdynska Schuurman, Petros Bein, Brandy Gonzalez, Jason Cohen, and Yhamir Chabur.   The webinar will be in English only, but with an option for translated captions in Zoom.  
 
This event is hosted by Nicaragua Solidarity Coalition, which has a new website:  NicaSolidarity.com.
