Message to President Nicolás Maduro Moros

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 23/11/2023 - 19:11

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Managua, November 23, 2023

 

Compañero

Nicolás Maduro Moros

Constitutional President

of the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela


To your person

 

Dear Nicolás :

 

Today as we celebrate your Life, your birth to this Plane of Life with the transcendent mission of Universal Energy, Creative and Full of Purpose, on behalf of your Nation and your People, and for Our Great American and Caribbean Homeland, we greet you with our Fraternal Embrace, Heart to Heart, wishing you Health and Abundance, Creativity and continuing Strength, permanent lucidity towards the collective inheritance of the Wisdom accumulated through all the many Struggles and Battles in which our Peoples, and today each one of us, have engaged.

 

May the God of the Universe, the God of all Victories, accompany you, enlightening and encouraging you each and every day, with the Courage, Vitality and the Sacred Flame of the enormous, infinite Love, which moves us to advance, living and overcoming as we go.

 

With Cilia, your Family and your Great People, this day and every day, we salute the Joy and Privilege of making our way together, with Verve and Brightness, Honor and Glory, from our Deep Spirituality, towards Certain Victories as well as those in the process of being generated in each space of Historical Time, which we live to the full.

 

Content and proud to share as Comrades and Compañer@s in all the struggles, a Warm Embrace, still more Embraces and continued Strength to Overcome, building and growing in every moment of this innovative and enlightening journey.

 

Happy Anniversary in these Times of New Triumphs!

 

We will live and we will overcome!
