We defend our National Sovereignty and we say, it has been written in large letters throughout history. For a long time it was written with blood, now we want to write, Sovereignty: Work and Peace. Sovereignty: Education and Peace. Sovereignty: Family, Home and Community Values, Work and Peace, because Work, from Sovereignty and National Dignity, is Integrity and is Prosperity.

 

We are United in Victories, we are All Together, United to Prosper, to Progress. And above all, we are a United People that safeguards Mutual Understanding, Harmony, Tranquility and Peace as Treasure. They wanted to take away our peace, but They Could Not, Nor Could They!

 

Our People have the unwavering and in-bribable Conviction to Peace, to Tranquility, and above all, to look at each other as a Family, to Love Our Neighbor, to be genuinely Christians that live in Fraternity, a Brotherhood of Human Beings who believe in Jesus Christ. Every day we creates a Motherland that is more attached to that Faith, to that Trust in the Values ​​of Jesus Christ, because we know that without Faith, without that Trust in the Father, we are nothing.

 

We walk and transcend all circumstances because we have Faith, and because we know how to live as Families, filled with Faith, filled with Love, and with the commitment to continue creating that Bright Future, which we all want and that we all deserve.

 

Puppetry and puppets have nothing to do here... We must be aware that it is not with darkness that the day is reached, it is not with hatred that the day is built, it is not by selling our Country that we can made the Motherland.

 

We must be aware that it is by a Sun and under a Sun that does not decline, enlightened and inspired by the Heavenly Father, united in our Traditional Family Values, such as Homes, Unity of Everyone, and Together we say, Let's Go Forward!

 

An embrace to all the Nicaraguan Families who are living these Historic Days with Joy. We ratify and reconfirm that we are, and will continue to be, the People President. Here the People has the Power and a Government that lives in a Project and with a Program to Fight Poverty. Because we want to leave behind that Poverty that they have imposed on us, with which they have tried to dominate us... Leave it behind! Fight against Poverty and Prosperity for All. All Families working, studying and prospering.

 

Thank you, thank you, we say thank you to all Nicaraguans who embrace each other in New Times, in New Days, in a Culture of Peace and Good.
