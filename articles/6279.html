Nicaragua's government denounces opposition support for sanctions

Submitted bytortilla onLun, 06/05/2019 - 10:05

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS


Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE GOVERNMENT OF RECONCILIATION
AND NATIONAL UNITY (GRUN)
TO THE NEGOTIATION TABLE

Monday, May 6, 2019

The Delegation of the GRUN to the Negotiation Table greets this new week of May, with the Joy of the Renewal of Life, Nature, the Fields and the Nicaraguan Hearts that are filled with Faith and Hope.

We greet the Nicaraguan Families and the International Community with the firm Will and Commitment to continue advancing in the Discussions at the Negotiation Table regarding the implementation of the Agreements already taken and to soon  achieve, new Consensus, all in strict adherence to our Constitution and our Laws.

1. The Delegation of the GRUN reminds the Nicaraguan families that, as we have informed every day, progress has been made in:

a) 236 persons linked to the failed coup d'état unilaterally released. Today they live with their families as a result of the gesture GRUN.

b) Route of Compliance with the accompaniment of the ICRC for the release of persons who have committed crimes against the Public Order, in compliance with the Laws and the June 18 deadline.

c) Constitutional Guarantees for Citizen's Rights, Safety and Tranquility for all Nicaraguans.

Consensus is still expected for:

d) Proposal of the Nicaraguan State for the  Voluntary, Free and Safe Return of those who left the country after the turmoil and the attempted coup d'état, promoted since April 2018.

e) Proposal for the Participation of the Apostolic Nuncio, The Representative of His Holiness Pope Francis in Nicaragua, Monsignor Waldemar Stanislaw Sommertag; the Special Envoy of the Secretary General of the OAS, Mr. Luis Ángel Rosadilla; and the Secretary General of the SICA, Brother Vinicio Cerezo, former President of Guatemala, as International Guarantors for the implementation of the Agreements.

f) Call for the suspension and termination of Unilateral and Illegal Measures, known as Sanctions, which affect all Sectors of the Nicaraguan People.

We refer to the Nica Act and to other threats, coercive and restrictive measures that are opposite to International Law, Charters and Conventions.

Obviously, we are not referring to sanctions against private individuals who have been mentioned. None of the Nicaraguans included in these arbitrary and illegal measures, have accounts, properties or businesses in the United States, and therefore, these Sanctions, although they are totally unjust, are of no national or personal interest.

2. We once again denounce the persistent refusal of the Counterpart to the Table  to share this call for the suspension of the Unilateral and Illegal Economic Measures against the Nicaraguan People.

Let us remember that they are the same characters, who last year refused, again and again, to remove the "Roadblocks of Death", and are therefore identified by our people, as responsible and guilty of the crimes and the destructive actions committed during that period, in the name of "Democracy".

3. We reiterate the complaints that our people have made against these same people and empty acronyms, when during last years' Failed Theater on the month of May, refered to as the National Dialogue, and while lynchings were being staged, and under Blood and Fire, they called for  the National Police to be withdrawn.

We remember that as a result of this selfish and absurd imposition, the greatest outrages were committed, including killings, burnings, kidnappings, tortures, perversions and satanic and demonic rituals, in the best style of the Inquisition, invoking exorcist practices, with arrogance and disrespect toward the intelligence of all Nicaraguans.


4. We denounce once again to the World and, above all, to our People, the serious media machinations that  accompanied their practices of hatred and greed. With media fury and farce, they fabricated and published fake news and boasted, impudently, of their crimes, leaving indelible tracks of their filibuster, for the Shame of History and with the illusory pretense of forcing the Nicaraguan People to kneel, with ridiculous ultimatums.

5. We have denounced and continue to denounce to the World and on behalf of our People, all the perverse practices, humiliation, cruelty, degradation, racism and terrorist infamy that we faced, suffered, and defeated, during the failed attempted coup.

 


These coup practices were directed, financed, and coordinated very closely, by those who today, from the Table, intend to continue imposing their vices, forgetting that Nicaragua won, and Reason and Truth, will continue to win.

6. We make it clear, to the World and our People, that we will not give up in our demands for Reparations for all Nicaraguans affected by their greed, traps, lies, the destruction of Lives and our Economy, as well as for the extraterritorial, arbitrary and unjust economic actions against Nicaragua.

7. We also make it clear that the implementation of all the points already agreed upon in the Table must be agreed in strict adherence to our Constitution, our Laws, our Legal Order, and our Institutions of National Authority.

8. On the Road to the Bicentennial of the Independence of Nicaragua, in 2021, we ratify today that as a worthy and free Nation, we advance our Democracy, recognizing ourselves as Nicaraguans, that think and act as the daughters and sons of our Always Blessed and Free Homeland.

9. We continue to demand that a Climate of Confidence is indispensable for Negotiation to move away from repetitive, useless, ridiculous and unsuccessful provocations, which, with imported formats and manuals, constitute a threats, hostile and childish acts, which our People repudiate.

10. All the agreed upon points at the Table, remain there in order to continue working with a Sense of Justice for All, with a Sense of Motherland, Dignity and Identity, and above all with Love and Loyalty for our People.

In May, the Month of National Dignity, we reaffirm our consistent willingness to build Reconciliation, Understanding, Justice, Peace and Reparation for All, with Respect and Dignity for our Constitution, our Laws and Institutionality, and with the Victorious Fire of the Nicaraguan Soul that Loves Peace, Solidarity, Goodness and Faith.

Justice, Reparation and Non Repetition!
We forgive, but we do not Forget!
Reconciliation, Justice and Peace!

Managua, Monday May 6, 2019

Delegation of the Government of
Reconciliation and National Unity
to the Negotiation Table
