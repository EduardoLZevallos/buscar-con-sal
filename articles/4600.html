ROSARIO: "Here Our Nation Lives Blessed and Forever Free! Sandino Lives On Here, because the Struggle Continues, Sandino Lives On...! Here we are Revolution, our Proud Sandinista People's Revolution"

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 12/07/2024 - 18:31

Initial comments by Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua
in her remarks for the Midday Edition of Multinoticias, Channel 4

July 12th 2024

Very good afternoon, Compañera. Good afternoon, Beloved Families, in this joyful celebration of Peace, in these Times of Peace and Goodness, in these New Times of Affection, Understanding, of the Union of all Nicaraguan Families, such that we want to move onward and have the Right to move onward, we want to work securely, we want to prosper.

 

And we know very well now, Compañeras and Compañeros, those who have not wanted Progress for our People... We are well aware now of the people who have desired Evil, at all times, and still desire Evil: The embittered, those who have no Life of their own, those who look like ghosts of themselves, decrepit, alienated by their own vices, eaten away by selfishness.

 

They are the mediocre people who live off of others who pay them to hear their hollow shells echo, to watch them strip off their vices, making even Darkness go out, as if they were nobody, because they are nobody, or as if they were nothing, because they are nothing. They sold their Soul to the devil, and today, bungling, lackluster, their Souls out of date, they cover up their cowardice, their hesitations, their betrayals in sacrilegious smoke and mirrors.. Mercenary traitors!

 

What are they, asked the great Carlos Martínez Rivas, what are they, my beloved? The dead, living dead, burying other living dead, digging them up only to rebury them!

 

That's how they have ended up, adrift, unsuccessful, defeated, lifeless... They are a culture of zombies, freaks and bugaboos, ugly halloween monsters, replete with Evil eating away at them and it shows in their faces.

They are shadows, mere shades, nothing but shadows. They are leftovers, dregs of empire... Nothing but shadows and Empire's leftovers.

 

While here, in the midst of all this Celebration of Peace and the Joy of Living in Peace :

 

Who Lives...?

Our Nation...!

 

Who Lives...?

Hope and Joy...!

 

Who Lives...?

Light and the True Truth...!


Who Lives...?

Sandino, General of Free Men and Women...!

 

Who Lives...? Who Lives...? Who Lives...?

The Revolution, the Sandinista People's Revolution, in Permanent Evolution!

 

As we know already, traitors are doomed to burn in the flames of their own Hell. We already know that. Here Our Nation Lives Blessed and Forever Free! Sandino Lives On Here, because the Struggle Continues, Sandino Lives On...! Here we are Revolution, our Proud Sandinista People's Revolution, and I say it again: The traitors, along with their masks, are condemned to burn in the flames of their own Hell. In the meantime, Here We Go Onward!
