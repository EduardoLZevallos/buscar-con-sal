Easter Message - Yours be the Glory over all the Earth

Submitted bytortilla onDom, 04/04/2021 - 13:42

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

YOURS BE THE GLORY
OVER ALL THE EARTH

Nothing can, and nothing will, stop the Victory of Jesus Christ.

He is not here; he has risen, just as he said. Come and see the place where he lay. Matthew 28:6

Dear Nicaraguan Families:

On this Weekend of Glory and Victories of Life reborn, because we are reborn with Christ, to Hope, to Easter that announces and sings of Love, Solidarity, Fraternity, and the Triumphs of Wise and Serene Surrender to the Will of God, whom we embrace, in Sincere Conviction of Resurrection of all the is Good corresponds to us to strengthen in each one of us and all the best that we can build together, celebrating gratefully the Gifts of the Lord; Health and the Strength, as well as Humility for our undefeated and invincible Spirit.

On this Easter Sunday, which is of Resurrection, which is of permanent Hope that no one can take from us, we promise to continue promoting Concord, Well-being, Rights, accompanying us from our valuable and courageous Culture, our Ways of Life, our Faith, our Languages, Traditions, Customs, our Joy of Home, Family and Community, and our unwavering determination of Dignity, assuming who we are, what we do, and what we want to be, in a World where we know ourselves to be privileged and proud Defenders of Life, in all of its forms, and of the Family that coexists, because we coexist, with the Virtuous and Powerful Force of Love, Hope and Peace.

Resurrection Sunday... Easter Sunday of Florida... Sunday for Union, Anointment, always invoking the Most High, Our Mother Mary, fully living all the Commandments of our Faith, as we have learned and practiced throughout our Lives, trying to love God above all things, and our neighbor as we do ourselves.

In Love for God and Love among ourselves, today on Easter Sunday of Resurrection, we decree ourselves, in the Powerful Name of Jesus, faithful, devout and modest practitioners of Affection, Peace and Solidarity; faithful custodians of our Family Values, of our Ideals, of our Labor for a Better World, with Respect, Dignity, Rights and of course, in growing Faith and permanent Evolution.

We already own that Better World of Justice, Freedoms, of Leadership and Complementary Democracy, of Work and Well-being for All; of fraternal, lasting and sustainable Sharing Relationships between one another, all Living Beings, and with our Mother Earth that shelters and nourishes us, and that we must take care of and save.

In Mysterious and Victorious Forces, Forces of Life, Brotherhood, Peace, Solidarity, and Deserved Prosperity, we gather joyfully and confident that the Eternal Truths have found us, encourage us, motivate us, drive us to be as God commands, His Creatures, Faithful, the Human Family and Community that Overcomes.

“My heart, Oh God, is steadfast, my heart is steadfast; I will sing and make music... For great is your love, reaching to the heavens; your faithfulness reaches to the skies. Be exalted, Oh God, above the heavens; let your glory be over all the earth”. Psalm 57: 1-2, 2, 7, 10-11.

Managua, April 3, 2021
