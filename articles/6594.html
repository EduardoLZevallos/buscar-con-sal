Government reinforces paths to Reconciliation and Peace

Submitted bytortilla onMar, 04/06/2019 - 14:51

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS

 

Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE DELEGATION OF THE GRUN
FOR UNDERSTANDING AND PEACE IN NICARAGUA

Tuesday, June 4, 2019

“O praise the Lord, all ye nations;
 praise him, all ye people.

 For his merciful kindness is great toward  us;
 and the truth of the Lord forever endured.
 Alleluia.”

 (From Psalm 117)

With the Firmness of Blue and White Victory of Motherland and of the Liberating Revolution that celebrates 40 Years of Fervor and Consequence, We Move Forward!

With the unwavering Spirit and Victorious Conviction of Freedom, Dignity and Fraternity, of a Free, Sovereign, Independent and Always Blessed Motherland, Proud of all her children and their Potential for Peace, Hope and Affection, We Move Forward!

We continue working toward all the Commitments assumed for the Nicaraguan People and Families. We continue to strengthen all the Paths so that Reconciliation, Coming Together and the Common Good, may be realized.

We advance daily with:

    1. Guaranteeing the agreed upon releases may be responsibly concluded by June 18.

    2. Guaranteeing the implementation of the Culture of Peace from all the Commissions that are functioning promoting Harmonious Understanding and Coexistence throughout the Country.

    3. Guaranteeing the Safe Return, and the Social and Family Reintegration of Nicaraguans who wish to return to their homes, their families, their work, and who are already doing so, as can be verified in the different Municipalities, Neighborhoods and Communities.

    4. Guaranteeing the development of all the Programs derived from the Law for the Comprehensive Plan of Attention to Victims, coordinated by the Human Rights Ombudsman and its different Directorates.

    We have completed the Records of the Families that suffered all kinds of inhuman, cruel, humiliating and destructive treatment, as a result of the hate crimes committed during the failed attempted Coup d’état.

    5. Continuing with the strengthening of our Democracy, according to our Constitution and Laws.

    All of us born in this Blessed and Free Motherland are obliged to know and respect our Constitution, our Laws and the Institutions of our Republic.

    6. Continuing with the strengthening of Electoral Democracy and Institutions, so that we may organize and carry out National Elections in 2021, according to the standards recognized by the Specialized Systems of the World.

    7. Guaranteeing all the rights contemplated in the Constitution, from Citizen Security, to the mobility that is essential for our Life, Work, Creative Economy, Commerce, Education, Recreation, Social, Cultural, Religious, Family and Community Activity, according to our personal and Collective experience.  

    8. Promoting the active Protagonism of Producers, Cooperatives, Traders, Entrepreneurs, Business Leaders and Workers in all areas of our economy, with the Rights guaranteed by our Constitution.

    9. Ensuring that everyone contributes to the recovery of our economy severely impacted by the failed coup. With responsibility and Commitment, all Nicaraguans must contribute to restore the indispensable Stability to restore the Good Path that Nicaragua, in Christianity and Solidarity, was undertaking before the terrorist cruelty of April.

    10. We continue to express the rejection of the majority of the Nicaraguan People against the Unilateral, Illegal and Coercive Political and Economic Measures that affect our Rights as a People and as honest and hardworking Families, committed to Continue Moving Forward and Advancing, Enterprising, Learning and Prospering.

    11. The Social, Economic, Productive, Commercial, Labor and Business Activity is developed with respect among us all and in primarily toward the Constitution and the Laws of our Nicaragua.

    We live in an inclusive and open society in which everyone is a Protagonist, and that with enormous vitality is advancing, Cheerful and Hopeful, for a future of Fraternity, Social Justice and Shared Progress.

Every day we are happier and prouder of our Achievements toward the strengthening of Democracy and Freedoms and above all for the Peace that the Majorities in Nicaragua support. Because we need it, and we demand it as a Basic Human Right of the peoples of this Heroic, Beautiful, Noble, Always Blessed and Always Free Motherland.
With the Strength of our Christian Values ​​of Faith, Family and Community, we care for and treasure our ways of Life, Generous, Good and in Solidarity.

“O praise the Lord, all ye nations;
 praise him, all ye people.

 For his merciful kindness is great toward us;
 and the truth of the Lord forever endured.
 Alleluia.”

(From Psalm 117)

Tuesday, June 4, 2019
