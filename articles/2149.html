Webinar - "Why Is Nicaragua Important in Today's World?" July 23rd 2023

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 21/07/2023 - 09:16

 

Nicaragua Webinar, Sunday, July 23:

 

"Why Is Nicaragua Important in Today's World?"

 

Noon Pacific time/ 1pm Nicaragua/ 3pm Eastern/ 8pm Greenwich UK

 

 

 

 Register:  bit.ly/NicaJuly23          

 

 

Please join us to hear from delegations visiting Nicaragua for the 44th anniversary of the Revolution.  They will offer firsthand observations of: 

~ The July 19 festivities 

~ Tours of hospitals & clinics 

~ Visits to renewable energy & agroecology projects 

~ Interchanges with Afro-descendant and indigenous communities 

~ Meetings with government officials 

~ More!  

They will share why they believe it's important to learn from Nicaragua today.

 

 

 

Grateful thanks to our co-sponsors, who help spread the word about our webinars!  If your organization would also like to co-sponsor, email Barbara@NicaSolidarity.net .
