Message to Senegal's President Elect

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 26/03/2024 - 18:50

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Managua, March 26, 2024

Excellency
Mr. Bassirou Diomaye Faye
President Elect
of the Republic of Senegal
Dakar

Dear President,

On behalf of the Heroic and Free People of our Nicaragua, of the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity and in our own behalf, we wish to express our most sincere Congratulations to you for your Electoral Victory in the General Elections of the Republic of Senegal.

Our Peoples have fought heroically against colonial imposition, raising the flags of Independence, National Sovereignty and Self-Determination, today we celebrate this triumph for the Senegalese people and for the African Peoples.

We also recognize the Valuable Principles of Solidarity of the Senegalese People in the Respect for the Exercise of the Inalienable Rights of the Palestinian People to Self-Determination and Freedom.

We shall not forget the special days of the Year 2007, when during our visit to your Country, we were able to know more of your Fighting Spirit and of your famous Literature and Art that so proudly represents Brother Léopold Sédar Senghor, whose magnificent work constitutes a Legacy for the Heritage of Senegal and of the Human Family.

From our Nicaragua, Always Blessed and Always Free, we send our Fraternal Greetings, with the will to continue strengthening the ties of Friendship that unite our Peoples and Governments and to continue working for the Development, Prosperity and Welfare of our Peoples.
