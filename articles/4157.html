Message to President Vladimir Putin

Enviado por tortilla el Lun, 06/05/2024 - 13:17

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Managua, May 6th 2024

Compañero
Vladimir Putin
President of the Russian Federation
In Person

Beloved Brother President :

In these Days of Celebration of the Victories of the Soviet People and their Red Army, of the Government and the Rulers of the Soviet Union, we greet you and with you all that Heroic People, and in particular the millions of Families who constructed the Triumph with the Sacred Blood of their Loved Ones, whom today we bless in the name of Humanity.

Brother President Vladimir Putin :

Today you begin a New Period of Government, one of continuous Struggle against Fascism, Nazism and all the deformations that derive from that destructive and lethal Ideology. As we greet your Inauguration as the President that the Russian People have elected and acclaimed for these New Times, we wish you Great Triumphs in the Struggle that the Russian Federation is waging today for a New World, without absolute dominions, without slaves, a World of Brother and Sisterhood and Cooperation, by Everyone and for the Sake of Everyone.

May God and all the Divinities of the Universe bless you, protect you, and strengthen all your efforts in Supreme and Superior Union with the Free Peoples of the World, who wish for Peace, Goodwill and the total cessation of every aggression oppressing, exposing and threatening the Human Family, and our survival.

Health, Strength and ever more Spiritual Energy to Continue Overcoming and continue promoting Life in Peace, Joy, Rights and Respect.
