Webinar - Nicaragua's Disaster Prevention and Mitigation, October 26th 2025

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 23/10/2025 - 15:13
Nicaragua Solidarity Coalition, October 23rd 2025
 

Learn about Nicaragua's renowned SINAPRED program. Discover how Nicaragua saves lives before, during and after disasters, and how nationwide drills train more than a million citizens to respond to disasters and mitigate their worst effects.

Speaker: Coleen Littlejohn, International Development Economist.

REGISTER:  bit.ly/NicaOct26
