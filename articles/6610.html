Government promotes an inclusive and open society in which everyone is a Protagonist

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS

 

Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE DELEGATION OF THE GRUN
FOR UNDERSTANDING AND PEACE IN NICARAGUA
 

Wednesday, June 5, 2019

In these days We commemorate multiple Revolutionary Celebrations, Celebrations of Life in Values of Freedom ​​and absolute Conviction of Victories of the brave and unwavering Nicaraguan people.

We celebrate the Life of hundreds of Nicaraguans, whose Liberating Blood, redeemed Nicaragua from the crimes and abuses of the Somoza Dictatorship, imposed on Nicaragua for 45 Years, by Interventionist Policies.

We commemorate the Heroism, the Strength and the Irrevocable Decision of the Nicaraguan Fighters and of an entire People that revolted against injustice with infinite Spiritual Force. A People that knew how to fight Prolific and Glorious Battles, that are a reflection of the mobilizing Faith of Energies committed to a Free, Sovereign, Independent and Dignified Motherland in Christian Solidarity.

The Delegation of the GRUN at the Negotiation Table confirms, in that very Spirit of Liberty, Dignity and Fraternity, to the Nicaraguan Families and the International Community, that we move forward fulfilling our Commitments that are Sacred and Unbreakable:

    1. By June 18 we will have completed the Release Processes.

    2. We continue to develop all the Processes related to the fostering of a Culture of Peace, working together from the Commissions for Reconciliation and Harmonious Coexistence, throughout the Territory.

    3. We continue guaranteeing the implementation of the Plans for Safe Return, and Social and Family Reintegration of those who are returning to their Homes, Families and Communities, and to the Good Life that our Nicaragua Advances, prioritizing Understanding and Harmony.

    4. We continue with the Plans and Programs contemplated in the Law for the Integral Attention of Victims from the Human Rights Ombudsman's Office.

    We are attending, without discrimination, all the Brothers and Sisters and Families who were Victims of Terrorism and the Crimes of hatred, cruel, humiliating, inhuman and destructive treatment, which characterizes it.

    5. All of us who were born and live in this Dignified, Blessed and Always Free Motherland have the Duty to know and respect our Constitution, Laws and Institutions that make up the Republic.

    Democracy is strengthened through Respect for our Magna Carta.

    6. We continue to strengthen Democracy and Electoral Institutions by guaranteeing the preparation and celebration of the 2021 National Elections, in accordance with the Norms established by the Specialized Systems of the World.

    7. We guarantee all the rights contemplated in the Constitution, from Citizen Security, to the mobility that is essential for our Life, Work, Creative Economy, Commerce, Education, Recreation, Social, Cultural, Religious, Family and Community Activity, according to our personal and Collective experience.  

    8. We promote the active Protagonism of Producers, Cooperatives, Traders, Entrepreneurs, Business Leaders and Workers in all areas of our economy, with the Rights guaranteed by our Constitution.

    9. We ensure the contribution of everyone to the recovery of our economy severely impacted by the failed coup. With responsibility and Commitment, all Nicaraguans must contribute to restore the indispensable Stability to restore the Good Path that Nicaragua, in Christianity and Solidarity, was undertaking before the terrorist cruelty of April.

    10. We continue to express the rejection of the majority of the Nicaraguan People against the Unilateral, Illegal and Coercive Political and Economic Measures that affect our Rights as a People and as honest and hardworking Families, committed to Continue Moving Forward and Advancing, Enterprising, Learning and Prospering.

    11. The Social, Economic, Productive, Commercial, Labor and Business Activity is developed with respect among us all and in primarily toward the Constitution and the Laws of our Nicaragua.

We live in an inclusive and open society in which everyone is a Protagonist, and that with enormous vitality is advancing, Cheerful and Hopeful, for a future of Fraternity, Social Justice and Shared Progress.

Every day we strengthen Democracy, Freedom and above all the Peace that the Majorities in Nicaragua support and need. Security and Peace are indispensable Human Rights for Life in this Nicaragua of Family Affection, Blessings and Victories.

With the Strength of our Christian Values of Faith, Family and Community, we care for and treasure our ways of Life, Generous, Good and in Solidarity.

Wednesday, June 5, 2019

Delegation of the GRUN
for Understanding and Peace in Nicaragua
