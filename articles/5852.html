Guarimbas: Managers of Chaos excerpts; upcoming Delegations

Full President Maduro year-end interview with Ignacio Ramonet:
“In Venezuela, the future of progressive movements in the world, of revolutionary movements in the world, is being decided.”

Raul Capote: Guarimbas: Managers of Chaos excerpts from Chapter one and two of his book. Capote delves into the various behind the scenes operations the US employs to try to overthrow governments it considers a problem for its interests.

Peoples Dispatch: China donates 70 tons of equipment to Cuba to restore its electric system China has donated almost 70 tons of power generator parts and accessories to Cuba. The shipment is part of China’s second assistance package in 2024 to help restore Cuba’s electricity generation capacity to around 400 megawatts. In recent months, the Chinese government has donated various components for the installation of photovoltaic parks (renewable energies based on sunlight). More than 95% of Cuba’s power generation comes from fossil fuels, while only 5% comes from renewable sources. 

TeleSur: Venezuela Rejects Lawfare Action Against Nicaraguan Government The Ministry of Foreign Affairs rejected the aggression of Argentine federal judge Ariel Lijo against President Daniel Ortega and high-ranking officials of the Nicaraguan government. Venezuela described the action as “a clear lawfare operation designed to attack a legitimate and sovereign government.” Such maneuvering violates the fundamental principles of sovereignty and non-interference in the internal affairs of States enshrined in international law. “We reiterate our unwavering solidarity with the people and government of Nicaragua, led by President Daniel Ortega and Vice President Rosario Murillo in their struggle for sovereignty, social justice and self-determination.”
