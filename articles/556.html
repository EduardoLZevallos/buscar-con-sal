Greetings to the 26th Congress of the Communist Party of Ireland

Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 17/09/2022 - 20:28

26 NATIONAL CONGRESS
OF THE COMMUNIST PARTY OF IRELAND (CPI)
DUBLIN, IRELAND, 17TH – 18TH SEPTEMBER, 2022

Dear Colleague Eugene McCartan
Secretary General
of the Communist Party of Ireland

Dear Comrades,

Receive from the Sandinista National Liberation Front (FSLN), the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity (GRUN), our President and Secretary General of the FSLN, Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra and our Vice President, Comrade Rosario Murillo, our Fraternal Greetings with the best spirit of Unity, Solidarity and Revolutionary Commitment.

We extend our warmest congratulations to the Communist Party of Ireland, on reaching the 101ST Anniversary of its historic founding, forged in the midst of the struggle for Independence and then the counterrevolutionary war, sponsored from abroad by the imperialist powers.

In commemorating this Anniversary, the People of Nicaragua and the FSLN are proud to recognize and celebrate the memory and historical legacy of all the Heroes, Heroines and Martyrs of the Communist Party of Ireland (CPI), who made the maximum sacrifice in favor of the People and the Motherland. Honor and Glory to each of them!

We salute this 26TH National Congress of the Communist Party of Ireland, which brings together Comrades that are committed to the cause of anti-imperialist struggle, the cause of the Free Peoples of the World, who demand to live in Peace, with Social Justice, Equity and with Rights. The just cause that today leads us all towards a new multipolar, anti-imperialist world order, in which there is no room for the abuses of savage capitalism, an exploitative and enslaving model that only offers poverty, conflict, chaos and crisis.

We join each of you, as you consider a Political Resolution and a new Constitution. Know, Dear Comrades, that the best wishes of the FSLN accompany you in your deliberations and decisions, contributing from our own trenches, to build that great "Wall of Solidarity" that unites us and strengthens us, in the daily struggle for more victories for our Peoples.

Nicaragua currently celebrates the Month of the Nicaraguan and Central American Motherland, the Month of our First Independence, when we commemorate the 201ST Anniversary of the defense of our National sovereignty, a victory achieved against the same colonial, neo-colonial, imperialists and supremacists powers of always, who seek to subjugate the Free Peoples of this World.

We advance with invariable determination and Revolutionary Conviction, and with the certainty that Unity and Solidarity among Peoples, strengthens us, in our permanent search for Peace, Love and Prosperity.

We know, Comrades, that the World has changed and that hegemony and imperialism have failed in the face of the awakening of Free Peoples, who reject subordination, enslavement and domination.

From our Nicaragua, Blessed and Always Free, that Never Surrenders or Gives in! We express our Respect, Affection and Fraternal Solidarity; we continue to advance with you, fighting for the Anti-Imperialist Cause, the cause of Freedom, National Sovereignty, Self-determination, Independence and Social Justice of our Peoples.

Sandinista National Liberation Front (FSLN)
