ROSARIO: "We all know how necessary a new Maritime Route is, and Nicaragua has always, in history, in our history, met the best conditions for this Project, so necessary as we said and so important for the world"

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 19/11/2024 - 18:31

Opening remarks by Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua,

in the Midday Edition of Multinoticias on Canal 4


November 19th 2024
 

A very good afternoon, Compañera, and a very good afternoon Beloved Families of this Blessed, Prodigious, Prestigious Nicaragua, Forever Free, Dignified and Sovereign... Full of love!

 

This is a Nicaragua where we are living through Miraculous Times, grateful to God, because He gives us these Times that we live through with fulfilment, with our Hearts full of Recognition, and also, living out, defending, protecting the Essential Heritage of our People, of our Nicaraguan Families: Peace and the Right to Well-being, the Right to escape from Poverty.

 

Last night, at that vibrant Business Summit between China and our Great, Latin American and Caribbean Nation, first, we saluted that Special Event, which shows the Spiritual Greatness, the Human Greatness of the People's Republic of China, which seeks, forges and develops Alliances with the Peoples of the World, creating that Community of a Shared Future.

 

And in that scenario of Alliances for Security, for Peace and for Equitable Development, so as to advance against Poverty, our Comandante Daniel presented the Canal Project for Nicaragua... The best possible moment because we all know how necessary a new Maritime Route is, and Nicaragua has always, in history, in our history, met the best conditions for this Project, so necessary as we said and so important for the world.

 

A necessary, essential project, in a Country that possesses and brings together every Strength: The Strengths that God has given us, the Strengths that we ensure with all our Soul, the Strengths that we know are ours, the Strengths that define us, as a Country, as a People, as Nicaraguan Families.

 

Necessary, essential projects for Peace, for Security and for the Fight against Poverty: The Canal through Nicaragua on the route that Comandante Daniel presented at this Great Summit of Hope, truly a Summit of Hope, a Summit meeting which allows us to go on realizing our Dreams.

 

Also this morning, in these Tremendous, Miraculous Times, our Comandante Daniel has sent to the National Assembly of Nicaragua a Reform to the Constitution of the Republic. For what? To strengthen the Rights of the Nicaraguan People, the Democratic Leadership of the Nicaraguan People, who are, first of all, the Owners of our Nicaragua, the Owners of our History, thanks to the Direct Democratic Leadership of Nicaragua's Families, who ensure Peace, ensure Stability, ensure Work for Prosperity.

 

And we are, we repeat, the Owners of our Land and of our History, and we deserve to live in calm, in security, in stability, working to prosper, with all our Rights recognized and validated in a Constitution that represents the Values and Courage of the Nicaraguan People, a People that truly deserves the best, the places and positions in the Glory and Victories of Hope and of the Certainties we have been building.

 

We are Going Onward, Always Further On, protecting all our Rights, validating all our Rights, knowing that the Future is being built by us All Together, in Unity for the Common Good, in Brother and Sisterhood, in Solidarity, as a Christian, Socialist, Solidarity inspired People... the People-President!
