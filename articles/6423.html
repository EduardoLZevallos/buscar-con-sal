Cuba Update 18 May 2019

Hear from Cuban medical brigade volunteer and join the Hands Off Cuba contingent on the anti Trump protest

Dr Enmanuel Vigil Fonseca, a member of Cuba’s Henry Reeve Medical emergency brigade will be speaking at public events this June. Dr Vigil has volunteered in Venezuela, Ecuador, Peru, Haiti and the Western Sahara, and was one of the 250 Cuban doctors to work in West Africa during the 2014 Ebola epidemic.

Hear Dr Vigil speak this June:

Saturday 8 June, 10.30am-4.30pm
CSC AGM, Hamilton House, London, WC1H 9BD

Enmanuel will join our other guest speakers, including Cuban Ambassador HE Teresita Vicente, in a very special AGM to celebrate the 60th anniversary of the Cuban Revolution. The Cuba60 exhibition will also be shown at the event, a film on Cuban medical brigades, and all attendees will receive a special limited edition Cuba60 commemorative lanyard.

• This is a free public event open to all. Full timings and details here

He will also be speaking at public meetings in Norwich, Brighton, Leicester, Birmingham and Manchester, and CSC fringe meetings at Unison and Unite National Conferences.

• Full details here

 

RMT Garden Party for Cuba

Wednesday 12 June 2019, 7pm til late
Maritime House, Clapham, London SW4 0JW

Celebrate and show solidarity with Cuba alongside friends and activists from the labour and trade union movement with live music, buffet, free bar, guest speakers and the chance to win two return flight tickets to Cuba in a special raffle.

The line up will include superb Cuban salsa band, Raíces Cubanas, led by Afro-Cuban jazz violinist Omar Puente. Plus a special set from the Alabama 3 – with their politically fiery brand of “country acid house” music.

Special guest speakers will include Dr Enmanuel Vigil Fonseca, Cuban internationalist medical brigade volunteer, and others from across the UK trade union and labour movement.

Tickets only £15 include entry, live music food and free bar

Join the Hands Off Cuba contingent on the march against Trump

Tuesday 4 June, 11am, Trafalgar Square

Join with us in the Trump – Hands off Cuba contingent at the Together against Trump protest from 11am on Tuesday 4 June. We will assemble in Trafalgar Square and march to wherever Trump is meeting to let him know loud and clear ‘Trump – Hands off Cuba!’
