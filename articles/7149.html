Webinar July 27th - Nicaragua's tourism model benefitting communities and travelers

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 25/07/2025 - 05:25

Nicaragua Solidarity Coalition, July 24th 2025
https://www.nicasolidarity.com/


REGISTER:  bit.ly/NicaJuly27
Join us to hear about the development of family- and community-based tourism in Nicaragua, and how it contrasts with other travel of today and of past decades.  Becca Renk of Casa Benjamin Linder will lead a discussion with recent delegation members Geraldine Cawthorne, Ken Yale, Gayle Nielsen, and Larry Fisk.  They will describe their recent travel under the current model, compared to past experiences.
