President Comandante Daniel's message to the Meeting of Majority World Political Parties

Enviado por tortilla el Mié, 15/03/2023 - 10:20

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Nicaragua Triumphs


Brother President of the People's Republic of China
and Secretary General of the Communist Party of China, Comrade and Friend Xi Jinping

Brother Presidents, Secretary Generals and Delegates of the Political Parties
of Our America and the Caribbean, Africa, Asia, and the World

Brothers and Comrades all:

The Neoliberal Model of alleged domination and submission of the Peoples of the World, is exhausted.

Our Peoples say so and so says the intelligence that mobilizes our Parties and Political and Social Movements, that fight everywhere, demanding a World free of absurd claims of subjugation, oppression, injustice and discord, with which they sow terror, insecurity and chaos, to prolong division and its consequent fragility, in the face of their obsessions for power and their terrible and inhuman consequences.

Faced with the abominable panorama of insecurity that generates wars, and their imperial, colonialist and neocolonialist stubbornness; the unstoppable Rebellion and Heroism of our Peoples continues to emerge, fighting to assert our Rights, Freedoms, Sovereignties, Cultures, and Social and Political Models, freely chosen in each place.

The World that we want to create must be for Peace, Understanding, Fraternal Understanding, Solidarity, Cooperation and Coexistence as a Human Community, which shares the advances of Science and Technology and is aimed at promoting Justice and Equitable Development, that is, the Good for All.

The commitment and responsibility of the People's Republic of China and the Communist Party of China, of You, Comrade Xi Jinping, has been admirable, coming nearer to our Countries and Peoples with respect; seeking the essential communications and constructive coordination to continue advancing in Cooperation for Work, Wellbeing, Prosperity and Security of All.

The Sandinista National Liberation Front, after decades of Guerrilla Fighting with relentless battles against the imperialist enemy that intervened in Nicaragua, occupied and murdered thousands, achieved the Triumph of the Sandinista Popular Revolution on 19th July, 1979.

Our Revolution represents the Courage and Dignity of so many Brothers and Sisters who with heroism and determination have known how to expel the invading imperialists and colonialists, and today, faced with the same interventionist and intervening policies, in both different and very similar modalities and forms, continues to wage all the necessary and indispensable struggles to defend Peace and the Right to live as we deserve, with Respectful, Fraternal Relationships, Sincere and based on Solidarity, with all the Peoples of the World and in particular with the Countries and Governments with whom we share Ideals and Values regarding our Independence, Sovereignties and Right to Security and Peace.

In this sense, our Nicaragua, which continues to fight against imperial domination, fully supports all the efforts of the People's Republic of China, the Communist Party of China, of the Heroic Chinese People, in each initiative that promotes civilizational growth and joint learning; fulling assuming our Rights and Freedoms, participating in all Just Causes and in everything that defines us as what we are: Industrious, Brave, Courageous and Noble Peoples, steadfast and consistent Defenders of Peace, Justice and Development, in a Multipolar, Respectful World of recognized and considered Cultures, that advances towards more and more integration of thoughts and ways of life that strengthens our commitment to Unity, Civilization and Prosperity.

The Sandinista National Liberation Front proudly participates in this important event and thanks the Initiative and the Call of the Brother President and Secretary General, Xi Jinping, to be permanently promoting forms of communication and efforts like this; to oppose domination, unipolarity and injustice, and to continue sharing languages and actions that assert these Paths of Encounter, Brotherhood and Unity for the Common Good.

We support and endorse the Proposals that you present to us, and with full conviction to continue working together for Life, Justice and Peace; we congratulate ourselves on our relationships, attunement and identification of our Parties and Peoples in all Forums and International Events, where we prioritize Light, Life and Truth.

In conclusion, Comrade and Friend Xi Jinping, and Brothers and Sisters of the Parties of the World, allow us to congratulate You, Your Party and Your People, with admiration and affection, for your re-election as head of the Presidency of the People's Republic of China.

Always and Beyond!
An Embrace from the Nicaraguan People!

Managua, Nicaragua
15th March, 2023


Daniel Ortega Saavedra
President of the Republic
and Secretary General
of the Sandinista National Liberation Front
