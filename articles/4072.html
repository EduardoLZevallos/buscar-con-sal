POWERFUL LOVE, LOVE ABOVE ALL

Remarks by Compañera Rosario Murillo

Midday, April 25th 2024

Today we are at April 25, April, this April like every month of April, Victorious, Victorious... April of Victorious Suns... This April is another April of Great Achievements, of a Day by Day Culture of Struggles and Triumphs, of Great Advances in the indispensable, absolutely Essential, Serene and Luminous Recognition of Peace, as our Common Good, as our Heroic Heritage and as a Path of Plenitude and Rights. With the Peace of all Times and which we have been able to protect at all Times, we are creating the New Realities of Well-being, of Life with Well-being, of Joy, in the Lives of Nicaragua’s Families.

In these Times of True Victories, we are preparing to continue paying Tribute to all our Glorious Combatants, Heroic Fighters, Combatants of all Time. Because Nicaragua has had and has now Children who love her, and that is precisely why we are Free and will never be Slaves again.

Every Dawn, New Challenges, New Tasks Ahead, New Challenges and New Tasks that we take on with the Courage and the Undefeated Soul of our Nicaraguan Identity... Pride in Being Nicaraguan! That pride we have of being as we are, as we continue to be, Valiant but above all In Love with our Nicaragua.

We are heading towards May, the Month of Dignity and National Sovereignty... And we are heading towards that May of so many Blooms... Month of Rains, Month of Holy Waters, Month of Flowers, Month of Mothers, Powerful Loves... Month of so much Traditional Festivity, celebrating Mother Earth and celebrating us in Praises, Songs, Creative Ways demonstrating the Great Power of God, and Faith... The Great Power of God, and of that Faith which animates us, motivates us and claims us for the Great Truths of the Universe... Love above all things!

From May until 45/19, Everyone, One and All, we are recalling the Exploits, creating New Glory and more Honor, more and more Love, more and more Color, in this Sacred Geography of Darío, of Andrés, of Zeledón, of Sandino, of Carlos, of Tomás, of Daniel.

Always Further On, this is how We Go Onward, telling ourselves, repeating to ourselves, that it is Always Possible to Go Further On with more Effort, more Heart, more Conviction, more Determination and, above all, more Union.

Yesterday we were listening to the Wonderful Messages of the Heads of State and Government of the ALBA of the Peoples, the Vision, the Commitment, the Union, which we must continue to forge so as to be able to advance along the Paths of Peace, consolidating Peace, in permanent vindication of our Paths of Brother and Sisterhood, Solidarity, Complementarity, thanks to the indispensable Culture of Peace, which makes us Indestructible, Conquerors of Human Misery, Spiritual Heartbreak and Decay of the Soul, which we do not know and will never know. Because we will never admit them into our Lives. We are Strong in Soul, in Heart, We are Indestructible Spirits!

Our gratitude to President Nicolás and to all the Brothers and Sisters of that Beloved Venezuela, who organized that Summit, that Meeting for Union and Awareness of the Indispensable Reinforcement of Peace, promoting the Prosperity that we all deserve.

We congratulate President Nicolás, I say again, and we wish every Victory to that Brave, Much Admired and Loving People of Bolívar, of Chávez, that People of Yours, President Nicolás... Victories you  deserve and will certainly achieve.

We also salute the accreditation of our Colleague, Educator, Professor, Harold Delgado, who today presented Style Copies from the People and Government of Darío, of Zeledón, of Sandino, to the People and Government of Colombia.

We are grateful too for the Placet granted by the Heroic, Valiant, Combative Republic of South Africa, its People and Government, to Compañero Danilo Javier Chang Cash, who has been representing us for some days now as Chargé d'affaires, and after presenting Style Copies and Letters of Credence to the, we repeat, Valiant Government and People of Nelson Mandela, he will be acting as an Envoy of our People, of our Government, strengthening our Historical Ties of Struggles and Triumphs.
