Government carries out Plan for Stability, Security and Peace

Submitted bytortilla onVie, 31/05/2019 - 09:12

Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS

 

Managua, Nicaragua

 

PRESS RELEASE
FROM THE DELEGATION OF THE GRUN FOR
UNDERSTANDING AND PEACE IN NICARAGUA

Friday, May 31, 2019

As another Victorious Week of Love, Peace, Security, Work and profound Healthy and Harmonious Joy has concluded, we greet the Nicaraguan Families and the International Community with the commitment to continue advancing in Harmony and Common Good for the Motherland.

Accordingly, we ratify all the Commitments assumed with the Motherland and the Nicaraguan Homes, Families and Communities, to revive and restore the best of each and every one of us.

Yesterday, May 30, Mother's Day, working in on the Path of Togetherness, Understanding, Stability and Peace, we have made progress with the following:

    1. The release of another 50 people linked to the terrorist and violent acts of last year, to whom the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity, has granted the Family Cohabitation Regime so they may return to their Mothers, Wives and Families. This release was in strict adherence to our Constitution and Laws and with the accompaniment of the International Red Cross, as Observers.

By June 18, our Commitments in this field, will be fulfilled.

    2. Hundreds of Commissions for Reconciliation, Justice and Peace have been installed, are convening, and continue to convene throughout the country.

    3. The Law for the Comprehensive Plan of Attention to Victims of the Terrorist Coup has been approved in the National Assembly of Nicaragua. The Plans derived from this Law will be known and applied as of next week.

    4. We continue to respond to requests from Nicaraguans who left the country last year, based on the tragic events of the failed attempted coup d'état, and which apply to the Plan for Safe Return and

Social Reintegration within the Model of Family and Community Values that we promote in Nicaragua.

    5. We continue to advance from all State Institutions, guaranteeing the Promotion and Protection of our Democracy, in all aspects of Life.

We promote the Respect of everyone for the Constitution and the Laws that govern our Republic.

    6. The Institutions of the State and Government guarantee the Right to Citizen Security, to freedom of circulation and mobilizations that are characteristic of our daily Life, our Economy, our Education, as well as our Commercial, Recreational, Social, Family, Communal and Religious Activities.

We guarantee all the indispensable Rights for the development of all branches of our economy, which is sustained by the protagonism of hundreds of thousands of Producers, Traders, Entrepreneurs and Workers throughout the country.

    7. We provide assurance that each Nicaraguan feels the Blessing of being a child of a Harmonious Country of Families committed to the Common Good, and of a Model of Christian Solidarity and Fraternity that promotes Well-being, Tranquility and Improvement in our Lives.

    8. The Nicaraguan people have continually expressed their rejection against Unilateral, Illegal and Coercive Political and Economic Measures that affect our Right to Live and Work with Programs and Projects that allow us to continue moving forward against Poverty.


    9. We guarantee that all Nicaraguans comply with the Laws and Measures that our Economy, bludgeoned by the Coup, demands for Stability to allow us to resume the Paths of Growth that Nicaragua was travelling.

    10. We advance in the Protagonism of Nicaraguan Workers and Cooperatives in all the Economic, Productive, Commercial, Labor, Social and Entrepreneurial Activities, according to the Constitution and the Laws of our Nicaragua.

    11. In a responsible manner we work on the Strengthening of the Electoral Institutions and in all the Processes that will allow us to organize with transparency and success, the National Elections of 2021.

As a Free, Dignified, Sovereign and Proud Motherland of Revolutionary Triumphs that installed Democracy in Nicaragua on July 19, 1979, We Move Forward, in Freedom, Fraternity, Hope and Peace, advancing with the Families and Communities of this Nicaragua, Blessed and Always Free, on Paths that continue to inspire the Peoples of the World.

We comply with our Plan for Stability, Security and Peace in Nicaragua, guaranteeing a Calm and Welcoming Life, in Christianity and Solidarity.

Friday, May 31, 2019

Delegation of the GRUN for
Understanding and Peace in Nicaragua
