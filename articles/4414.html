Message to the Presidents of the Central American Integration System

Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 15/06/2024 - 16:16

(Unofficial translation)

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Managua, June 15th 2024

Your Excellency
Señora Xiomara Castro
President of the Republic of Honduras
and President Pro Tempore
of the Central American Integration System

Your Excellency
Mr Nayib Bukele
President of the Republic of El Salvador

Your Excellency
Mr. Bernardo Arévalo
President of Guatemala

Your Excellency
Mr. Johnny Briceño
Prime Minister of Belize

Your Excellency
Señor Rodrigo Chaves Robles
President of the Republic of Costa Rica

Your Excellency
Mr. Laurentino Cortizo
President of the Republic of Panama

Your Excellency
Mr. Luis Abinader
President of the Dominican Republic


Brother and Sister Presidents :

First of all, receive a cordial and fraternal Greeting from our Blessed and Always Free Nicaragua, in these complex times through which the Human Community is living, and facing the challenges that we must all assume together so as to seek Peace and Just Development, with the aim of fighting Poverty, efforts we know we are all making, each one with their People, and each Country according to its circumstances.

With this Message between Central American Brothers and Sisters, we wish to ratify and confirm, that Nicaragua will receive the Pro Tempore Presidency of SICA, in accordance with the schedule and forms established in our Regulations and Protocols.

Our Foreign Minister, General-in-Retirement Denis Moncada, and the entire team of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Nicaragua, will coordinate the relevant occasions and events so as to optimize the work of our Pro Tempore Presidency, which we will be carried out with Everyone and for the Good of Everyone in effective coordination with all our fraternal Countries.

With full confidence in the validity of our Aspirations, Dreams and Rights, we reiterate to you our Warmest Greetings, with the assurance that as Peoples of a beautiful and victorious Region, we are going onward, building together the Future which our Peoples, and everyone among them, deserve.
