Message to the President of Palestine on the passing of Saeb Erekat

Submitted bytortilla onMar, 10/11/2020 - 13:41

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Managua, November 10, 2020

Brother President
of the Palestinian People and State
Comrade Mahmoud Abbas
Your hands

Brother President:

We have felt deeply the death of Dear Brother Saeb Erekat, who today passed to another Plane of Life in Jerusalem, as a Symbol of his Heroic Struggle for Justice for his People and his Sacred Land.

We had the honor of meeting and talking with Brother Saeb Erekat, when together with Comrade Commander Yasser Arafat, we shared hours and days of Reflections and Proposals for a World of Justice and Peace.

In the face of this sensitive and irreparable loss, we convey to You, Brother President, and through You, to all your People and in particular to all the Comrades of Brother Saeb Erekat, his Family, his Loved Ones, an Embrace of Solidarity, in the continuity of the Struggles that we wage for a World of Brotherhood, Respect, Dialogue and Peace.
