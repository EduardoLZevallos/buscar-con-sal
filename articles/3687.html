Heroines of Our Nicaragua, Blessed, Dignified, Sovereign and Free

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 07/03/2024 - 21:22

(Unofficial translation)

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

HEROINES OF OUR NICARAGUA
BLESSED, DIGNIFIED, SOVEREIGN AND FREE

Women are the Life Fulfilled of our Nicaragua, Blessed, Sovereign, Dignified, For Ever Free!

We have given Life, fighting with Love and Hope, on every Front, Defending Peace and the Right of Nicaragua’s Families to live peacefully and safely in our own Land, working, without discrimination or exclusion, to prosper; strengthening Harmonious and Respectful Coexistence, every day, among Everyone, and for the Good of Everyone.

We are Thousands, We are Millions of Brave and Noble Nicaraguan Women who are classed as National Heroes, with Respect, Admiration and Affection from Everyone.

Today, March 8th in the National Assembly, we have launched the Proposed Legislation assuring for Women, Our Nicaragua’s Warriors of Light and Love, the Recognition of Everyone, for being the Inspiration and Example of the Courage to fully live out the Strength of our Nicaragua, Spiritual, Prodigious, Miraculous.

We assume the Banners of Dignity, in every space for the expression of our Distinguished Condition as Patriotic Nicaraguans, Industrious, Intelligent, Skilled, Sensitive, capable of living up to every trial and challenge at all times.

In these New Days, We Go Forward, committed to Work, Well-being, Security, Harmony in our Homes and Communities, with the Right to exercise all our Faculties, our Leadership, Talents, Ingenuity and Acute Thinking, continuing our essential contribution to Equitable Development, to progress in the Fight against Poverty.

Nicaraguan Women are the radiant Victories of this People of Ours, which shines with Luminosity, Joy, Strength and Faith. Being passionate about this New World, we know that we have been forging the Present and the Future, from our deeply embraced Causes of Justice, True Truth, and Making Sacred the Love that shines, moves and fills Souls.

Love and Brotherhood... Love and Generosity... Companionship, which means all the Paths we travel together, Women and Men, in sobering and liberating Missions. Because it is up to us to learn and to free ourselves from all hatred, a difficult feat, eliminating slights, disqualifications, and eradicating the unacceptable vulgarity of machismo that violates, limits, attacks and hinders and is an expression of the worst forms of domination, supremacy, arrogance, and everything that cuts short Qualities and kills Human Potentialities, which we must defend, raise, and increase every day.

We've made our way so much... We are so much... There are so many of us... We are Humanity that proposes Fulfillment for Everyone, Cooperation, Friendship, Fraternity, Kindness, Generosity Solidarity and the best Social, Cultural, Communications Practice, among ourselves in every Country, and every People, and in the necessary, beautiful relationships with all the Peoples of the World.

Our Advances, our Achievements, our Victories, our Triumphs, are the Advances, Achievements, Victories and Triumphs of our brave and Values-endowed Nicaraguan People, who in the Sandinista Popular Revolution learned to banish the will-o'-the-wisps of envy, greed, avarice, selfishness, vanity, destructive traits in no way representative of what we can be, in a World that we want to be Better.

It is our World, our Planet, our Nature, to inhabit it all fully, with the Colors, Aromas, Flavors and Blossoms that we must continue to pursue and strive for, without rest, without delay, without fainting, without cowardice, without yielding to hesitation.

As Women, as Human Beings, as Revolutionaries, as Evolutionaries, as Rainbows and Crystals, it is up to us to feel Part of the Essence, the Presence, the Cosmic Force of Universes that we proclaim, knowing that they belong to us and that among all the difficulties, challenges and griefs, transcendence is imposed, which is Spirit, Providence, Times in which to continue assuming our Totality, our Integrity, our Powerful Spiritual Abundance, along Paths of Peace, of Nationhood,  of Glory and Victories.

With every fiber of our Being, we are also the suffering we have been denouncing of millions of Women and Children who, stripped of all their Rights, but never of their Audacity and Courage, fight against arrogance, petulance, and the imperialist contempt for Life. We are talking about the genocide decimating Heroic Palestinian Families. Imperialism takes away all forms of existence, manifesting its own degradation, its misery and meanness. How shameful for an impotent Human Community, whose Organisms created to defend Peoples from any form of aggression, degradation or war, not only do not work, but rather serve, directly or indirectly, the interests and voracious and unbridled appetites of the Colonialists, Neocolonialists and Imperialists of the Earth.

Once again our serene, but powerful and consistent denunciation and condemnation of everything that seeks to bring down, strip the wings and the angelic potential of the Human Community anywhere. That is why we condemn the Government of Israel, its barbarism, and too its Promoters and Defenders.

All our Efforts, out of Solidarity and Christianity, Humanist, Committed to Life, have been, are and will be dedicated to strengthening Peace, Justice, Hope and the Right to Respect and the Well-being of Peoples.

Among all the aggressions of the Imperialists of the Earth, and thanks to the Dignity and Courage of our Women, Men, Youth and Families, Nicaragua has strengthened its Patriotism and its Awareness of Rights for Everyone.

We have advanced in understanding that the Unity of Everyone, for the Sake of Everyone, is indispensable to achieve our Common Good Purposes and Goals.

Together we have resisted, repelled and expelled every kind of filibuster, all the adventurers and outlaws, who via different forms of aggression, invasion, occupation and attempts at subjugation, wanted, of course without achieving it, to take over Nicaragua, to turn it into one more dull Star on the mediocre, dishonorable, unworthy Map of their conquests.

Filibusters, Invaders, Yankee and pro-Yankee Presidents imposed, overwhelming troops, betrayals that show up the servility and cowardice of the usual anti-patriotic opportunists; Coups d'état, Dictatorships, Assassinations, yet more Coups, undeclared Wars, other forms of Dictatorship, and the irrationality of Economic Blockades, with a pack of stateless criminals baying for the imperial hand to impose ever more poverty and calamity on our Country.

But They Could Not Prevail, Nor Will They! For here we are, the heroic Nicaraguan People... The most Heroic Women of all the eras and of all our Nation, Blessed and Free... Its Families, its Youth, its Grandparents, Everyone United, United All "millions imposing the Truth”, motivated, grateful to God, and Always Further On, in the Thought, in the Vision, in the Conviction that a Better World is not only possible, but essential.

A better world we are already inhabiting! And in Nicaragua, Always Further On, Women today celebrate Peace, and the Norms, the Laws and Practices of Equity through which we have recognized that we are Creators of Life, Banners of Freedom; Torches and Sacred and Eternal Fires, that we have been creating Improvements in Life, in Our Lives and in that of all Nicaragua’s Families.

Nicaragua is made of Vigor, Vitality, Color, Love, Glory and Victories!

Nicaragua is made for Truth, Peace and Joy!

Nicaragua is made for Dignity!

Nicaragua is Hope!

Nicaragua is an Emblem of Abundant Triumphs!

Nicaragua is Blessed
with the Heart and Soul
of Great Women!

The Power of Love
the Power of Women
Always Overcoming
Always Further On!

Managua, March 8th, 2024

Rosario Murillo
