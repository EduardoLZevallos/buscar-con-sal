Should you wish to receive witness statements as soon as they are available for your own reporting, please email Naomi Colvin at Bridges for Media Freedom at bridges4media@riseup.net – we have also attached the prosecution and defence’s skeleton arguments for your reference should you need them.

Despite a brief postponement – see here – the hearings are set to continue on Monday with American lawyer Eric Lewis scheduled for a full day of cross examination. Also expected to give evidence later in the week are Daniel Ellsberg of the Pentagon Papers, Middle East journalist Patrick Cockburn and our very own Nicky Hager.
