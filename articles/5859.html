Nicaragua's Interior Ministry publishes list of prisoners benefiting from house arrest or other precautionary measures

Submitted bytortilla onVie, 15/03/2019 - 17:20

PRESS RELEASE

The Ministry of Interior of the Republic of Nicaragua informs the Nicaraguan Families and other Nationalities that, through the General Directorate of the National Penitentiary System, 50 orders have been issued in favor of persons who were detained for having committed crimes against security and public tranquility. These persons have received the legal benefit of family cohabitation or other precautionary measures.

Given in the City of Managua on the fifteenth day of the month of March of the year 2019.

Public Relations
Ministry of Interior
