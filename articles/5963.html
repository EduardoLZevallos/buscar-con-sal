Webinar - Cuba, Nicaragua, Venezuela - January 26th 2025

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 23/01/2025 - 17:28

WEBINAR, SUNDAY JANUARY 26

REGISTER:  bit.ly/NicaJan26  

 

Noon Pacific time / 2 pm Nicaragua / 3 pm Eastern / 8 pm Greenwich UK

The United States and its allies have long imposed harmful “sanctions,” economic coercive measures, on Cuba, Nicaragua, and Venezuela.  The US also threatens to increase them! But we are promising to fight back, and our struggle has brought us closer together, as activists for all three countries.  Please join us as our webinar speakers describe all the actions taken during Human Rights Week – and how YOU can join our future collaboration. We're just getting started!
 

Speakers:
Jennifer Atlee, Nicaragua Solidarity Coalition
Leonardo Flores, Venezuela Solidarity Network
Onyesonwu Chatoyer, National Network on Cuba

The webinar will be in English only, but with an option for translated captions in Zoom.  Major sponsors are Americas Without Sanctions (AWS), Sanctions Kill Coalition, Nicaragua Webinars, Nicaragua Solidarity Coalition, Venezuela Solidarity Network (VSN), and  National Network on Cuba (NNOC).

Grateful thanks to all our co-sponsors, who help spread the word about our webinars!  If your organization would also like to co-sponsor, email NicaraguaWebinars@gmail.com.
