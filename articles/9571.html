Cuba contains COVID-19 Webinar - Wednesday June 24th 8.00pm EST

Submitted bytortilla onMar, 23/06/2020 - 16:37

International Committee 
For Peace, Justice and Dignity
REGISTER FOR ZOOM EVENT
..an interactive event with activists, medical professionals and elected officials who are building the campaign for medical collaboration with Cuba, to save lives. Learn about the campaign, get tools you can use in your area, plan with others. 
 
Following 30 minutes of presentations, we will divide into three breakout groups for one hour where activists can discuss and plan how to:                                   
  • Build support for medical collaboration among health and scientific professionals. 
  • Pass action resolutions with elected officials at city, county and state levels.
  • Media, digital outreach & getting involved
 
Chairperson:
Isaac Saney - National spokesperson of the Canadian Network on Cuba
 
Presenters:
Dr Abraham Vela - California graduate of the Latin American Medical School in Havana, Cuba
Niki Ashton - New Democrat federal member of Canadian parliament from Manitoba
Dr Andy Coates - Assistant Professor of Medicine and Psychiatry, Albany Medical College. Critical care hospitalist on the COVID-19 team
Sandra Pappas - Democrat State Senator from Minnesota.
She introduced a resolution in the Minnesota Senate for US-Cuba collaboration to fight COVID-19

On registering you will be invited to pre-select which breakout
group you want to join
