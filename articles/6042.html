FEET OF CLAY

"No, I cannot nor do I want to be on the side of those silver-toothed buffaloes. They are my enemies, they are haters of Latin blood. They are Barbarians. That is what makes every noble heart tremble today. That is what makes every noble heart protest today. That is what makes every person of dignity protest today...”
Rubén Darío
The Triumph of Caliban

The Government of Reconciliation and National Unity of this Blessed, doubly Blessed, Forever Sovereign, Free and Victorious Nicaragua declares once again unacceptable the words of officials of the Government of the United States of America, when their butchery and diabolical behavior throughout the world represents shameless affront, abuse, suffering, death and pain for millions of Human Beings who have lived and are living the hell of Imperialism and its Policies and Practices.

The Anthem of the Sandinista National Liberation Front that triumphed with the People of Nicaragua over the criminality of the Somoza Tyranny imposed by the United States states clearly “we are fighting against the Yankee, the Enemy of Humanity.”

That Slogan and that Premise are permanent, as long as the United States, invaders and destroyers of so many Cultures and Peoples in the World, maintains its aggressive, supremacist desire for domination, over the Freedoms and Rights that belong to those of us who are born and live in Sovereign Lands.

Rubén Darío in his immortal Poems denounced the barbarism of the Imperialists of the Earth, and announced Golden Dawns for the Peoples who, aware of our Patrimonial Rights, among them above all the Courage with which we defend what is ours, We are Going Onward, knowing that God is Great and will surely illuminate, making Conscious of His Power, the Great Power of God, those who think they can supplant Him, replace Him, without realizing that God is God, and no one can be like him, although the foolhardy pretend in their fierce vanities, they can do anything.

We human beings can do nothing without the power of God! We human beings can do nothing if vanity blinds us to the extremes of flaunting delusions and the drunkenness of absurd, demented pretense.

"... Roosevelt, you would need to be God Himself,
the most terrible Marksman and strong Hunter,
to be able to hold us in your iron claws.

And, though you may seem to have everything, one thing you are missing: God!”
Rubén Darío
Ode To Roosevelt

Sandino’s Children
Neither Sell Out, Nor Surrender, Ever!
We fight against the Yankee,
The Enemy of Humanity!

Managua, February 5th 2025
Government of Reconciliation
and National Unity
Republic of Nicaragua
