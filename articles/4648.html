Address of Compañero Ramiro Valdez

Long live Cuba!

Comrade and Brother, Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra, President of the Republic of Nicaragua; beloved Compañera Rosario Murillo, Vice President of the Republic of Nicaragua; Sisters and Brothers of the Worthy Nicaraguan People.

I bring a Revolutionary and Supportive Embrace from Army General Raúl Castro and President Miguel Díaz-Canel Bermúdez, who, like all our People, admire our Sister, Beloved Nicaragua.

On behalf of the Cuban People and Government, I congratulate the Heroic Nicaraguan People on the 45th Anniversary of the Triumph of the Sandinista Popular Revolution.

It fills us with pride to share with this People a common History of Resistance and Dignity in the face of foreign interference and the pressures of imperialist domination.

Yesterday on the field of National Liberation and Emancipation, today facing the dirty war of Imperialism, its aggression, blockades and sanctions. But, in the face of every challenge, frontal or disguised, the examples of Sandino, Martí and Fidel guide us towards new forms of Resistance and Victories.

Nicaragua has the Right which it won in its Struggle, to live in Peace and without external interference. Nicaragua has the right to face its challenges with the work of its children and International Cooperation, without any conditions being imposed on it.

Cuba firmly rejects the Unilateral Coercive Measures imposed by Yankee Imperialism and its allies against Sister Nicaragua, which affect the Nicaraguan population and constitute open interference in the internal affairs of this nation.

Likewise, we condemn the gross manipulation of Nicaragua’s reality, which seeks to leave the image of the Country in flagrant disrespect of the Right and Self-Determination of Peoples.

Sandinista Nicaragua is a Nation of Men and Women emancipated by their tireless Struggle for True Independence, without external tutelage of any kind.

With a colossal effort, peace and genuine Freedom were conquered here. The progress they can show today in economic and social matters is irrefutable, under the successful leadership of the Government of Reconciliation and National Unity headed by Comrades Daniel and Rosario. This experience allows Nicaragua to confront external aggression and destabilizing actions against the Well-being and Tranquility of its Citizens.

Cuba is honored to be and to have been at the side of the Nicaraguan people throughout this struggle for emancipation. 45 years ago the Sandinista Revolution triumphed and the Brother and Sisterhood between our Peoples and Governments was sealed, based on respect, trust and mutual support.

On behalf of the Cuban People, I would like to thank Nicaragua for its Solidarity, its permanent Support, its Sincere Friendship, in the face of the challenges imposed on Cuba by the hostile policy of the United States, which has turned into a real economic war.

Our Countries face great challenges to preserve their Social Achievements, defending their Revolutionary Principles. Today, more than ever, Cuba and Nicaragua need the solidity of their bonds of Friendship and Solidarity.

Our Revolutions have great Strengths to Move Forward, they have the support of our Peoples, who have been able to defend their Right to Self-Determination and Independence. They have the example and guidance of their Historical Leaders, who have left us a Legacy of Dignity and Rebellion, and they have the support of other Fraternal Countries that recognize the value and Independence of Cuba and Nicaragua for integration and the transition to a more Just and Equitable World.

The People of Nicaragua will always have in the People of Cuba a Brotherly People, we are more than two Countries, we are two Fraternal Peoples, who share common Principles, Values, and Ideals, we have a history of Resistance, forged from the steel of authentic Revolution.

To you Comandante, to our beloved Rosario, to the Nicaraguan People, our Fraternal Embrace and our Support and Solidarity. Cuba will always be at the side to this glorious Land.

Long live Augusto Sandino!
Long live the Sandinista Popular Revolution!
Long live the Sandinista National Liberation Front!
On to Victory always!
Our Nation or Death, we will overcome!

Address by Compañero Barthélemy Kére

Compañero President of the Republic of Nicaragua; Compañera Vice President of the Republic of Nicaragua. First of all, allow me to express my deep gratitude to the Authorities of the Republic of Nicaragua for the particularly warm welcome and attention that my Delegation and I have received since our arrival in this magnificent capital of Managua.

We are deeply honored and touched by this great gesture of friendship that reflects the quality of the relations that our Countries have, whose Leaders and Peoples share the same Values of Peace and Solidarity.

The Victory of the Sandinista Revolution, for which we are celebrating its 45th anniversary today, was a catalytic event for the development of Nicaragua and Latin America as a Continent in general. Through the Revolution, the Republic of Nicaragua has fully found its Sovereignty and has managed to restore the Dignity of its Brave People.

Today, despite the sanctions that are unjustly imposed on it, Nicaragua continues to be a Model of Social Development, and inspires many countries around the world, including Burkina Faso.

That is why, on this Happy Occasion, I would like to congratulate the excellent relations of friendship and cooperation that have united our two Countries since the establishment of cooperative relations on November 30th 1983.

Unfortunately, our President was assassinated in 1987. But in recent years, the authorities of our country have wanted only to express their appreciation for the great consideration that our Country and our People have received, which have also helped us to revitalize cooperation between our States.

A great hope that encourages me to congratulate you today is the fact that the Republic of Nicaragua opened an Embassy in our Capital, in November 2023. This embassy adds to the different Agreements that have been signed between our two countries, helping constitute the basis of the cooperation that we intend to nurture in the future.
That is why, in order to help each other face the challenges we have in the present, and for our future, Burkina Faso congratulates you for this commitment of the Government of Nicaragua and we stand by your side.

So I can assure you that our Country will continue to work tirelessly to promote Solidarity relations between our two Nations.

Beloved Compañeras and Compañeros, before concluding my speech, allow me to reiterate my gratitude for the warm welcome given to our Delegation. I congratulate the Authorities from this Beautiful Country, for the excellent organization of this Event, and also, from the depths of my heart, I wish every Success and Progress to the People of Nicaragua.

Excellency, Señor Presidente, allow me to take this opportunity to invite you so that soon a Delegation from your country will be able to visit us in our country, in Burkina Faso. A country of men and women of integrity and a hospitable land, a country that is your friend, the country of the late President Isidoro Sankara, so that we can strengthen our bonds of Friendship and Cooperation.

Congratulations on the Celebration of the 45th Anniversary of the Sandinista Revolution.

Long live the Cooperation and Friendship of Burkina Faso and the Cooperation of Nicaragua!

Thank you very much.
