Message to the Muslim Community the World Over

Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 15/06/2024 - 18:22

(Unofficial translation)

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

Beloved Brothers and Sisters of the Muslim Community
All over the World

“In the name of God, the Gracious,
the Merciful.”


Beloved Brothers and Sisters, All of You,

On these Blessed, Holy Days, you celebrate the realization of one of the most important pillars of the Pilgrimage (El-Hajj) for the period of 2024. On this great occasion, on behalf of the People and Government of Reconciliation and National Unity of the Republic of Nicaragua, and our own, we extend our most sincere congratulations on the occasion of the Blessed Eid Al-Adha, and we pray to Almighty God that all pilgrims return safely to their homes with their hearts full of Faith, Goodness and Blessedness.

Undoubtedly, the importance of this religious occasion, beloved and appreciated Heart and Soul by Muslims all over the World, carries many connotations and meanings of Great Faith, an occasion on which the meanings of Sacrifice and Redemption are manifested in sincere and faithful fulfillment of the Will of Almighty God, in a unique moment that God has granted to the Faithful as a means of Mercy, Peace and Joy.

Unfortunately, at the time this great religious occasion is being celebrated, faced with the continuing events and difficulties of Life in Palestine, especially in the Gaza Strip, which is being subjected to genocide in sight of the world, we affirm our Solidarity and our Position at the side of our Brothers and Sisters in Palestine, especially in Gaza. We pray to God to grant the People of Gaza firmness, strength and empowerment, to bless their Faith and Patience, to be their help and support, and to eliminate their suffering and injustice.

We greet you with respect and affection from Nicaragua, a land Blessed and Forever Free, on this important opportunity for remembering and renewing our Fraternity and for strengthening our Unity and Solidarity for the Good of Humanity, whereby we pray on behalf of our Nation, and in Offering Our Prayers draw closer to God for the creation of a World where Prosperity, Happiness and Peace prevail throughout the World.

A happy Eid... May the Peace, Mercy and Blessings of God be with you.
