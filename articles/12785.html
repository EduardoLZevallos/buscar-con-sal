Webinar - Hybrid War and US Regime Change in Nicaragua - September 1st 2021

Submitted bytortilla onMié, 01/09/2021 - 15:44

Gloria --

Please join CODEPINK tonight at 8pm ET / 5pm PT for an exciting actionar: Hybrid War, Sanctions & U.S. Regime Change in Nicaragua. Vijay Prashad of the Tricontinental Institute for Social Research, Alex Main of the Center for Economic and Policy Research and Jill Clark-Gollub of Friends of Latin America will engage in a timely discussion of the RENACER Act and U.S. attempts at regime change during this Presidential Election year in Nicaragua.  After the discussion, participants will contact their Representative and urge them to vote no on the RENACER Act, which would impose brutal sanctions on the Nicaraguan people. Click here to RSVP!

Then join us on Friday for a fundraiser benefiting Cuba and Haiti, where blockade-induced shortages of food and a devastating earthquake, respectively, have hit folks hard. We’re holding a solidarity fundraiser and art auction to send material aid to these Caribbean nations. The donations are tax deductible and will go to grassroots groups in Cuba and Haiti. Check out our donation page here! We’ll be livestreaming part of this event and there will be art you can bid on online. RSVP here for the livestream! 



We hope to see you online at one or both of these events. You can take further action by clicking here to stop sanctions on Nicaragua! Then click here to insist that the U.S. let Cuba live!

In radical solidarity,
Leonardo, Teri, Michelle and Medea
