Declaration - The Power of Nicaragua is Life

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 21/03/2023 - 21:51

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

THE POWER OF NICARAGUA
IS LIFE

Declaration
of the Government of Reconciliation
and National Unity

There is no better context to talk about these issues than this International Day to condemn the atrocious crimes that Empires have committed against millions of Human Beings, and in particular the North American Empire, in the World.

Twenty years ago, on 20th March, 2003, the genocidal government of the United States of America promoted and unleashed, based on lies, on the absurd, on the custom of establishing their hate crimes and crimes against humanity on the worst falsehoods, the most absurd deceits, the biggest and most ridiculous fallacies, in a colossal deception and scam to the World, toward Humanity, to the Human Community; the ferocious, senseless, crazy, inadmissible and condemnable invasion and destruction, of a Culture and a Country, which was advancing in Scientific Development and Prosperity for its inhabitants.

Twenty years ago, the Human Family rose up denouncing and condemning the hoax, the crimes, the hatred and the greed that at that moment brought us together; it represented all the crimes, hatred and greed of all the invasions, occupations and looting of the Countries and Peoples of the World, by the United States and Empires... All accomplices of genocide and crimes against humanity!

In this Commemoration of Blood, Sweat and Tears, but also of Heroic Resistance, we join the Free Peoples of the World, who are denouncing, rejecting and condemning the brazenness of the greatest murderers and criminals in Humanity´s recent history, those who yesterday and today, yet again, dare to spread and multiply lies, slander, infamous disqualifications and deceptions about the Countries and Peoples that are not docile to them, according to their narrative of greed and the same and repetitive desires for domination and submission of those of us who are Sovereign and Free in the World.

Here, in the Heroic Motherland, Always Blessed and Free, of the Universal and Great Rubén Darío, and of the Patriots, Generals José Dolores Estrada, Benjamín Zeledón, Augusto Sandino, and so many tens and hundreds of thousands of Heroes and Martyrs, who have known, because we know, how to defend our Right to be Free, Sovereign, Just, in Fraternal Solidarity and, of course, Christians... Here, we denounce you, imperialist lords of the Earth, because you do not have the slightest Moral Authority to speak, if it is not about yourselves and your atrocities.

We do not recognize any authority of yours to lie, violate and sully, as you always do, our Historical Greatness; in the face of the shame that you should feel, and that your People feel, for the vulgar outrages and the elementary and brutal falsehoods, which seem designed for an elemental World, stripped of intelligence, a World of cheap pamphlets that no one reads anymore, nor does it occur to them to buy or try to digest.

On this 21st March of the Year of New Victories of Peace and Common Good, from Our Nicaragua Blessed and Always Moving Forward, once again we accuse, once again we denounce, once again we condemn North American Imperialism as the worst war criminal and criminal against humanity of all time, and we do not tire in demanding Respect for our Peoples, our Sovereignties, our Countries and our Political, Social and Economic Models, that are freely chosen and that reflect the virtues and magnitudes of our Triumphant Resistance; because throughout of our Story of Bravery, we have expelled you from these Sacred Lands, Blessed by the God of All Victories.

We feel proud of our Paths, of our daily advances, and we know that, evidently, the days of the Yankee Empire are numbered. We warn you, Sirs, that Peace, Hope, Certainty, Rights, Justice and Truth, is not played with. Do not dare to continue tampering with Liberties, the Rights, and the Courses of Work, Concord, Brotherhood and Prosperity, that we carry in this other World, which is no longer a servant, subject or slave of your satanic appetites.

Imperialists lords, here we fight to Win and there are no diabolical, petty, mediocre servants who can conquer us, because they have never conquered us. And if they couldn't before, they won't be able to do so now. And if we know that they couldn´t, we know that they will not be able to!

Always, Always Beyond!

Managua, 21st March, 2023
