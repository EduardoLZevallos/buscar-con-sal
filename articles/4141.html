IN VICTORIOUS DIGNITY

ALWAYS FURTHER ON

 

Remarks by Compañera Rosario Murillo

 

Midday, May 3rd 2024

 

Tomorrow is Saturday, May 4th, Beloved Families of our Blessed Nicaragua, and the Bells will ring out in joyful peals of Life and Hope... Bells to the Wind, and to Times of Triumph, in May, another month of May, May once more of Dignity and National Decorum.

 

Tomorrow, May 4th, the Wisdom of our Native Peoples; of our Zeledón, our Sandino; Andrés, Estrada; our Comandantes, the Sandinista National Liberation Front with so very many of us, Carlos, Tomás, Daniel, will echo among the Winds of Change that they are already, and that we all are now, in Peace, Brotherhood, Solidarity and the Common Good, as a Family and Community.

 

This May our Songs are coupled, united to the Fields, to the Songs and Battles that we Peoples fight everywhere, demanding Justice and True Truths... Demanding to stop the cruel genocide, treated as if it were cinema, of the Palestinian People... Demanding respect for the Highest Dignity of the Palestinian People... Demanding that Love be, in Truth, forever stronger than Hatred, and that the Horizon of this Planet of ours and the Cry of the entire Human Family be one in our Demand for Justice, for Values, for True Truths, for Peace.

 

Tomorrow, when we adorn the Monuments and Mausoleums, the Luminous Cathedrals, those Cathedrals that are the Tombs of our Heroes, there, where as Heroic Nicaraguans, illuminated by the Dignity of Nicaragua, there where they are Rebirth, Resurrection and Life to the Full, we will feel our Hearts united in a Great Battle... Peace for everyone... Dignity as a Universal Proclamation, and Voices and Hands and Souls raised with the Undefeated Banners of the Human Community, that we are all... By expelling, as we want to banish from the face of the Earth, hatred, insolence, petulance, the hateful pretence of domination, and the perversion of those who think they own the World, wanting to prevail by means of massacres and all that weaponry which threatens to do away with us all.

 

"Always Further On," said Sandino... We too say Always Further On... Dignity means Peace... Dignity which means Love... Dignity which means Struggles, Battles, Combats, Vigorous and Victorious, unstoppable now, so that a Life of Great Fraternity, Solidarity, of Protagonism and Complementarity, becomes ours, because we strive to bring it closer, because we know how to bring it closer through our firm Convictions of that urgently needed World, that Multipolar World of Respect, Integration, Cooperation and Union, for the development of all Sciences, and in particular, the Science of Affection, of Wisdom, of Harmony, Serenity... Paths where we walk with Strong, Sublime Spirits... Paths for us to make our way to become Strong, Sublime, Transcendent Spirits, building Firmaments and Futures for Everyone, for the Good of Everyone.

 

On our Day of National Dignity, Let us Go Onward with the Free and Dignified Peoples of the World, beyond Selfishness, banishing all pretense at Supremacy, but in complete agreement on the Certainties of Justice and Peace.
