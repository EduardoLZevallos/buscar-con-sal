In the Eternal Fire of Carabobo

Enviado por tortilla el Mar, 24/06/2025 - 14:44

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

IN THE ETERNAL FIRE
OF CARABOBO

Compañero
Nicolás Maduro Moros
Constitutional President
of the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela
In Person

Beloved Nicholas :

Today, June 24th, when together with Venezuela with Bolivar, Chávez and All of America, Our America-Caribbean, we celebrate the 204th Anniversary of the Heroic, Historic, Great Battle of Carabobo, which sealed the decision of our Peoples to be Free, Independent and Sovereign, from Nicaragua, Always Blessed and Forever Free, this Nicaragua of Darío, Sandino, and of our Brave and Indefatigable People, we ratify our Sacred Union in the Greater Nation to continue defending with Patriotism the Right of our Sovereign Nations and Communities, to live safely, in Justice, Peace, and Self-determination.

To celebrate Carabobo is to celebrate Bolivar, the Great, the Liberator; the Dreamer, the unbreakable Leader of our Valiant and Unyielding Unity, and to celebrate the inviolable Spirit of Struggles and Victories that characterizes us, ratifying our Union, to continue defending Respect and Harmony as a Way of Life for the Human Community.

From the Libertarian Soul and Conscience of Carabobo, with deep identification among the Great Spirits of our Liberators, this Symbol of our Greatness unites us today in the call that you have made to the Human Family with such Strength and Conviction, to defend Justice and Truth, as an infinite expression of the Power of Love, which must overcome hatred and establish Heart and Reason in all Life’s Battles.

We join that intense call of yours, in reflection and action, to continue changing the World, to ensure our permanence as Humanity and to continue, without fail, in our legitimate and invariable Cause of True Life, without intoxicated Supremacy, without dementia or delusions of non-existent greatness.

Only the Unity of the Peoples makes us free and that is and should be our daily task... We are with you, defending the Ideals and Values that should lead Humanity to ensure Peace, with responsibility, eloquence and profound and inalienable Spirituality.

We continue to call on each other to unite Wills, to raise Consciousness, lifting up our Voices, clearly and forcefully, given the Right we have as Peoples of this other World that Neither Sells Out, Nor Surrenders, Ever!

We insist:

On living together, sharing and taking on the risks and challenges, by means of positive, constructive dialogue, via Organizations that truly represent the Will, the Needs and the Solutions that the Peoples of the World propose.

Nicolás, Always Further On is and will continue to be our Watchword for every Battle and every Victory.

Our Cause continues, lives and endures "because it is the Cause of Justice and the Cause of Love."

Struggle is the Highest of Songs!

Managua, June 24th 2025
