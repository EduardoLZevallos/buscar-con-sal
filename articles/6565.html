The Day of Victories

Enviado por tortilla el Jue, 08/05/2025 - 15:23

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

THE DAY OF VICTORIES

Declaration by the People and State of Nicaragua

We celebrate the 80th Anniversary of the Victory over Fascism, in these Times when the World battles precisely, with all the Spirit of the Heroes from the Soviet Union and other Nations who fought and achieved the defeat of that terrible catastrophe which devastated and scourged Humanity.

Today we celebrate that 80th Anniversary and activate all the Forces of the Human Spirit to bury, definitively, that threat which today, more than ever, constitutes a very real risk of the extinction of the Human Species.

The barbarism unleashed by Nazism was defeated and its millions of victims are not only those who were massacred, those fallen on the battlefields, or their families, but also the millions who were imprisoned and brutally martyred in the concentration camps of Hitler's Germany.

To the relatives of the Heroes, to the valiant Red Army, to the Soviet Union, which today as the Russian Federation continues to wage essential battles against Fascism, our Respect, Admiration, Recognition, and Support on every Front, to defeat, to continue to defeat, that demonic plague, that spawn of Evil, that Hell on Earth, which is Fascism.

From all latitudes of this Planet we cry out for the End of Wars, for Peace as a Heritage of Humanity; we cry out for the Good of Everyone to prevail over attempts at domination and the extermination of Cultures, Peoples and the Human Family.

The Nicaraguan people, our People the National Assembly of Nicaragua, our Government of Reconciliation and National Unity, our Youth and our Families and all the Entities that make up our Proud, Dignified and Sovereign Nicaragua, Revolutionary, Sandinista and Internationalist, call out once more for everything indispensable to continue to be done, in Union with All; so that a Sense of Humanity of the most transcendent and complete spirituality may prevail, and so that the Peoples of the World may strengthen our Fraternal, Solidarity inspired and Complementary Unity, for Well-being and Life with Respect, Esteem and above all Consideration for the Rights of Everyone, the Right to Security, Work and Freedom, to Fraternal, Responsible Cooperation and Prosperity.

May Justice and Peace be re-established as the Principles and Final Objective in this our History of the XXI Century.

Therefore:

We declare May 9th, on the 80th Anniversary of the defeat of Fascism and in the month of the Birth of our Undefeated General, Nicaragua’s Greatest Hero, Augusto C. Sandino, the Day of Courageous and Uninterrupted Struggles and Victories for Brother and Sisterhood and Peace in the World.

The Earth, as a Paradise for All Humanity!

Managua, May 8th, 2025
