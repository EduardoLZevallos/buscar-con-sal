On the Day of Condemnation of Violence Against Women

Enviado por tortilla el Sáb, 25/11/2023 - 23:46

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

ON THE DAY OF CONDEMNATION

OF VIOLENCE AGAINST WOMEN

 

On this International Day of Courageous Struggle by All the Women of the World, from this Nicaragua of Permanent Revolution and Evolution, of Genuine Truth, of Light, of Life, of Future, we join the Great Spirit of Women everywhere demanding Respect, Consideration, Rights and Peace.

 

This November 25th, our deepest Fraternal Sentiments of Solidarity go above all to the Women of that Great, Heroic Land of Palestine, where they continue to suffer brutal aggression and unspeakable genocide, imposed by the Imperial, Colonial, Neocolonial and Fascist Powers, who always destroy Life with hatred and perversion as their invariable emblems.
 

We condemn all war, aggression and intervention resulting from injustice, humiliation, and Courageous Struggles against the perils and risks that threaten us all.

 

We repudiate and condemn the wars and aggressions imposed on us, with "inhuman, unworthy, cruel, humiliating and degrading treatment”, which have violated and continue to violate all our Rights and all we Deserve.

 

To women all over the world, our impassioned Heart... To the Brave and Heroic Women in Africa, Latin America and the Caribbean, Asia, North America, to the Women in all the Continents and Territories of this World, we unite in a Militant, Combative Spirit, repudiating once again all the forms of violence and aggression that afflict us, but do not defeat us.

 

We unite in Will, Dignity and a steadfast Commitment to make progress against all forms of indignity and violence!
 

We are writing a New Story. As United, Lucid, Intelligent and Valiant Women, we are the Force of Love, in all the Battles and all the Victories Ahead.

 

Against hatred and unreason, we oppose the formidable Power of Love, Life, Hope and Trust in the Spirit of Humanity.

 

Among so many Urgent Tasks Ahead, Nicaraguans know that the Priority is Peace. Peace as an urgent obligatio to be able to live, to advance, to overcome hatred, perversity, and to assert all our Rights and Pfull Potential.

 

Against everything that destroys

Love that builds!

 

Managua, November 25th 2023

 

Rosario Murillo
