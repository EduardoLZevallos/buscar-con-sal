Edén Pastora is born today, in the Eternal Heaven of Heroes

Submitted bytortilla onMar, 16/06/2020 - 16:22

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa


EDÉN PASTORA, THE LEGEND GROWS.
EDÉN PASTORA IS BORN
IN THE ETERNAL SKY OF HEROES

Today, Tuesday, June 16, you make your Transit to another Plane, vibrant and hopeful of Life, our Dear Comrade, Comandante Cero, Edén Pastora Gómez. And the Legend Grows. And Edén Pastora is born today, in the Eternal Heaven of Heroes. Go to that other vibrant Plane, we used to say, hopeful, of Life, those Paradises that God has reserved for the Human Beings who give their best efforts, all their Heart, to love and serve thy Neighbor.

With much Simplicity, with great admiration, with deep Respect and with undefeated Love, we say to Edén: Here we are and here is your People, enlightened by God and by all the Noble Souls that this Blessed and Always Free Motherland has given us to make Pathways, to continue making Pathways as we Walk forward, and to always do it with Sense of Good, of Faith, of Struggle for Peace, Unwavering Struggle for Peace, of Humanistic Victories, of Fraternity, and Solidarity, Victories of Indispensable Concord for the World to advance.

Edén, Legend, Heroic Narrative, Pride of our Nicaragua ... born in Ciudad Darío ... And how much do we owe to those Sacred Lands ... How much do we owe to all of our Nicaragua that it is a Garden where the Sun never declines, God our Lord, makes the Energy of so many Good Souls flourish every day whom have fought bravely for the Common Good, with Love and Good Will.
Edén Pastora Gómez, Comandante Cero, how does your name resonate in History, how you are present in the Memory and in all the Victories we are forging. Here is your People, your Family, the Youth that admires you so much, and here we are paying Homage to your Bravery, to your Audacity, to your Freedom, to your Spiritual, Christian, Nicaraguan, Sandinista Solidarity, learning every day, to be better and to work better, in the Name of Almighty God. Our Heavenly Father has given us a People of Extraordinary Human Beings, an Extraordinary People, to continue walking, fighting and conquering with absolute confidence in the everyday Victories and those of all Times.

Edén, how we love you! We are not going to miss you because you are with us, you are on at the Southern Front, you are in July, in August, in the Palace, in all the Moments of this Great Spring, for which every day we are grateful to God as His People, whom we are.

Nicaragua: An uninterrupted Spring of Motherland and Freedom, Joy, Peace and Future, a Spring that we are recreating every dawn, every hour, every day, with the Powerful Name of Jesus Christ, who gives us Strength, with so many Cheers, Souls, Hearts, who have given everything for the Motherland, who continue to give everything for the Motherland and for the Faithfulness of the Nicaraguan Families, for the Dignity of this Great People of Darío and Sandino, of Beings so Specials that they continue to provide us with their Example, Light, Life and Truth. Extraordinary Beings...! Together With all of them we go Always Beyond!

To Yolanda, Dear Sister, to all the children and grandchildren of Edén, to the children and grandchildren of Yolanda, our Love, our Great Love, Boundless, in the certainty that We are all together, and we are going together, with Edén, building what is necessary, indispensable Triumphs so that the Good, Simple, Noble Life of Well-Being and Bliss, which is the deserved Heritage of Nicaraguan Families, continues to show and reflect the Face and Sacred Heart of Jesus, who is the Face and the Sacred Heart of our People.

Forward Comrades ... The Future is Ours!

Until Forever, Edén ... Here you are with us in our Free Motherland, Motherland and Life, the Victories of every day. The future is ours !

Edén, Brothers, Sisters, Yolanda, here we are, everyone together, and We Move Forward, with that Strength that God renews in us every day.

Thanks to Life, and Thanks to the Life of Comandante Cero, Edén Pastora Gómez, who is here walking with us, with us!

Managua, June 16, 2020
Government of Reconciliation and National Unity
and Sandinista National Liberation Front
