Always Further On! Advancing in Revolution!

Enviado por tortilla el Vie, 22/11/2024 - 21:36

Initial and Final Remarks of the Declarations of Rosario, Vice President of Nicaragua,
in the Midday Edition of Multinoticias Canal 4

November 22nd 2024

A very good afternoon, Compañer@s, and a very good afternoon, Beloved Families of our Forever Blessed, Strong, Great, Nicaragua... Always Sovereign, Always Dignified, Always Combative, Always Loving and Always full of Faith!

 

Today is a historic Day. Our People are claiming tOur Right to have a Magna Carta that represents them and gives Prominence and Power, to Families, Men, Women, Young People, over all aspects of Life in Society, guaranteeing the Fundamental Rights of us all. Nicaragua's Citizens, who honor and are proud that we are building the Future free of Poverty.

 

We have seen in each of the Chapters of that Historic Document, which is now in the National Assembly, not only the vindication of the Heroes, Martyrs, Fighters, Combatants, who deserve and have always deserved a place in history and in the Glory of our Proud Nicaraguan Identity.

 

We have also seen the vindication of so much Holy Blood, which our People have offered up so that today we can live in Peace and Security, which no one can dare to violate; in Sovereignty, Dignity and National Pride, which no one can undo and which characterizes us as our profound Nicaraguan Truth, by the Grace of God!

 

We have seen justice... We have cried out for justice... We have made progress in justice... Justice, Truth, establishing the Ways of Struggle against that ignominious Poverty imposed on us by the Imperialists and their flunkies, their underlings, their shameless servants, who do not belong to this Blessed Race that knows very well, because we do know, about Struggles and Honor.

 

In Glorious Paths of Christianity, Socialism and Solidarity, our Revolution of Love and Peace is going Onward, and we know that Always Further On is the Rallying Cry, the Emblem, the Banner, the Flag. They are Banners that distinguish us for that growing ability to identify the transcendent moments, to move onward from the deserved Victories of Nicaragua's Courage, Nicaragua's Valor and Nobility, and Nicaraguan Loyalty that ensures our Patrimony as a People of God. Loyalty proof against every test!

 

A Blessed Day, A Blessed Time, Blessed History, Blessed, Blessed, A Thousand Times Blessed Nicaragua's People, a People of God!

 

A Free Nation, We Go Onward as a Free Nation, striving against Poverty, fighting Poverty, combating Poverty...! We are sure that this Spiritual and Profound Precept of a Free Nation, Nationhood And Freedom, Nationhood in Freedom, Sovereignty, National Decorum, the Always Further On, Miraculous and Impressive, is the essential characteristic and the fundamental trait of a People Full of Light, one that Never Sells Out, Nor Surrenders, Ever!

 

All together we say :

 

Long live our General of Free Men and Women, Augusto Sandino...!

 

Long live the People of Universal and Infinite Darío...!

 

Long live the Heroes and Martyrs of this Nation that resonates with its Rhythms, Melodies and Symphonies of Liberation and the Supreme Definition of Union, Fraternity, Hope, Joy, Solidarity, Future...!
 

Long live the Undefeated Strength of the Nicaraguan People, as together we create the Future...!

 

Long live Brother and Sisterhood, Unity, Peace...!

 

Long live the Revolution...!

 

Long Live Daniel...!

 

We give thanks to God, and as always, as ever, from this deeply grateful Heart of ours... Grateful to our Heroes, to our Martyrs, to their Families, and to all this Wonderful, Extraordinary, Always Dignified and Free People of which we are proudly part.
 

We overcome, Brothers and Sisters, and we will overcome Poverty...! That is the main adversary of Peace, of Security, and it is the adversary that we have been defeating with so much Force of Hope, so much Force of Joy, so much Force of Justice, so much Force of True Truths.

 

And I want to read precisely, as a demonstration of the True Strength of our People, the Preamble, the Beautiful Preamble of the Law that is in the National Assembly, of Partial Reform to the Political Constitution of our Republic of Nicaragua.

 

Evoking the Struggle of our Indigenous Ancestors that has its maximum expression in the Caciques Diriangén, Nicarao and Adiact, Paradigms of Dignity, Tenacity and Bravery, who forged and traveled Inspiring Paths, in courageous and decisive Liberation Struggles.

 

Evoking Fray Antonio de Valdivieso, the first Non-Indigenous Martyr, murdered by the colonizers in our Territory.

 

To the Hero of the Independence of Central America, Priest and Doctor Tomás Ruiz Romero, the First Indigenous person to obtain a University Degree.

 

To Cleto Ordóñez Bermúdez, Precursor of the Popular, Anti-Oligarchic and Anti-Imperialist Struggles, who fought for a Political, Economic and Social Order of Justice and Equality.

 

To General José Dolores Estrada Vado, Andrés Castro Estrada, Emmanuel Mongalo and Rubio, Distinguished Representatives of the Combative Tradition of our People, who defeated the filibuster coup and the Yankee American intervention in the National War of 1856.

 

Evoking the Indian Archers of Matagalpa, Worthy Representatives of the Struggle of the Native Peoples in the Battle, the Historic and Heroic Battle of San Jacinto.

 

To the Universal Poet, our Universal Poet Rubén Darío who embraced all Cultures, always striving for Peace; Rubén Darío, Hero of the Nation's Cultural Independence.

 

To General José Santos Zelaya López, Leader of the Liberal Revolution, Promoter of Secular Education and the separation between State and Church.

 

To General Benjamín Zeledón Rodríguez, Sower of Freedom, National Sovereignty, Honor and Unconditional Love for the Nation, Inspiration and Banner of Anti-Imperialist Struggle.

 

To the General of Free Men and Women, of Dignified, Intelligent, Brave and Fighting Peoples, Augusto C. Sandino, Founder of the Army in Defense of National Sovereignty of Nicaragua, who defeated the US military intervention. To that General of Free Men and Women, Augusto C. Sandino, Father of the Sandinista and Anti-Imperialist Popular Revolution, the Sun that illuminates us and never sets.

 

To Blanca Stella Aráuz Pineda who represents Nationalist and Libertarian Sentiment, and the Wisdom of Nicaraguan Women.

 

To General Miguel Ángel Ortez Guillén, who wields the machete and the sling defending forests, rivers and cities, as he did against the Yankees who would wet themselves in panic at the memory of his name.

 

To Major General Francisco Estrada Pérez and Brigadier General Juan Pablo Umanzor, outstanding Combatants, Members of the General Staff of the Army in Defense of Nicaragua's National Sovereignty, killed together with our General Augusto C. Sandino.

 

To Rigoberto López Pérez, Hero, Poet, Worthy Descendant of our General Sandino, whose Act of Justice signified the Beginning of the End of the Dictatorship.
 

To the Students who were the protagonists of the Glorious Days of Youth, embodied in the Martyrs of July 23rd 1959.

 

To Colonel Santos López, a vital link between the Army in Defense of National Sovereignty and the Sandinista National Liberation Front.

 

To Carlos Fonseca Amador, the Supreme Inheritor of Sandino's Legacy, Founder of the Sandinista National Liberation Front and Head of the Sandinista Popular Revolution.

 

To Tomás Borge Martínez, with his incredible Example of Persistence, Loyalty and Revolutionary Firmness, Founder of the Sandinista National Liberation Front.

 

To Camilo Ortega Saavedra, Apostle of the Sandinista Unity, an Uncompromising Combatant, Fighting for the Love, Hope and Joy of the People.

 

To Julio Buitrago Urroz, Father of the Urban Resistance, Bearer of the Moral Heritage of the Nicaraguan People, whose Heroic Action showed the Way to Liberation.

 

To Leonel Rugama Rugama, Poet, Immortal Guerrilla, one who did not surrender and became a Symbol of the Indomitable and Heroic Spirit of the Nicaraguan People.
 

To Germán Pomares Ordóñez, Defender of the Oppressed, Brave Guerrilla, Liberator, Founder of the Sandinista National Liberation Front.

 

To the Women of Cuá, Archetype of Strength and Bravery, Inspiring New Battles of Love, Peace, and a Good, Christian and Solidarity inspired Life.

 

To Dr. Pedro Joaquín Chamorro Cardenal, Martyr of Public Freedoms.

 

To Luis Alfonso Velázquez Flores, the Boy Hero, Icon of Revolutionary Character and Conscience, who embodies the deepest Spirit of our People.

 

To Gaspar García Laviana, Priest and Guerrilla Comandante, Model of Love for God through boundless Love of his Neighbor and complete dedication to the Freedom of the People.

 

To the Young People of San José de las Mulas, who represent Nicaragua's Youth defending the Values of the People and Revolutionary Principles.

 

To Cardinal Miguel Obando y Bravo, Cardinal of Peace and Reconciliation.
 

To the Great Roberto Clemente, Hero of Solidarity, Hero of Our America and Caribbean and of our Blessed and Forever Free Nicaragua.

 

To the Eternal Comandantes of Sovereignty, Independence and the Latin American and Caribbean Revolution: François Dominique Toussaint-Louverture, Jean-Jacques Dessalines, Simón Bolívar, José Martí, Emiliano Zapata, Francisco Villa, Francisco Morazán, Ernesto “Che” Guevara, Salvador Allende, Fidel Castro Ruz, Hugo Rafael Chávez Frías, Luminous Lives and Guides of Hope and the Rights of our People.

 

To all the generations of Heroes, Heroines and Martyrs who forged and developed the Liberation Battles and Struggles for National Independence, in Eternal Gratitude following their Luminous Examples.

 

And Compañer@s, as we were saying, we are certain, we know for sure that we are going to overcome Poverty, because we have Overcome, because We are Overcoming, and we will Overcome...! And the Enemies of the Nation, those who do not belong to this Nation, Neither Could They Prevail, Nor Will They!

 

This is how we are now, Compañeros and Compañeras, and We are Going Onward...! So much to thank God for...! Such Blessings! How Impressive ! How Much Strength! We are a People, and we never tire of saying it, Tremendous, with Great Spirit. It illuminates us and is a Sun that does not set!

 

Our Lord God is guiding our Steps, by His Hand and for His Glory, We Are Going Onward, striving to Overcome Poverty, building the Future, Always Further On...! Because we can. Because we count on that God, who is there blessing this Fraternal Love, this Brother and Sisterhood, this True Christianity that animates us every day. Which encourages us, enlightens us, and thus, from that Deep, True Christianity, we are able to achieve all the Victories.

 

Warm embraces from our Comandante Daniel, and from all of us. Here we are, All Together, building with Hope, that Future free of Misery and Poverty, that Future we all want and deserve.

 

Warm embraces, Compañeras and Compañeros The Future is Ours! Always Further On, advancing in Revolution! A warm embrace.
