The Strength of Peace !

Submitted bytortilla onJue, 04/04/2019 - 18:15


THE STRENGTH OF PEACE !


Peace is the Way... Work, Peace, Homeland and Future... Peace from the bottom of our Hearts... Peace as Conviction, and Conversion. Peace, which builds. Peace that opposes all forms of exclusion, discrimination, separation, or withdrawal from all Thought or Good Work.

Peace is the way. Build and work as Craftsmen of a Masterpiece, the best and Highest Feelings and Practices that bring us closer, unite us, and dispose us to Respect, to Comprehension, to Understanding, to Affection.

Peace as the Supreme Work of God and of Everyone. Peace as Divine Breath that brings happiness. Because Joy comes from Calmness, from Serenity, from Tranquility, from Peace !

We are responsible for the Restoration and Revival of all the Sources of Peace, of Future and Luminous Future in our Country. We are responsible, one and all, to hear the Prophecies, the Sacred Words, the Word; become each day a Spring of Light, Water of Life, Living Water, Spring of Faith, Hope, Fraternity and Solidarity.

Peace is Urgent. The most urgent! Peace is Wisdom! Peace is Science! Peace is the Fruit of Good Will that we have, that we bring, that we have, that we are. Peace is the Inner Change, the Transformation and the Cultural and Spiritual Growth of a People that loves God, that believes, that trusts, that waits ... A People that cries Blessing, Blessing, Blessing ... Peace is the obligatory exercise at each Dawn and the exhaustive examination at each Sunset.

Peace With Everyone, Between Everyone and For the Good of Everyone. Peace as Experience, Continuous Learning and Permanent Practices.

We congregate, Almighty God, as a People, as Families, as Human Beings consecrated to Peace that is Love, and can do everything, can transform everything, can create everything, again and again.

Nicaragua has the Merit of Miraculous Experiences that have made Understanding and Peace flourish, improving our Lives, in different difficult circumstances. Nicaragua has today the privilege of assuming the teachings of History, of our History, to raise and highlight the essential Commitments to walking as a family, healing wounds, cleaning hearts, purifying souls, and ensuring to overcome any signs of violence, overcome all the terrible events that were conceived, provoked and developed, as a result of a cultural rupture that wanted to apply values ​​alien to our Traditions, filling the Homeland with darkness and aberrations that we must leave behind.

Our Good Life, our Culture of Good Life, of Family, of births and rebirths of the Human Community, has allowed us to recover and recover in the Miracle of Faith, in the Powerful Mandate of Hope.

Homes, Families, Counties, Communities, Peoples, Cities of our Nicaragua, we live as God intended, and we want to continue living as God intended. We are applied to the honorable task of healing wounds and settling our steps in all the Sacred that we are and that we have, according to our own historical and cultural experience, that does not involve foreign Models or Patterns, inapplicable to Couples, in the Family, in the Homes, but rather enhances what we are, what we have, what distinguishes us, what defends us and what we want to continue cultivating and conserving as Victories and Blessings of our Spiritual Greatness. As a People we are great because we know how to overcome fragility and vulnerabilities, typical of the Human Condition.

We ask, we invoke, we cry, to God Our Lord, for the Renewal of the Fraternity, the Understanding and the Prodigy of a Peace that loves, that understands, that knows how to sow the best always.

Today April 4, in the Spiritual and Fraternal Commemoration of being taken from us, because the Life of Reverend Martin Luther King was taken from us, we commit, we continue to commit ourselves with that patient Work, Missionary, Tenacious, Powerful, that internalizes Nonviolence as a Life Commitment, Light and Truth.

We have a Dream ... Martin Luther King said: "I have a dream". We have a Dream: The Dream of knowing how to awaken and encourage all our Strengths to become more and more Heroes of Peace, of Understanding and of Fraternal Coexistence in Nicaragua.

We are a People of Fighters, a Courageous People, a Free People, which consolidates, as we consolidate, our understanding of Prosperity, in that indefatigable, tireless struggle for the Miracle and Miracles of Nonviolence, Harmony, and Peace that Blesses and brings, takes, brings, Human Fullness and Good Pride to our Lives.

Managua, April 4, 2019
Rosario Murillo
