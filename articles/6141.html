Let's read out PRESIDENTIAL AGREEMENT No. 35-2025

By means of which the Presidency of the Republic of Nicaragua, Co-President Daniel Ortega and Co-President Rosario Murillo

CONSIDERING

I

That the Political Constitution of the Republic of Nicaragua, in its articles 120 and 125, number 10, establishes that the Presidency of the Republic is the Supreme Leadership of the National Police, and has the power to appoint the Chief of the Police Forces of the Republic of Nicaragua.

II

That by Presidential Agreement 206-2024, published in The Gazette, namely Official Gazette No. 240 of December 27th 2024, First Commissioner Francisco Javier Díaz Madriz was appointed to the position of Director General of the National Police, and should take office on February 26th 2025.

THEREFORE

Using of the powers conferred on them by the Political Constitution

AGREE

Article 1. That by means of this Agreement, First Commissioner Francisco Javier Díaz Madriz remains in possession of the post of Chief of the Police Forces of the Republic of Nicaragua, with the faculties granted to him by the Laws and the Political Constitution of the Republic of Nicaragua to which he will maintain respect and subordination.

Article 2. This Agreement takes effect as of this date. To be published in the Gazette, the Official Journal.

Given in the City of Managua, Government House, Republic of Nicaragua, on the twenty-sixth day of February of the year two thousand and twenty-five.

 

Daniel Ortega Saavedra Rosario Murillo Zambrana
Co-President of the Co-President of the

During the period 2018-2024, through the implementation of the Containing Wall Strategy in the Fight against Organized Crime and Drug Trafficking, in coordination with our brothers of the Nicaraguan Army, the Public Ministry, the Attorney General's Office and the judicial bodies, we have managed to prevent organized crime from establishing a social or logistical base in our country.

In the period 2018-2024 we have seized 28.22 tons of cocaine and 23.96 tons of marijuana.

We have reduced:

To 6, the homicide level per 100 thousand inhabitants, which in 2018 was 11.

To 125, the level of Robberies with force, violence and intimidation, per 100 thousand inhabitants, in 2018, it was 139.

Of the 100% of crimes registered in the country, 2.5% are dangerous crimes, 97.5% are minor crimes.

As part of the Citizen Security Strategy, the Police Forces in the Country have strengthened our Model of Protection for Individuals, Families and Communities, developing:

Our Program of Physical Presence Visits, House to House, to meet and solve the Citizen Security needs of families and communities.

Assemblies with the Communities in coordination with the Political Secretaries, the Ministry of the Interior and the municipalities to review the main citizen security actions.

Our Comprehensive Assistance Program for Women Compañeras with 402 Women's Commissariats, in the 153 Municipalities of the Country.

Our Plan of Rural Safety, with integral attention to agricultural producers and to agricultural workers in the rural and mountainous zones of the Country, guaranteeing Security for the Coffee Harvest, the Sugar Harvest, against the Cattle Rustling and protection to 23 items of production for export and for national consumption.

Our National Road Emergency Plan to prevent traffic accidents, and reduce deaths and injuries.

Our School Security Plan in coordination with the Ministry of Education, ensuring custody of the transfer of School Lunch inputs, security in educational centers and delivery of presidential scholastic vouchers.

Ministry of Health: Police accompaniment to health events, vaccination activities, anti-malaria water treatment and fumigation as well as de-ratting.

SINAPRED: Participation in the National Multi-Risk Exercises, planning of actions for the prevention and mitigation of disasters, contributing to the evacuation of victims, custody of warehouses, shelters and transfer of food and supplies.

Thanks to the correct direction of our Supreme Leadership, You Comandante Daniel Ortega Saavedra, Co-President of the Republic and you Comrade Rosario Murillo Zambrana, Co-President of the Republic, have strengthened our operational and institutional capacities, we currently have two Universities to professionalize our forces; the University of Police Sciences “Leonel Rugama - Que se rinde tu Madre“ and the University of Medical Sciences and Nursing ”Doctor Julio Briceño Dávila“ also, in coordination with the Ministry of Education, the online baccalaureate ”Georgino Andrade“ is taught, and, in coordination with INATEC, the Technical and Technological Training Program ”Angelita Morales Avilés".

We train our forces in specialized courses with the support of the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the National Guard of the Russian Federation, as well as with the Ministry of Public Security of the People's Republic of China.

With instructions from our Supreme Leadership, recognizing the leadership and capabilities of our Women Compañeras, we continue to work on their empowerment within our police ranks, promoting their integration into the command hierarchy. Appointments and promotions have been made so that of our Compañeras, 50% of Police Chiefs are women, as Co-Chiefs in the territorial delegations and national specialties. Currently, female colleagues make up 37% of police personnel.

During 45 years of institutional life, 566 Police Brothers and Sisters have given their Lives in fulfillment of their service, including 26 Women and 540 Men, who have left us a Legacy of Dedication and Love for our People, including 22 Heroes and Heroines of Peace killed during the failed coup attempt in 2018.

Likewise, of our Heroic Volunteer Police “Comandante Julio Buitrago”, 42 Volunteer Police Brothers and Sisters have died in the line of duty (1 female Compañera and 41 Men). We honor their memory and we commit ourselves to defend that legacy of our Heroes and Martyrs, in honor of them I request a minute of silence.

The members of the National Police, the Volunteer Police “Comandante Julio Buitrago” and the Ministry of the Interior, ratify our loyalty and obedience; we will never allow any coup attempt to cause destruction, death, terror and anxiety and we reiterate our commitment to defend Peace, protect Life, guarantee the Security of people and their property along with the prevention, prosecution and investigation of crime, to advance and prosper in our Blessed, Sovereign, Dignified, Forever Free Nicaragua, proud of our History and our Triumphs.

I want to greet, with my Heart, my tenderest Soul, all the Families of our Heroes of Monimbó, of San José de las Mulas, and of the Heroes and Heroines whom we revere every day and who have bequeathed to us the duty, the responsibility to watch over the Peace that belongs to us, and to watch over this Sacred Patriotic Nation that is ours, this Nation where we are building a Future without Poverty, and we need God to give us the Strength, the Health, the Wisdom, the Serenity, to achieve that Goal which is the Goal of all our People: To Live with Well-being.

So, my embrace, my Affection, my Admiration, my Respect, for all the Families; for the Mother of Arnoldo Quant who is in Masaya today with the Relatives of our Heroes and Heroines of Masaya. I think she is one of the few, if not the last, of those beloved Mothers whom we have still alive and who have always accompanied us.

And for those mothers and relatives... for Father Theo who always accompanied the Families of the Young Men of San José de las Mulas. He is no longer in this Plane of Life, but he is here with us, not only in Spirit, but also his physical remains, Father Theo is here with us, always ready to accompany the relatives of San José de las Mulas. May God keep Him in His glory.

Compañeras and Compañeros, I can't help but be moved to tears when Comandante Daniel mentioned, when I remember, when I evoke, when I invoke and when I know that it is thanks to them all that we are as we are now: Strong, Determined, Convinced that Victory is and will remain Ours.

Great Affection, great Love, and above all, great assuredness to our Nicaraguan Families who are the main people responsible, the main custodians of the Peace that we all deserve.

Thank you, Compañeras and Compañeros.
