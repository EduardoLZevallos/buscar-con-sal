COMMUNIQUE OF THE GRUN

MAY 23, 2019


“If it had not been the Lord who was on our side, when men rose up against us,

Then they would have swallowed us up quick, when their wrath was kindled against us.

Then the waters would have overwhelmed us,

the stream gone over our soul.

Then the proud waters would have gone over our soul.

Blessed be the Lord, who hath not given us as prey to their teeth.

Our soul escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers…

Our help is in the name of the Lord, who made heaven and earth.”

(Psalm 124)

Another day of Fortitude and Hope. One more day to declare ourselves in LOVEFORNICARAGUA, with the Force of Christian Mission and Solidarity, in permanent compliance. One more day to ratify Commitments, deeds of Good Faith, Words and Acts that spring from the Heart, to continue enlightening with Confidence all the Paths of the Motherland.

Today, Thursday, May 23, in times of Family, of Mothers, of Communities full of Faith and Christian Love, we advance with Certainty continuing to build, through our selfless labor and respectful, sincere and fraternal relationship; the Stability and Peace that the Nicaraguan people so eagerly need.

Throughout the Country, Families live their daily routines: Working the fields, Education for Progress, Health that protects, Roads and Highways that communicate us, and all the Programs and Accomplishments of our Economy of Creative, Family Oriented and Communal Trade, which has contributed so much and contributes, to the endurance of a Model that has been visibilizing and distinguishing its true Protagonists: The Humble, Hard Working, Modest and Peace-loving Families, of all our Nicaragua Blessed and Always Free.

In the Pacific and Central Areas of the Country, the Winter Phenomena’s have required the issuance of Yellow and Green Alerts, for the protection and early warning of the Families that live and work in Vulnerable or Critical Points.

Since yesterday Wednesday, SINAPRED, the Municipalities, the COMUPRED´s and the Organizations of our proven and effective System of Attention and Emergency Mitigation, are deployed in compliance with their Duty, preparing all the conditions to guide and protect the inhabitants of those areas.

Agriculture, Livestock, Cooperatives, the entire State, Government, National and Municipal Public Services, Transport Networks, all Popular Centers for sale of produce and consumer goods; all Fairs and Promotions of the Popular, Entrepreneurial and Victorious Economy celebrate the Nicaraguan Mothers. The entire Festival of the honorable Labor of the Nicaraguan Majorities is carried out with the guarantees offered by Nicaragua in Peace and Security, by the Grace of God.

We continue working all the Points presented to our People and to the International Community yesterday, with which the Dignified and Sovereign Nicaraguan State and People, consolidate the Processes of Reconciliation, Meeting, Respect, Understanding and Harmonious Coexistence, as it corresponds with a Country and a People that has known how to recover all the Times, and to transit all the Challenges victoriously, as a Blessed and Free Motherland.

We are full of Hope, strengthened in the Harmony and Values ​​our Culture of Family, Life and Pride of as Nicaraguans, children of Rubén Darío, Andrés Castro, Benjamín Zeledón, Augusto Nicolás Sandino, and all the Heroes and Martyrs of this Nicaragua that believes in Jesus Christ, and prays in Spanish.

“Blessed is the man that feareth the Lord, that delighteth greatly in his commandments.

His seed shall be mighty upon earth;

the generation of the upright shall be blessed.

Wealth and riches shall be in his house; and his righteousness endureth for ever.

Unto the upright there ariseth light in the darkness; he is gracious, and full of compassion, and righteous.

A good man sheweth favour, and lendeth;

he will guide his affairs with discretion.

Surely, he shall not be moved forever;

the righteous shall be in everlasting remembrance.

He shall not be afraid of evil tidings: his heart is fixed, trusting in the Lord.

His heart is established, he shall not be afraid, until he see his desire upon his enemies.

He hath dispersed, he hath given to the poor; his righteousness endureth for ever; his horn shall be exalted with honour.

The wicked shall see it, and be grieved; he shall gnash with his teeth, and melt away; the desire of the wicked shall perish.”

(Psalm 112)


Managua, May 23, 2019
Communication and Citizens Council
Government of Reconciliation
and National Unity
