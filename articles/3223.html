Christmas Message

Enviado por tortilla el Dom, 24/12/2023 - 06:59

Gobierno de Reconciliación y Unidad Nacional
Unida Nicaragua Triunfa

CHRISTMAS FOR PROCLAIMING AND MAKING OUR WAY IN PEACE, LOVE, HOPE
AND FAITH IN GOD, IN CHRIST JESUS, AND IN HUMANITY!

 

Our Embrace, in Sister and Brotherhood, to all the families of Nicaragua, gathered together over these Christmas and New Year Holidays, full of Hope and Joy, amid the hardships, challenges and commitments, amid all the struggles and with the inescapable Obligation of Rebirth, for Life, for Love, for Peace.

 

To every beloved Nicaraguan Family, our Heart beats committed to the Christian Faith, to our irrevocable Socialist Vocation, and Always to the Path of Solidarity.

 

We join together to bless and give thanks, in praise to our Father, to Our Mother Mary, and to celebrate the Redeemer, the Savior, who brings us the Certainty of New Times, New Paths, New Commitments, in the greatest and most inalienable Love, challenging us to imitate Him, in His Sacred Mission to inhabit this World, with Dignity, Faith, Joy, and daily, enduring Sister and Brotherhood.

 

May God teach us, and may we all learn, more and more every day, to be people of Peace, of Great and Magnificent Good Will and of unchanging Commitment with the Convictions and Practices of Love of our Neighbor, of permanent Joy, of Principles, Uninterrupted Openings towards Peace, Peace, for ever Peace, to continue building a Future for Everyone.

 

CHRISTMAS FOR PROCLAIMING AND MAKING OUR WAY IN PEACE, LOVE, HOPE AND FAITH IN GOD, IN CHRIST JESUS, AND IN HUMANITY!

 

With immense Affection, Daniel, Rosario, and our whole Family.

 

Managua, December 23, 2023
